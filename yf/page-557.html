He could always keep one stone. "Mmm, a ruby, my favorite!", he cried in a pleased tone, because a dragon's favorite food is, of course, gems. But soon while searching they found a small strongbox, hidden in the middle of a pile of gems. The strongbox looked like a clock at first glance. Rarity was excited: "Oh how nice! Should we give it to Twilight Sparkle? She would surely like it", suggested Rarity. Of course Spike was for this: Twilight Sparkle was his boss and, at the same time, a dear friend. When they came home in the evening from the gem hunt, Rarity packed the strongbox in a nice bag and gave it, as promised, to Twilight Sparkle. "Oh, thank you", were her first words as she opened her unexpected present. Twilight Sparkle liked the stringbox very much and placed in on a special spot on her writing desk. As thanks, she invited her friends for a piece of cake and they spent a nice evening together. But when Twilight Sparkle later plopped into bed and Spike finally started snoring in his basket, a thought suddenly came to her: "I've yet to see what's in the strongbox!" The unicorn hopped immediately out of bed and sat down with a candle at her desk. Sie pulled the strongbox up and looked at it from all its sides. "Aha!", she cried and pressed the small button on the sides. But the strongbox didn't open. "Maybe it's locked", pondered Twilight Sparkle. But she couldn't discover a keyhole anywhwere and her friends didn't give her a key. The whole night Twilight Sparkle looked through books about the opening of mysterious items. "I wonder if these magic words will work?", she pondered. But no, the opening of the strongbox stayed an unsolvable secret. Even with the magnifying glass, she couldn't discover any clues to open her present. At some time, Twilight Sparkle fell asleep at her desk. The next morning Rarity came to the Golden Oaks(?) to pick up Spike. The two of them found Twilight Sparkle in her uncomfortable position. Rarity was concerned: "What's she doing there? It's definitely not beauty sleep." But Spike calmed her:"She does that often, when her curiosity doesn't let her sleep." And then they saw the reason for Twilight Sparkle's all-nighter: " he didn't open the strongbox!" they said as if out of one mouth. Twilight Sparkle was rudely awoken by Spike's laughter. Then the three looked closer at the contents of the strongbox. They found many white pebbles and a note, on which there was a single sentence: "Friendship is Magic: just like these stones.".
