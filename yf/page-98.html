I've been thinking a bit about the complaints of how the "Fluttershy goes completely ballistic" scene was out of character for her. As much as I love the scene, it did seem just a little out of character for her. But when you think about it, we've never really seen how Fluttershy would react to being rejected by something she's cared about so much. Just look at how excited she was to be with the animals in The Ticket Master. She never even thought of the possibility of the animals avoiding her. And it's not like we haven't seen her get angry before either, so we know it's possible. She didn't really even "snap" until the very end, where at that point she had tried everything she could for what appeared to be hours, and all ended up completely fruitless, and caused her to get frustrated and snap. There's also the fact that she doesn't even understand why the animals are avoiding her, which would probably frustrate her even more
