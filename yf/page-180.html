Twilight - Leela. Both are the take charge, no nonsense, organized leaders of the group. And Leela's hair color is the same as Twilight's

Applejack - Farnsworth. Mostly because I couldn't think of anyone else that fit her

Rainbow Dash - Bender. Both are the jerkiest, most competitive characters of the group

Fluttershy - Fry. Both are the nicest of their groups (debatable with Fry, but whatever). And because Flutterfry is funny

Pinkie Pie - Zoidberg. Both are the most bizarre and random of their groups, both obsessed with eating food, and are similar colors

Rarity - Amy. Both very fashionable and the most girly of the groups

Spike - Nibbler. Both are the team pet/extra character that shows up in the series every few episodes

Scootaloo - Cubert. Both are kind of jerks

Sweetie Belle - Tinny Tim. Both are the nicest and cutest of the kid characters

Applebloom - Dwight. Cause they're both laid back, smart kids
