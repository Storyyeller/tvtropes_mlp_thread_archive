#1919 Gelzo Tue, 19th Apr '11 1:04:45 AM from the vault
Regarding parasprites and Pinkie's backstory.

Parasprites are likely not exclusively native to the Everfree Forest surrounding Ponyville, since it was implied that they were infesting Fillydelphia. However, they do appear to be rare occurrences if we are to believe Celestia was genuinely ignorant of them. In any case, Pinkie was the only one immediately familiar with them, so living in Ponyville definitely wouldn't have helped her find out.

Now, she's mentioned that since she was a little filly she's ice skated and was taught by her grandmother to laugh at her fears. That doesn't seem likely to have happened before getting her cutie mark, so it seems to me that her parents thought it might be best for her to go live with her grandmother when it was revealed that being a "rock farmer" wasn't her calling. Her grandmother might not have lived in Ponyville, but if that's true, something in between must have motivated her to move to Ponyville.

I don't remember any indication that it reached terminal velocity.
