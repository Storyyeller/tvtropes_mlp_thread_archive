Yeah, I know that a lot of people have bitched about some of the stuff that Hasbro does, but seriously by industry standards they've been incredibly lax in what they allow the fans to get away with. A huge part of that has to do with pretty much the entire studio being in on it. I mean when you have one of your directors being rabidly protective of the fans from day one, the other goes out and buys this, your voice actors gleefully follow suit, and most of your animation staff is unusually active in exposing the guts of the business and mingling with fan artists, there's kind of a vibe that goes through the network and merchandising company not to screw with this setup lest you shoot your own cash pony in the foot by alienating one of your most talented studios. It's something that I haven't really seen this side of the Pacific in pretty much ever ‚Äî at least not on this scale.
