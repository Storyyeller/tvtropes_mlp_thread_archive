#9908 Pykrete Fri, 24th Feb '12 12:48:27 PM from Viridian Forest
It'd pretty much have to be C++/C# based. Flash has major issues with memory management, and its graphics processes tend to bring good systems to their knees over trivial stuff. It's great for little things, but tends to break down pretty badly after a certain point.

Let's put it this way, if you're generating potentially hundreds of objects in a few seconds, you probably want to actually have a way to straight-up delete them. Flash doesn't have that, and it's way too easy to leave enough loose ends that the garbage collector doesn't find it.

Most of the animation would be top-down spritework and still portraits anyway, so yeah. I'd probably use Flash to generate the image, then capture it from there.

Anyway, here, best pony. And everything that artist has ever drawn for that matter — does an amazing job of giving a sense of depth without looking creepy.
