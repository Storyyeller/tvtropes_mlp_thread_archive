About My Chemical Romance: They really are a good band. Like, actually talented and fully aware of what sort of music they're doing and all. People give them a bad rep, but I think it's mostly because they don't actually understand that the entire Emo look and over-dramatic lyrics and such was all done ironically. The album The Black Parade was meant to be a concept album (which I LOVE, concept albums are THE BEST), and it was supposed to be ridiculously dark and dramatic. The way the band members dress up in the videos for it? They're actually dressing up as a fictional band, a persona they made up for the sake of the album. They're supposed to look fake. Plus the story of the album is actually really well thought out. It's not just some emo trash. /rant.

EDIT: Just listened to the whole The Black Parade album, I'd forgotten how great it really is. And the guitar work on it is obviously above the usual simplistic alternative rock/punk stuff that people usually lump it in with.
