#15198 Sydxelia Sat, 1st Sep '12 8:32:55 PM
Grimm is a police procedural with supernatural elements. A detective, Nick Burkhart, suddenly finds himself with the ability to see seemingly normal people change into various supernatural creatures, called Wesen. According to the show's premise, he's a descendant of the Brothers Grimm, and the stories they wrote about really happened. As one of the last of the Grimms, Nick has to bring the bad Wesen to justice, while trying to keep his ability secret from both his detective partner and his girlfriend (both normal humans.) The whole thing sounds kind of silly on paper, but the show itself has kind of a dark tone. Some of the scenes are actually quite gruesome.

As of Season 2, Episode 3, Nick's detective partner finally knows about Nick's ability, and the existance of the Wesen. The reveal was everything I had hoped for.
