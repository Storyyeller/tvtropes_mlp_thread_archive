Okay, so I finally succumbed to sheer internet meme peer pressure and watched an episode of this.

It's... Okay. Really, it's just another run-of-the-mill children's cartoon in my eyes. The animation is nothing special, the voice acting is decent, and the individual episode plots just seem to be different ways of delivering An Aesop to children.

It's not bad, but I certainly wouldn't call it the greatest thing to happen to Western Animation since Avatar: The Last Airbender.

There's really only one thing about this series that stands out to me: the potential for creating image macros. There are some scenes with outstanding potential for image-macro-fication, but that's really the only thing that's particularly interesting about it.
