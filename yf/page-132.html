Meh. Cartoon crushes happen, and honestly, are kind of to be expected; if the human mind can construct fictional people and have emotional reactions to them, there's not much reason to think that "attraction" would somehow be exempt from the set of emotional responses one can have to the character.

RE the ponies specifically: they generally behave and are depicted as girls around the age of 20, while having a few traits that are more childish than one would expect. When someone says "crush", I'm assuming here that they mean emotional moreso than physical attraction, so Haldo (in this case) effectively has a crush on a fictional ~18-20-year-old girl. So it doesn't strike me as particularly unhealthy (if someone is physically attracted to cartoon ponies, well, that's pretty weird, but they aren't hurting anybody, so whatever).

Keep the line between fiction and reality clear in your head and don't hurt anybody, and as far as I'm concerned, you're doing fine.
