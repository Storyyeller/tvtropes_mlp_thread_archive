Combination Attack: Mane 6

The Elements of Harmony: Only when all of the Mane 6 are in the same party, and their HP is greater than -1, can the Elements of Harmony be used. Only Twilight Sparkle can call this attack, and it has to be declared before Initiative is rolled on the turn that it will be used. For the duration of the round, the Mane 6 count as one character for determining Initiative and success/failure rolls. For this purpose, the highest roll between all of the Mane 6 is used. The attack works in much the same way as a Desperation Attack. Roll 1d100 against total of percentage remaining HP for Mane 6. On success, the Elements of Harmony reduce enemy to 0 HP (Half-Consciousness) and has a 30% chance to cast Petrify. If the attack fails, the Mane 6 lose their action and take a penalty of -10 for all d100 rolls for the next round. If The Elements of Harmony is used, no other Desperation attacks may be used by any memebr of the Mane 6 for the remainder of the encounter (including Meltdown).
