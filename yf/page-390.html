Alright, sorry for the doublepost, but people trying to write Luna's archaic speech and not actually researching it out is making my inner theater geek do a barrel roll. To anyone who plans on writing fanfiction that involves Luna, here in long form and here as a cheat sheet.

Key notions:

  • Thou/thee/thy/thine is analogous to I/me/my/mine.
  • Verbs applied to the second-person (thou) conjugate with -st/-est. Dost, hast, makest, speakest, etc., and obligatory odd ducks like wilt and art. It's a leftover of German.
  • Verbs applied to the third-person (he/she/it) conjugate with -th/-eth. Doth, hath, explodeth, etc.
  • Ye is second-person plural (you guys).
  • The phrase "thy does" should never, ever appear in a sentence. Of anything.
