The Lion King counts as furry. I don't see why MLP shouldn't.

Just giving animals human personalities counts as anthropomorphism.

That being said, liking MLP doesn't make you a furry. "Furry" is such a loaded word at this point that it's better to just define it as "anyone who agrees to be labelled to as furry".

Which actually really reminds me of the word "brony". Apparently, there are people who think: "I'm a fan of MLP, but Bronies are fucking nuts!" Because to them, "brony" means "hardcore fan" or "lunatic". Then there's the debates about whether women can be bronies, or if the word is age-specific. Ugh.

Which then reminds me of the word "feminist". I'm pretty sure that word means "a person who thinks that women and men should be equals". I'm a feminist by that definition. But of course, there are the lunatics. And now I know plenty of people who think feminists are all "men are all rapists!" nutsoes. And I also know a couple of more intellectual people who think that feminism has run its course because men and women are already equal, and we should have a new word which doesn't emphasise the women anymore.

I hate these labels. They're all about trying to establish some completely arbitrary groups, sometimes going "Hey! You belong in this group with me! We're brethren!" and sometimes going "I'm going put certain groups of people under this label and then call them all fucking lunatics! That'll teach them!"
