#8266 Santos L Halper Mon, 9th Jan '12 9:04:20 AM from The KuK
Some ideas on geography:

  • Cities:
    • The capital of Dar al-Hisaanin is Asilandria*
      A play on Alexandria and the Arabic term asil, which means "purebred"
      , which is also home to the Great Library, the second only to Canterlot's royal library in size.
    • The second biggest city is Neighshapur*
      A play on the onomatopoeia "neigh" and the Iranian city of Nishapur, which is famous for its turquoise
      , which is not only known for its jewelry and mineral wealth, but is also the headquarter of Luna's and Nightmare Moon's cult.
    • Destrierascus*
      A play on Damascus and "destrier", a type of Medieval European warhorse
       is the oldest settlement in Dar al-Hisaanin, but fell into decline after the century-long civil war which was caused by Luna's betrayal.

  • The country's sole access to Equestria is the Turquoise Road, which is a series of trade routes that goes through several neighbouring areas, and is generally pretty dangerous. More important ponies can travel through a special carriage/wagon drawn by pegasi.
