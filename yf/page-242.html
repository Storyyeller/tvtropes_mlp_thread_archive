I was thinking about parallels between the pony pantheon and classical Pagan deities*
I hope this does not offend anyone: it's just for fun, I would make a parallel with Christianity instead if it were appropriate.

I'm going with the Roman pantheon, which is the one I know best.

Luna is easy — she's already the moon goddess, and the sister of the sun god.

Discord is Discordia, just as easily — he changes gender, but Discord would probably roll with it with no trouble .

Celestia, however... there are a few possibilities here. One is associating her to Sol/Sol Indiges/Sol Invictus*
There is an ongoing debate on whether these last two deities were the same...
, the aforementioned sun god: that would make sense, as he was considered the protector of the late Roman Empire — it fits quite nicely with Celestia being the ruler of Equestria.

Or she could be Caelus/Caelum, the sky god — the name fits better, after all. But the myth differs: I cannot really imagine Celestia castrating her own father and throwing his testicles in the sea, though I imagine that it would make an interesting piece of fanfiction .

Or again, Celestia could be Aurora, yet another sister of Luna and the herald of dawn: that's kind of Celestia's thing anyway, and in this way she does not have to change gender. Plus, Aurora has a tiara, precisely as Celestia does.

That would imply, however, that Celestia, differently from Luna, does not control the sun directly — rather, Celestia and Luna's brother Sol moves the sun, and Celestia only chooses whether to allow it to rise. Again, this could be an interesting interpretation.

Thoughts?

EDIT: has anyone, to your knowledge, already tried to rewrite Hesiod's Theogony as a creation myth for Equestria? If not, I might just do that, for the hell of it. It would require adding a lot of non-canonical alicorns, for the other deities, and the style would be kept as is — that is, a little on the heavy side — but I am easily amused.

Also, there would be a lot of incest, of course. But I get the feeling that the MLP shippers have done worse already, so eh
