I'm now gonna share my backstory for Discord from my story-in-planning.

Discord was the grand general of the Nightmare armies, and the right hoof of the Nightmare Emperor. His widespread campaign of terror and destruction made him infamous among Nightmares and Equestrians alike, earning him the title "The Dread Lord Discord". With him leading their dark armies, the Nightmares had won many decisive battles. However, he had secretly desired to take the Nightmare throne and rule over the lands himself, but he remained loyal to the Emperor until the time would be right.

As a Nightmare, Discord wielded destructive powers greater than that of any pony in that time. Discord could also shapeshift into any pony or other creature and mimic their speech and behavior perfectly, making him the perfect spy.

When the Pony Clans had driven the Nightmares to the brink of defeat, Celestia, Luna, and the original bearers of The Elements of Harmony confronted Discord at the Nightmares' last remaining fortress. A brutal battle erupted, but ultimately, Discord was defeated. Because of the crimes he had committed against ponykind, Celestia cursed him with the draconequus form he has today, and stripped him of his powers. Ashamed by his failure, Discord fled to a faraway land to plot his revenge.
