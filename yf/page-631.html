#15758 Bluespade Wed, 12th Sep '12 8:02:54 PM from Fort Worth, Texas
You gotta kill the minotaur. It's the game's first real boss. If you climb the tower first and kill the two archers before the battle they won't be shooting at you during it. And then you can jump off the tower and do lots of damage to it. Or if you have the 5 black firebombs you picked up earlier in the level, you can just use those on him. Like 4 of them will kill him, you don't even have to get close.

Back stab you just press attack when you're behind them. Kick is just forward+attack. Jump attack just walk off a cliff and press attack. All require just one button press. And no, enemies aren't weak to specific attacks of that sort. Back stab just does a ton of damage to everything and so does the jumping attack. Kicking just staggers the enemy so you can get easy hits off on them.
