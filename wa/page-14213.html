@Japanese Pinkie song: I would need to see the lyrics written out to translate it more exactly, but from what I could tell, they had translated it very accurately, right down to the same tune. I think they even used an Engrish "Granny Pie" instead of having Pinkie refer to her "obaa-chan" which would be a friendly way to refer to one's grandmother in Japanese. The main difference was that the "Giggle at the Ghosties" line itself was something that, so far as I can tell, translates more to "laugh and make them fly away" and I think it was something like "warai tobasou." It sounded very much to me like the ponies from the original show speaking/singing in Japanese (Pinkie's voice in particular really does sound like the original character just speaking a different language).
