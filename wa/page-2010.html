@50227: So very tempted to write that crossover, except that there's a distinct lack of evil adult ponies.

One of the nice things about having a pastel cast is that you get all the visual cues of different races without the unwanted implications and contrived P Cness. If you're going to map race onto the protagonists for the sake of making them all different, it should acknowledge their cultures and not be derived from personality stereotypes of races - Applejack and Pinkie Pie are particularly American-Caucasian in origin, for instance. By contrast, whatever Fluttershy and Rainbow Dash are should be based on whatever your internal visualization of Cloudsdale happens to be.

But personally, I can't picture them without the pastel. It's like picturing Adventure Time characters with elbows and knees.
