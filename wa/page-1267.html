I kind of don't like to define things outside of the show in case we want to change once we think of an episode that involves that idea (which can and has happened) but here's how it currently works in my head:

Luna is not yet full grown, and some sort of evil magic turned her into Night Mare Moon. When she grows to an adult, her real form will not look so much like NMM.

Note the parts I put in bold. This is literally described as Lauren Faust's headcanon and could change at any time, especially now she's not in charge anymore.
