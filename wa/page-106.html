She does not communicate well. She either overcommunicates or doesn't communicate at all.

And the dance. She failed that dance horribly. And even after it failed, she tried the exact. same. thing. One definition of insanity is "Trying the same thing over and over again and expecting different results."

So, long story short, Lauren Faust is dangerously genre savvy herself, knows how to avoid a sue, and when to make a Sue so that she can desconstruct her.
