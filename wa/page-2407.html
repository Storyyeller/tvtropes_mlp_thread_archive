So, random topic. How do you guys think magic actually works in this setting? While the vagueness of how magic works in this show is probably intentional, Twilight pretty much outright stated that it had internal consistency and rules in "Feeling Pinkie Keen".

(Warning: incoming headcanon)

Because magic seems to be naturally occurring, it almost seems to me that magic is some sort of field force, like gravity or electromagnetism. Without any disturbances, the background magic is balanced and evenly distributed, so it has no effect on the world. If something disrupts the field to concentrate energy in one place, the flow of magic to and from this concentration as it attempts to regain equilibrium creates the various magical effects seen in the show. Unicorns are able to use their horns to actively manipulate the flow of magic to cast their spells. Other ponies passively manipulate the field, assisting flight with Pegasi and enriching the earth with Earth Ponies. I like this better than magic simply being powered by life energy or some similar source, otherwise Pegasi and Earth Ponies would be drained constantly by their passive abilities. Unicorns get exhausted from overuse of magic because it takes a lot of concentration and effort to channel the energies around them. It also allows for innately magical objects and places to exist without a power source.
