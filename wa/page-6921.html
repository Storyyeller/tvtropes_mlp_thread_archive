Colin: Mostly basic mistakes that cause major problems. It always irritates me to go into a 5 or 6 star fic and find out that despite the huge fanbase endorsement, it's got major pacing issues or the writing is wildly inconsistent or the narrative lapses into big passages of blatant telling instead of showing. I can overlook a few spellings and grammar mistakes and probably even some plot holes and minor pacing issues (assuming the rest of the story is good enough), but this type of stuff makes it look like the author didn't even bother to proofread his/her own work.
