The Nordsvensk brukshäst is a horse that can be easily mistaken for a pony, because of its general body shape and size. It is descended from the same stock of Tarpan that the Gotland Russ (Gotland Pony) is from.

You can hardly find a horse breed and a pony breed that are more closely related while still being demonstrably a Horse breed and a Pony breed.

Well, actually, Gotland Ponies can be considered horses if you go by the skull shape/proportions, but if you go by the legs and hooves, it's a pony.

In fact, when people have attempted to recreate the old Tarpan by selective breeding, the Gotland is almost always used.

So, in this show, if there was a pony that was only ambiguously a pony and only ambiguously a horse, something of both; how would that work? How would it be drawn? Would it be considered a new breed (Pegasi, Unicorns, Earthponies, Seaponies, Gotland?)?
