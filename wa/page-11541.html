#288521 Japanese Teeth Fri, 28th Sep '12 10:34:12 AM from Meinong's jungle
I totally agree that Lyra needs to get more focus on the musician aspect of her character (which is why I headcanon that her official job is a music teacher), but I can kinda understand why that side of her doesn't get as much focus. There are half a dozen or other ponies (including, but not limited to, Vinyl and Octavia) that are music-related, but only Lyra is associated with the hands thing. It's only natural that people would want to focus on the aspects of her character that are unique to her. The other thing is that Vinyl and Octavia are always shown performing whenever they actually appear in the show; music is a central part of their characterization. With Lyra, she's never actually been seen playing an instrument, so it's easy to drift away from that part of her character.

And yes, moderation (or temperance, if you want to be archaic about it) is certainly a virtue. Any good thing can go horribly wrong if you go too far with it.
