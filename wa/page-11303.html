well anyway. further reports on my journey through the wide world of mlp fanfiction since I finished FOE.

finished reading Wayward Courier, another fallout/mlp crossover. The Courier's Transportalponder glitches and sends him to Equestria instead of the Mojave. Shenanigans occur, ponies get traumatized with stories of how bad a place the mojave is, diamond dogs and changelings get their shit rocked, and The Courier gets it on with Luna. Really, they were both drunk! Such a thing would normally turn me away, but nothing was actually shown and it was entirely Played for Laughs, so I was OK with it. It was overall an ok story, I'd say.

Also read Mass Effect 2 - DLC: The Equestrian Equation. Now that was a fun one! I've tried a few mass effect/mlp crossovers, but most of them are "ponies are already an established member of the galactic council, proceed with plot of ME", a concept that just doesn't do anything for me. But this! After finding the Reaper IFF, Shepard gets a mission from TIM to track down a lost team of Cerberus scientists who were investigating a strange, unknown power signature when they went silent. Each chapter ends with a a set of options for the readers to choose from. This isn't your normal human in equestria fic, not by a long shot...

finally, I read Fair Feather Friend. RD finds an injured Falcon and decides to make it her pet. Shenanigans ensue. There's not much more to say about it, very fun read.
