#201484 Japanese Teeth Tue, 22nd May '12 1:47:11 PM from Meinong's jungle
I'm basically with Lurker on this one; I like it best when Pinkie's quirks derive primarily from her personality rather than from her self-awareness. The Looney Tunes style gags are only there to emphasize the quirks of her personality, not define it completely. I have nothing against that style of humor (I thought 8BitTheater was hilarious), but it tends to screw up the willing suspension of disbelief again when everything in the story is internally consistent except for her.

I don't think it's wrong to interpret the character as breaking the 4th wall on occasion, but I do think that a lot of people emphasis that aspect of the humor too much to the exclusion of the character based jokes. Stuff like the "I keep balls stashed all over town in case of emergency" is funny just because Pinkie is the type of pony who would totally go to the trouble of stashing balls in random places. It shows off the randomness of her personality without giving her 4th-wall breaking abilities.
