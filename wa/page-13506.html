Thinking this episode over, I really like how they tie it together with "Just for Sidekicks" so that Twilight's line "I'm sure Spike's doing a good job taking care of our pets" is important for both episodes. In JFS, that line is the catalyst for Spike's apology for being a terrible petsitter. And in "Games Ponies Play", the line is shown to be a sign that Twilight is finally learning how to keep calm under pressure.

Back when JFS aired, I actually thought that Twi's sudden trust in Spike in that scene seemed a little out of place. Learning that it's intentional character growth makes it much better.
