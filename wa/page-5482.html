@Moe Dantes: I'm in complete agreement with you that we shouldn't have to specifically mention that our posts are just an opinion, since that goes without saying.

Personally, I thought I was going to hate Dragon Quest since I'm not really a fan of Spike but I ended up loving it, partially because it was funny, partially because the metal soundtrack took me completely by surprise and kept me entertained throughout the episode, but mostly because of the worldbuilding.

I've been a big fantasy fan all my life, but the problem with fantasy is that most of it's settings are very lazy. Everything tends to adhere to your basic standard fantasy tropes: Medieval Stasis, Elves/Dwarves/Humans, battle of good and evil, chosen one, old wise wizards, magic swords, you know, the usual. So it always intrigues me when a fantasy work branches out and does something actually original with the concept. And MLP is one of the most original fantasy settings I've ever come across. I mean it's main race are sentient ponies. So any episode that features world building is going to be of interest to me.

EDIT: I liked It's About Time waaaaaaaay better than Lesson Zero, which was one of my least favorite episodes. Could have used less Pinkie though.
