#61465 thefran Wed, 28th Dec '11 3:18:04 PM from a microwave oven
You always suck at the beginning; it's just a question of whether you're going to keep it up.
The huge problem with drawing is that it is not systematic in any way whatsoever.

How to learn how to write? Think of a story (very important). Define characters. While plot. Clear up grammar. Eliminate inconsistencies. Get rid of the useless synonyms and insanely overcomplicated sentences. Big words don't make anything better.

How to learn how to draw? Draw everything for years. If you screwed up, there is no way to learn how to improve.
