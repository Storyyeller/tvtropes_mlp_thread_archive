#347770 Applelight Limited Wed, 13th Mar '13 9:14:35 PM from Manehattan to Canterlot
Well the trick with shading is to pick the location of the light source and stay consistent with it. Even if you have to draw it on temporarily.

When I'm drawing and shading atm, I pick four shades of the same colour (which photoshop arranges out nicely). The darkest shade is used as the outline, the second brightest shade is the main colour, the brightest is the highlight, and the second darkest is the shading. You can easily apply that to pencil work.

That's probably really obvious sounding, but it took me a long time to realize it, and I'm still trying to master it.
