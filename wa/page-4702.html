Random? Perhaps, I do not know, all I know is that I cannot comprehend a single race or body type or facial structure for anything. I mean first of all it would be boring to draw and lead to blog creations coughjosecocough, but furthermore my world view is simply that the world is huge, diverse, unique, and vast and I less resort to tokenism and more reflect that the world we live in had no tabula rasa.

I grew up in an area surrounded by all types, from kindergarten to second grade my best friends were a white kid, an Asian kid, and a Filipino kid, and the coolest most popular boy in class was an Asian guy. And then I went to international schools for 5 years of my formative years of existence. Limitations are nonsensical to me, it's like it makes no sense to limit yourself first of all because the world isn't and second of all you're being a boring artist coughjosecocough. I support that no tabula rasa in the human form exists.
