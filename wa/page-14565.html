Both sides act like idiots while nothing gets done.

I'm pretty this applies to all politics ever with more than one dominant party. The options are either enable the government to do stuff, at which point whatever actions it takes will inevitably screw over a large portion of the population, or keep the government in check, which prevents them from doing anything, even things that would benefit most people. Either way people are going to complain about it because they're never happy unless the government is doing exactly what they want it to do, no more and no less.

Regarding Equestrian government, my take on it is that Celestia is sort of like the final authority, but she delegates most of it out to Mayors and Governors and stuff. She probably retains a sort of veto power, but wouldn't use it unless somepony really screwed up.
