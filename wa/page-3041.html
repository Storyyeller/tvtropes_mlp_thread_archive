I have to say characterization wise the episode made sense. Applejack's problem stemmed from a mixture of pride and letting everybody down. AJ's selflessness and desire to do good (however occasionally misguided) defines her and this did a solid job of demonstrating what happens when she gets so singled-minded in that respect.

Pinkie was a bit much at times, but it completely fit as to why she was used to talk to Applejack. Every other one of the mane six would likely not got anything out of her (Fluttershy's would never been forceful enough, Twilight's rational arguments would be dismissed, I can't imagine any approach by Rarity that would work, and Rainbow Dash would likely just get impatient too quickly).

Incidentally part of me would not be shocked by some Day in the Limelight episode with Derpy showing her to be savant at something and a lesson about the unexpected talents people can have.
