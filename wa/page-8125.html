Okay, this thread gets so many posts in such a short time, it's not even funny! Usually, I find myself on threads that are lucky to even get one post from anypony! Seriously, how do you guys go through a thread like this without Thread Hopping?

So, like I said some number of posts ago, I find myself identifying with Twilight Sparkle the most.

I like Rainbow Dash, because she's a Tomboy, and I have a weakness for tomboys.

Pinkie Pie is the kind of character that I normally would consider annoying, because she's so bright and happy and that kind of personality usually grates on my nerves. But she's actually not annoying. In fact, that "Party of One" episode demonstrates that being a Sad Clown really does not suit her, and that schizophrenia moment...yikes! I think she might have a mental illness of some kind. I'm probably not the only one who thinks that, if that dark and gruesome fanfic Cupcakes is anything to go by....

Rarity is big into fashion, and I really don't know that much about fashion. It's also strange how she seems to be a little self-centred and generous...contradictory! Still, I can understand not wanting to get your hands literally dirty!

Applejack has the cowgirl personality down to an art form. I have to admire those kicks she makes on trees...I know I couldn't kick that hard!

Fluttershy is the kind of character who I normally wouldn't look at twice. She's a Proper Lady, and not to mention an Extreme Doormat at times. If she was Japanese, she would be a Yamato Nadeshiko! Still, the episodes "Dragonshy", "The Best Night Ever", "Lesson Zero", "Putting Your Hoof Down", etc. show that when she stops being nice...be afraid. Be very afraid. By the way, that rabbit she has seriously needs to be slapped!
