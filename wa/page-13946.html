Well both Chrysalis and Gertrud have a butterfly motif. Spike and Steven Magnet because the diamond theme makes Rarity's death even more ironic. Sombra because he's vaugely shaped and all black as is Elly/Kristen (her gimmick does NOT translate well to Equestria). Cocatrice because it's a silly looking monster that is incredly dangerous, just like Anja. The timberwolves are made of wood which is a motif for Elsa Maria and the it doesn't hurt at all scene works best with AJ if the wolves are Elsa Maria. Philomena was used because I had real trouble thinking of any monsters that could be seen as romantic, so I used her because fire = passion Sayaka's fall is do to love after all.

Most of the episode 10 witches I phoned in because I couldn't think of anything that remotely matched their brand of strangeness. The exception is Discord who is the same kind of fun loving free spirit as Pinkie, who I cast as Madoka, without any concern for others and is the LARGEST threat to Equestria/the world. Nightmare Moon is tied to Walpurguisnacht (Walpurgist NIGHT) and creates an appropriately dark atmosphere for the fight.
