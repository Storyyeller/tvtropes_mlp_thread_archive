#371838 Seraphem Sat, 1st Jun '13 9:20:33 AM from Delamare
It wasn't a "Queen" just a sorceress who had a magic amulet which enhanced her power, while amplifying her darker emotions. She was threatening the Earth Pony tribe that had adopted Cadance, and the pegasus went to confront her, using her love powers to break through the amulets dark influences.

Basically the amulet wasn't the Alicorn Amulet, but something else, which amplified and reflected the wearers emotions, good or bad. And when the sorceress started getting angry and resentful of being feared by the ponies, it started a feedback loop which pushed her further and further into anger and rage. Cadance broke it just with her love, and next thing she knows she's on the ethereal plane Twilight was sent to, alone, till Celestia happened to come by and was surprised to find another being there.

Oh and no mention of Celestia ascended Cadance, rather she ascended on her own from her deeds, Celestia being surprised when she found out, but taking the new alicorn to Canterlot and adopting her as her niece.
