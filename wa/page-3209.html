The point of the training grounds in this case is to provide something people seem to lack that keeps them from picking up the pencil: inspiration and motivation. The theme gets the mind working at the deadline presents a clear goal with the promise of being able to show off one's work (and if they're lucky, like just about anywhere on the Internet, they might get some pointers). And so what if some of it looks bad? Everyone has to start somewhere (my first June 2011 NATG entry was pretty awful) and actually doing it is the only way to get significant improvement.
