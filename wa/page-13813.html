#345312 kegisak Mon, 4th Mar '13 7:27:03 AM
IT looks good, but the shading is inconsistent, making it a bit hard to tell where the light source is. When doing shading it's best to keep in mind a specific source(Or multiple sources depending on the complexity of the shot), possibly even physically drawing the source. Places closest to that will have highlights, and places furthest away will be shaded. Intensity of light, and the angle, will vary the depth and size of the shadows, etc. As you have it now it looks like most of the light is from the front, but his wings are highlighted closer to the back... unless in the intention was the light was slightly behind him so the 'forward' edges of his wings were caught in the shadows of his neck...
