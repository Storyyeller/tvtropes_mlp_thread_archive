@Paci, you're being short-sighted in associating it with the setting, rather than the system. I assure you, there is nothing in the rules that ties in too strongly with that. Actually, it will be EASIER than most other systems, say, D&D, because other systems were built around combat. The Storytelling System was made for plot-centered stories to begin with.

@Kyler, yup, Elements of Harmony is obviously the choice to replace the Virtues. :)

Here is an example of how I imagine the Virtue/Vice system could work:

Rarity:

  • Virtue: Generosity. Once a session, by giving up something of personal value to help another, Rarity recovers all her Willpower points.
  • Vice: Vanity. Every scene where Rarity ignores more pressing matters to maintain appearances (hers, someone else's or any object she's working on), she regains a Willpower point.

Rainbow Dash:

  • Virtue: Loyalty. Once a session, by giving up concentrating on a personal goal to help her friends, Rainbow Dash recovers all her Willpower points.
  • Vice: Mischief. Every scene when Rainbow Dash teases a friend about a mistake they have just made or enjoys someone being exposed to a minor hardship and ridicule, she regains one Willpower point.
