#167334 Enlong Sat, 21st Apr '12 9:42:07 PM from The Magitek Holyland
You know, one thing I really love about this show is how much of it makes sense when you stop to think about it. Like the Elements, and what each of the six attributes means beyond the one-word descriptions given (and in the case of Magic, stuff that is entirely inferred which turns out to be dead-on).

Or, in today's case, the repeated examples of how unity is a powerful force all its own. It's been channeled in many different ways: whether that be a fiery heart born of unity between the three races, six gemstones meant to hold the aspects of unity between all people, or, in today's episode, the raw, revitalizing power of unity between two people who want to give their lives to each other.

Unity is power. And it's funny. So far, we've seen a number of threats, past and present. We've seen one who rejected the idea of unity as a lie. We saw a creature who sought to pervert the very tenets of unity. We flashed back to a horde of creatures who sought to destroy it to feed on its absence. And our newest villain is one who seeks to steal that power for herself.

I just really love the way the themes just weave together.
