#340234 Korodzik Sat, 16th Feb '13 4:09:49 PM from Bolanda
I can't help but wonder where will the writers go with this.

On one hand, if this sticks, then either Twilight or the rest of the mane six will probably go Out of Focus. It's probably hard to write plots about a superpowerful princess busy running the country. (Now imagine The West Wing, Equestria edition.) (I just also realized that they have another Physical God, Discord, as their ally now...)

On the other hand, it's very unlikely that the beginning of Season 4 will have her stripped of her title. This happened with so much fanfare (and she's "begun a new life"!) that it's not really something that can be taken back: "Well, that was fun, back to the status quo and let's never talk about this again!"

The only way this can go, I think, is a major shift... maybe to an adventure format? (Remember how King Sombra was supposed to be a recurring villain throughout Season 3? Maybe they'll dust off this concept.) Except, as already noted, with so many powerful entities on the good guys' side it would be probably hard to challenge them credibly.
