@Bagofmagicfood, last page: I watched the episode... it was poorly paced, clumsily written, the voice acting was dull and lackluster, the song at the end was uninspired and the whole thing was just sort of saccharin. The conflict was barely there, and there wasn't nearly enough time to build up enough emotional tension for the climax to feel good at all. The colours were dull and ugly, and the animation was awkward and sloppy.

And yet... I actually sort of really enjoyed it. It's years before my time, so it can't be nostalgia, but there's something to be said for the sort of simple earnestness. I can really see why MLP became such a hit in the 80's. For all it's faults, it's actually got a lot of life behind it. Compared to G3 and 3.5, which are pretty but empty, it's actually really nice.

The plot itself? Pretty close to what I was thinking, except showing off the race's talents except for just having a knack for being in the right place at the right time. But it might be interesting to try adapting an old episode into the modern setting, in fic format.
