#11168 Japanese Teeth Fri, 22nd Jul '11 12:06:49 PM from Meinong's jungle from Meinong's jungle
I think what RS means is that the two options of what the cutie marks could be are as follows:

  • They just appear at random, deciding what a pony's purpose in life is.
  • They appear as a result of the pony finding their desired purpose in life; or to restate it, they appear when a pony realizes what they want spend their lives doing.

I lean towards the latter, and I think it just makes more sense in view of the show itself. With that interpretation, Silver Spoon didn't really get such a raw deal; there's nothing she wants more in life than to just be doted on, so that's what she's going to be. She doesn't aspire to any other purpose, so the cutie mark reflects that. If she wanted to be something more than what she is, the mark would be different.

It still raises the question of what happens when somepony's "purpose" changes, but I'd guess that either the cutie mark changes, or they find a new purpose that could still be reflected by the same mark (i.e. Silver Spoon becomes a silversmith or cook or something).

My favorite CMC is probably Apple Bloom because she's adorable, and has the most Character Development. I like the other two (and they're also adorable), but Scootaloo seems more like a stock tomboy character, and Sweetie Belle just doesn't seem as interesting when compared to Scootaloo or Applebloom, probably because AB got more episodes, and Scoots' personality is a lot more active and in-your-face.
