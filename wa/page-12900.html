#322487 Japanese Teeth Fri, 28th Dec '12 8:25:25 AM from Meinong's jungle
@Osias: My understanding of it is that it's fanfic if it utilizes the setting, characters, or cosmology from the show's canon. The only way a parody could not be a fanfic is if every element was replaced with something else. Like you replace Applejack with Pearsteve, Equestria with Hippoland, and cutie marks with adorasymbols or something. But if your using any of that stuff straight, characters from the show, or set in the same universe, or whatever, it would technically count as fanfic.

And even if you replace all that stuff, it would still be a fanfic of whatever universe you're crossing it over with.
