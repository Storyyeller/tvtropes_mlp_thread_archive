#304148 Perpetual Lurker Fri, 16th Nov '12 1:41:00 PM
@Pykrete

It gets better. It's not just the price list that's exposed XML. It's the entire game outside of the art assets. Turns out that the engine itself is just a simple XML parser running on a loop, and the XML itself comprises the majority of the game's source code. People are already beginning to mod the shit out of it. Depending on how much they can do with it, they may even be able to create a whole game from scratch using the same assets.

I have to hand it to Gameloft. Aside from it being stupidly insecure, that's a really neat way to ensure cross-platform compatibility with a single code base.
