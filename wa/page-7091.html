As for the situation with Cows and Sheep, I've seen similar situations in other science fiction and fantasy works. They're client races, meaning that they depend on another race for something and provide a valuable service in return. Cows provide milk and Sheep provide wool, in return for the shelter and protection that they are not entirely capable of themselves, lacking the magic and strength that the ponies have. They are not denied any rights that a pony would have, and we've seen cows walking around Ponyville independently on at least two occasions.
