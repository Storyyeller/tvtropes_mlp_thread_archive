#318301 Japanese Teeth Mon, 17th Dec '12 6:22:38 PM from Meinong's jungle
@David: Beyond the Impossible certainly does have a place, but not in every show. It worked fine for Gurren Lagann for two reasons 1. the show was build entirely around it; the cosmology literally runs on Rule Of Cool 2. it had a defined endpoint in mind. They knew how many episodes they had to produce and thus they could end the series when they can't push it any more.

Neither of those things are true of MLP; the entire show is built around character interactions. The cast was designed with the purpose of fostering interesting conflicts between them. It was always intended to be based first and foremost on characters. Not that occasional pockets of adventure aren't good, but the show just isn't designed for that. I have no doubt that they could if they wanted to, but the problem is the more emphasis gets put on the epicness, the harder it is to focus on the characters.
