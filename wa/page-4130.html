A friend of mine actually had a terrible, but sort of tragically hilarious theory on Scootabuse: Equestria's peace is contingent on Scootaloo's unhappiness. In essence, Scootaloo is a container for the world's unhappiness. If she were ever to be truly happy, all of the unhappiness would spread over Equestira. She can be happy for a while, but no cutie mark, no true love, etc. etc.

It's horrible and has absolutely no grounding in anything, but the ludicrous bleakness of it is actually sort of entertaining. Which I suppose is why people like Scootlabuse to begin with; the over-the-top horridness is funny. Sort of like all those Rarity is abusive to Sweetie Belle things.
