Okay, it's a bit of a long story. I'm at a beach house with my parents, and a good friend from high school. Another of our mutual friends got married last summer, and his first anniversary is coming up. So, being good friends, we decided to send him a gift. And, being insane, we naturally had to come up with the most random gifts possible. Eventually we came up with the following:

1. A giant plastic ear (we found it at a gift shop; it was literally just hanging there with a "BUY A GIANT EAR!" sign on it)

2. A hermit crab shell with the Incredible Hulk painted on it.

3. A wooden alligator carving with silver plastic legs clumsily glued on, purchased from a dollar store.

This isn't the incredibly stupid part. The stupid part is that we decided to include a letter to our friend from himself. From the future, in which he's a Future Badass. It started as a few pieces of random advice and an explanation of the stuff we sent with him, but it quickly snowballed into...this. Keep in mind we were both horribly sleep-deprived and in one of those states where literally anything is hilarious. And yes, we stayed up until 2 in the morning writing that.
