Also, here's my version of the canon levels:

  • Canon: Stuff that is explicitly shown in the show
  • WOG Canon: Stuff that doesn't appear in the show itself, but is confirmed by the makers.
  • Unconfimred fanon: Doesn't contradict canon in any way, but can't be proven either (ages, Octavia's relationship to Pinky, etc.)
  • Exaggerated fanon: Canon attributes exaggerated to non-canon levels (Tyrant Celestia, insane Pinkie, etc.)
  • Anti-canon: Explicitly contradicts canon. (What-ifs)
