Based on scenes in several episodes, including:

  • ponies cleaning up so that winter can end and spring arrive, implying that if they don't, the seasons won't change.
  • Weather Patrol doing climate control up to and including manufacturing individual snowflakes
  • ponies holding a race so that leaves could fall off trees
  • Princess Celestia and Princess Luna rising the sun and moon respectively
  • the following comment: "The Evergreen Forest ain't natural! Trees grow, animals take care of themselves, and clouds move ALL BY THEMSELVES!"

All of this points to there being virtually no natural weather or flora in much of Equestria (excluding said forest).
