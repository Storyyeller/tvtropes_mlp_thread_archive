For comparison, I dug through and compiled all of Zap2it's episode synopses (oddly, they aren't listing their pages for episodes 1-26 and 36):

  • "Friendship Is Magic Part 1": After trying to warn Princess Celestia about the return of the wicked Night Mare Moon, Twilight Sparkle and Spike travel to Ponyville where they meet Pinkie Pie, Applejack, Rainbow Dash, Rarity and Fluttershy.
  • "Friendship Is Magic Part 2": Twilight and the ponies team up in a search for the Elements of Harmony, which will bring back the kidnapped princess.
  • "The Ticket Master": Twilight Sparkle receives two tickets to the Grand Galloping Gala and all her friends want to go.
  • "Applebuck Season": Applejack is determined to complete her Applebuck season harvest alone.
  • "Griffon the Brush-off": Pinkie Pie y Rainbow Dash hacen bromas a los otros ponis; Pinkie Pie es una tercera rueda.
  • "Boast Busters": The arrival of Trixie upsets the other ponies in Ponyville.
  • "Dragonshy": A sleeping dragon must move out of town.
  • "Look Before You Sleep": Twilight has a slumber party.
  • "Bridle Gossip": The ponies come down with a bizarre sickness.
  • "Swarm of the Century": Parasprites descend upon Ponyville.
  • "Winter Wrap Up": Twilight Sparkle prepares for the Winter Wrap-Up.
  • "Call of the Cutie": Apple Bloom searches for her purpose in life so she can receive her cutie mark.
  • "Fall Weather Friends": During the annual Running of the Leaves, competition turns sour.
  • "Suited for Success": Rarity gets overwhelmed when she offers to make individual dresses for each of her friends.
  • "Feeling Pinkie Keen": Twilight Sparkle wants to uncover the source of Pinkie Pie's abilities.
  • "Sonic Rainboom": Rarity loves her new ability so much she disrupts the competition.
  • "Stare Master": Fluttershy offers to babysit the Cutie Mark Crusaders.
  • "Show Stoppers": The Cutie Mark Crusaders hope to discover their true life meaning.
  • "A Dog and Pony Show": Rarity is taken by a group of Diamond Dogs and is forced to find gems for them.
  • "Green Isn't Your Color": Rarity recruits Fluttershy as a model to promote her dresses.
  • "Over a Barrel": Settling a complicated land dispute.
  • "A Bird in the Hoof": Fluttershy borrows the Princess's sick bird so she can heal it.
  • "The Cutie Mark Chronicles": The crusaders look for Twilight and the other ponies to learn how they got their cutie marks.
  • "Owl's Well That Ends Well": Spike is jealous when Twilight becomes friends with an owl.
  • "Party of One": Pinkie Pie tries to learn why her friends are avoiding her party invitation.
  • "The Best Night Ever": The ponies' expectations for the Grand Galloping Gala may be too high.
  • "The Return of Harmony - Part 1": Discord escapes from his stone prison, and Twilight and her friends must act quickly to find the Elements of Harmony to stop him.
  • "The Return of Harmony - Part 2": Discord causes a rift between Twilight and her friends and she must bring them back together in order to defeat him.
  • "Lesson Zero": Twilight loses one of her friendship lessons for her letter to Princess Celestia.
  • "Luna Eclipsed": Luna shows up at the Nightmare Night, macabre party to change her bad pony image.
  • "Sisterhooves Social": Sweetie Belle is upset when her sister Rarity refuses to take part in a rough celebration of sisterly unity.
  • "The Cutie Pox": Apple Bloom panics when she gets not one, but a dozen cutie marks.
  • "May the Best Pet Win!": Rainbow Dash hosts a flying race to see who will be her pet.
  • "The Mysterious Mare Do Well": There is a new hero in Ponyville who is masked and stealing Rainbow Dash's thunder.
  • "Sweet and Elite": Rarity must choose between her friends and important social connections in Canterlot.
  • "Secret of My Excess": Spike gets greedy on his birthday and has a huge growth spurt that could decimate Ponyville.
  • "Family Appreciation Day": Apple Bloom doesn't want Granny Smith to embarrass her by speaking in her class.
  • "Baby Cakes": Pinkie Pie tries babysitting Cake's twin toddlers but finds it harder than she thought.
  • "Hearth's Warming Eve": The six friends are in a play about the founding of Equestria for the Hearth's Warming Eve's holiday pageant.
  • "The Last Roundup": Applejack disappears and the friends set out on a journey to find her.
  • "The Super Speedy Cider Squeezy 6000": The Flim Flam brothers challenge the Apples to a Cider Making Contest for the farm.
  • "Read It and Weep": Rainbow Dash secretly discovers the joys of reading when she's laid up in the hospital.
  • "Hearts and Hooves Day": The Cutie Mark Crusaders make Miss Cheerilee and Big Macintosh fall in love.
