Akinator's a combo of Guess Who, Twenty Questions, and Cleverbot - beat him, and you only make him stronger!

It's a program/website that basically plays twenty questions with you about a fictional character you think of - questions like 'are they involved with vampires', 'does your character die', things like that - and, at the end of twenty questions, it presents its best guess. If it's wrong, you can keep going, and if you've come up with a character no one else has run through yet, you're prompted to submit a picture and name for that character and add it to the database.
