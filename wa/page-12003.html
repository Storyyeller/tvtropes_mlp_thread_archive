On the subject of Human/Equestrian relationships, it gets a bit complicated, but then, there are different ways of expressing love, and even barring that, different kinds of affection. I can definitely see cases where a relationship can develop, but it probably won't progress past the cuddling stage. Seeing how Equestrian culture and morality works, I don't think going farther is necessary. I really don't want to try figuring out how procreation would work (it probably won't).

In False Masks there is a pretty much one-sided pairing of Nearly Everyone/Harry (magic's involved; it's not a harem plot). He's not the least bit comfortable with the attention, as he thinks of the Main Six as surrogate family more or less, making the whole bit awkward, and random natives giving him presents and showering with adoration are more than a bit unnerving.
