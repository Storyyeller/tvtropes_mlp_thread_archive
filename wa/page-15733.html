#393307 Thirikal Sat, 24th Aug '13 8:20:38 PM
@ Story: Tips for doing better RP? are we talking forum RP or tabletop RP, because while examples of good rp from both styles share some characteristics, they are fairly different. As for advice regarding both, never make your RP completely random. You should have some sort of goal your trying to achieve with the RP, telling a story or joining one. Secondly, have a firm grasp of the sort of character you wish to potray during your roleplay experience. Thirdly, don't roleplay as yourself, as that defeats the fundamental purpose of roleplaying to begin with. Incorporating elements of your personality into the character is acceptable to some degree. Fourthly, when roleplaying with others, remember the ideal is to tell a collaborative story so don't try to hog the spotlight. This is all pretty basic stuff, but it'd be foolish to ignore the fundamentals.
