Time for a midday fanfic report!

Dear Thread Manor...

I don't really have a theme for todays stories. They run the gamut from epic action to surprise feels to comedy slice-of-life. So lets jump in at the action side! Then those surprise feels, then the comedy!

The God Empress of Ponykind -in progress. The God Emperor of Mankind finally awakes from the Golden Throne, in order to stop the forces of Chaos laying siege to Holy Terra and take the fight to the Chaos Gods themselves in The Immaterium. After a long, grueling series of battles, the God Emperor stands triumphant over the 4 Chaos Gods, and lets himself be taken by a warp storm so that this new age can proceed on it's own without His influence. Except the warp storm didn't kill Him. He instead finds himself on a barren, volcanic world in the form of a xenos. Specifically, a strange horse creature with a white coat, a horn, wings, and a flowing, ethereal mane...

this is an Equestria origins story with a bit of a twist on it.

Life is a Lemon -in progress. Lemon Dreams is a very happy pony. Well, she wouldn't quite agree with that statement because she's convinced she's not a pony. She's a lemon, and nopony can convince her otherwise. But she really is very happy! She lives on her lemon orchard with all her brothers and sisters(the lemons) and everything is perfect for her! But such a delusion of lemonhood could not have come about naturally. What could have possibly happened in her past to make her believe she is a lemon?...

oh geez, this only has a Slice-of-Life tag, but don't believe it. It starts off comedic and lighthearted, but then it gets real heavy feels real fast.

Quizzical. Quizzical Greystone is a very drab yet brilliant filly. She has a simple light grey/dark gray color scheme, her interests are purely scientific(her cutie mark is a Venn Diagram), and calling her voice monotone would be an insult to the word. Because of all this, she makes pre-Ponyville Twilight look like a social butterfly. On a suggestion from Luna, Celestia assigns her as assistant friendship researcher to Twilight in the hopes that she'll learn a thing or two.

This one is mostly comedy/slice of life with some drama near the end. It's a nice story overall.

Fur and Feathers -in progress. Twilight botches a spell and turns everyponies pets into ponies.Turning them back is beyond Twilight's abilities, so they have to wait for the Summer Sun Celebration in a week when Celestia will be in town so she can fix it. Hilarity Ensues.

This is a fun one. From Gummi having just as much reality breaking powers as Pinkie, to Opal being a completely unrepentant jerkass, there's laughs to be had by all!

Your Faithful Fanfic Reader
