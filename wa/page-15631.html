Cartoons from the 80's tend to be praised for the same reason Ocarina of Time is considered the best Lo Z game by a lot of people: It was first. (Clarifying this before I go any further, OOT was still a good game, but not as good, I feel, as Wind Waker or Twilight Princess)

The 80's was basically when animation for television, or at least a series as opposed to a number of shorts, became viable. Therefore, animated series were a pretty novel concept and the first ones to garner any sort of following or commercial success were praised for starting a medium more than their own individual merits.

Of course there's also the fact that many of the people 'in charge' of doing all the praising grew up with those cartoons, and therefore aren't exactly unbiased...
