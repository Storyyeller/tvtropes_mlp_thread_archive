#234847 Tiggers Are Great Mon, 25th Jun '12 5:45:28 PM
Boy, trying to rate the Mane 6 is hard to do, because every single one of them brings something to the table!

  1. Twilight Sparkle tends to be put in first place, and it's not hard to see why. She's The Hero and the leader who keeps the group together, actually has a personality a number of us, especially nerds, can relate to, and has a number of flaws that keep her interesting.
  2. Rainbow Dash would probably be put in second place. She's The Lancer and is the opposite of Twilight in a number of ways. If you're an athlete or go Tomboys, you'll probably like her a lot.
  3. Fluttershy is likely to be put in third place. She's very feminine, and boys usually don't go for feminine. On the other hand, those emotions she keeps bottled up can be terrifying when she releases them!
  4. Pinkie Pie...I would put her in fourth place. She's a Genki Girl, and normally the sort of character I could never relate to. Then again, the episodes "Party of One", "Baby Cakes", and "A Friend in Deed" go a long way of showing that she has depths and flaws that make her interesting.
  5. Rarity, I'm putting in fifth place. She's into fashion, dress-making, and is rather snobbish, which are rather difficult for me to relate to. But she has shown that she tries to balance her generosity and self-centeredness, not to mention the fact that she has gotten a large amount of screen-time.
  6. Applejack, for me, has to be put in last place. Now, don't get me wrong, I like how she speaks with a Southern drawl, harvests apple trees, is generally honest, and all that. She has a little problem with pride, which bit her in the tail pretty hard a couple of times. For me, the problem with AJ is that she is pretty flat compared to the others. She needs to star in some episodes, and try to fix that!
Oh, Lauren Faust, we stand on guard for thee!
