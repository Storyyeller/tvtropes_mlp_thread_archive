#109048 Perpetual Lurker Mon, 27th Feb '12 1:16:46 PM
@Bluespade I guess "platforming" is a poor word to use, because you wouldn't be jumping from platform to platform so much as avoiding obstacles. Imagine a dense jungle environment. Sure, you could fly above it in relative safety, but below the canopy is where your objective is, and you don't know exactly where it is, either. In order to find it, you have to fly closer to ground level, avoiding predators, traps, or perhaps even natives while you search. Powerful winds and falling rocks could make misuse of flight hazardous while scaling a mountainside. There are plenty of ways to make it work, and if there's anything I enjoy most about a platfomrer, it's creative use of freedom of movement.
