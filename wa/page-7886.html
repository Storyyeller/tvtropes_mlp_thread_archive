#197140 Bluespade Thu, 17th May '12 7:14:27 PM from Texas
@Kegisak: Chie is a good physical fighter who specializes in crowd control, while Kanji specializes in single opponent damage (which means he's better for bosses). Chie also gets some useful passive abilities and damage boosting spells.

Yosuke is the party Magicarp. Actually, he's not bad, but he's nothing special throughout most of the game. He does decent wind damage, has some useful buffs and a few bad status spells, but he's weak phsyically and is only like the 4th best spellcaster. Until you get his last couple abilities which boost his wind damage up to extremely high amounts (making him probably 2nd only to Yukiko) and grants him a VERY powerful physical attack with a high critical rate.

Yukiko is the best healer AND the best damage mage, but she's pretty squishy and easily killed. She also has really high luck, which means you can use her regular attack for pretty reliable critical hits. They won't do any damage worth mentioning, but they knock enemies down so you can do an All Out Attack.

Teddy is 2nd best damage mage (after Yukiko), 2nd best healer, and has a few useful status things. He'd basically be an inferior version of Yukiko if it weren't for the fact that he's much more resistant to damage than she is and has some decent physical attack.

Naoto is great for dungeon crawling with instant kill attacks (which can target ALL enemies), very powerful non-elemental spells, and multi-hit physical attacks. But she's not very good for bosses. Once I got her I used her for every single non-boss battle, because I liked her so much.
