#83959 Enlong Mon, 30th Jan '12 3:04:36 PM from The Magitek Holyland
What's interesting is that the "original designs" for the characters was from before she was even called on for the show. They were simply fan-art of hers for various G1 ponies.

In fact, after the show began, she got rid of the original fan-art, I think because of the same copyright reaons that prevented her from actually using Posey and Surprise and the rest.

Of course, that didn't stop them from making little references: Like Twilight's mother looking like G1 Twilight, or Fluttershy's role in Hearth's Warming Eve being named "Pansy", or that Wonderbolt that has Surprise's color scheme.

Another interesting thing from the pitch bible was that Faust wanted Rainbow to be a prankster in more than one episode, but the execs wouldn't have it.

Of course, that changed as of Luna Eclipsed, so there ya go.
