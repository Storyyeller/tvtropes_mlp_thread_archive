I have to emphatically disagree. I won't deny that there are a lot of fans who'd like to see the show with more of an edge; but I have to say that I don't understand that at all. Cynicism goes against a lot of what it stands for (aside from selling toys, that is, in a massive ironic blow to my argument), so if the show does lose its soul, it'll be in the other direction - sanitized, not cynicized, if I'm allowed to be twee.
