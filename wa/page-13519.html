Hey, you know what we need to take out minds off all the hubbub? a fanfic report!

Dear Thread Manor...

we have a lot of adventure and action today, some in the form of crossovers! Crossovers first, then the original stories.

My Little Chrono Triggers are Magic- in progress. When Rainbow Dash attends her hometowns Millennial Summer Sun Celebration, she meets and befriends a strange pink pony. Then, when testing the newest invention of Dash's best friend, Twilight Sparkle, the pink pony is sucked into a strange portal. Dash and Twilight embark to rescue this pink pony, and things will never be the same...

yep, it's Chrono Trigger with ponies. RD = Chrono, Twilight = Lucca, Pinkie = Marle, Rarity = Glenn, Fluttershy = Robo, and AJ = Ayla. The only thing that bugs me about this story is that everypony is lesbian. Twi Dash, Apple Do, and AJ and RD are both magical lesbian spawn.

My Little Naruto" Friendship is- WHERE AM I!?- in progress. Naruto dies. Death, however, decides he should get a second chance for being such a unique individual. What Death doesn't tell him is that this second chance won't be in his own world or his own body. Shenanigans ensue.

the story is amusing enough, but while there's hints of one I, can't actually see any long range plot goal. oh, and ship teasing with naruto and Twi. I'm sure that will anger some people.

Mother of Invention- in progress. AJ wakes up all alone on a deserted island. She doesn't have the slightest idea how she ended up there, but she's going to have to put her outdoors skill to the test if she wants to survive.

It's just AJ and only AJ. there's very little actual dialogue. Makes for a fairly interesting read.

The Discordian's Daughter- in progress. When Twi, Dash, Pinkie, and Mrs. Cake take a tour of a house of an ancient Equestrian alchemist recently opened to the public, they make a startling discovery. They discover a secret basement and find that this alchemist was secretly a Discordian, a follower of Discord. But more importantly, they discover a young mare, the alchemist's daughter, who should be long dead but is somehow still alive and remembers nothing past when she woke up not too long ago. How has she remained alive so long? Why was she locked up in a hidden basement? What did her father do to her? And is it really safe for her to stay with Twilight while she unravels this mystery?

Just wait till you hear her talk!

my favorite form this batch would have to be the The Discordian's Daughter. an interesting story so far, I can't wait to see where it goes!

Until next time!
