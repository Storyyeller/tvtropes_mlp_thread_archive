My take on E Ps is somewhere between Keg and Applelight's. Basically, the idea is that they have the same amount of magic as unicorns or pegasi, but it's almost entirely passive. A unicorn wouldn't kill a plant by tending to it, but an EP would be far more efficient at it. Like a unicorn running a farm might be able to scrounge together enough for themselves to live off of, but an EP who puts in the same amount of effort is going to have a lot of surplus. Unless they're Pinkie, because she's apparently bad at farming.
