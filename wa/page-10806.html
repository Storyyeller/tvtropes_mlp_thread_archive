http://maxiburger.tumblr.com/post/21925109926/which-are-in-your-opinion-the-aspects-of-mlp-japanese

http://everfreeradio.com/?p=2411

Joshua Walker already had some really good points in that Everfree Radio article. But if you’re asking my personal opinion… I’d say it’s

  1. The cuteness of Lauren Faust’s designs
    • Never underestimate the power of Kawaii. Cuteness is a much more cemented thing in Japanese culture than in ours. For example you don’t really hear men call stuff “cute” in our society (at least not irl), in Japan on the other hand it’s even one of the main desirable attributes in a woman. Of course they share our views on what can be considered “sexy” too. But either way, the cuteness mania is reflected in their fashion, product designs, mascot genre and pretty much all media. And you can’t deny that the Fi M designs are more kawaii than those from earlier Pony generations.
  2. The characters fall into Japanese archetypes
    • Or really, moe-types… Rainbow is a KY bokukko, Fluttershy is the perfect Yamato Nadeshiko and Applejack is an ikemen onee-san. Studious yet socially awkward Twilight certainly appeals to Japanese culture just as much as (oft forgotten) Rarity with her grown-up working morale and designing prowess. On the other hand, brash and loud Pinkie doesn’t seem to click that well with them… unless she switches into Yandere mode. The fact that the show is set in a fantasy world without clear nationalities probably helps them to identify with the characters too.
  3. Good Voice acting
    • Japan loves good voice acting. MLP has some really good voice actors.
  4. Exploitability
    • Japan seems to be even keener than the West when it comes to creating derivative works. So any sort of ambiguous line of dialogue or weird shot has the potential to boost a work’s popularity if processed through fanarts or fanvids. Of course this also holds true in the West, and we’ve pretty much already done all of the possible homework. I’d even suggest that some of our fanart encouraged a number of Japanese watchers to give the show a try. Especially the gijinka versions. Very possibly the porn too.
