@Badwolfwho:Well ya know how you start out looking like a zombie made out of beef jerky? That means you're Hollowed. It doesn't have any negative effects on your character other than looking like crap, but if you use Humanity, then visit a Bonfire, you can choose Reverse Hollow and it will transform you back into human form. This is called unHollowed. When you're unhollowed, you can be invaded by other players who will try to murder you, and you can also use the White Soap Stone (which you don't have yet, but will get soon) to summon other players to help you. Certain bosses in the game are basically intended to be fought with allies, so don't feel bad for resorting to it.

There's a bunch of other things affected by whether or not you're Hollowed once you join a Covenant, but you don't really have to worry about that yet. Oh, and if you're playing offline (the game is much funner if you play online btw) there are sometimes NPC characters you can summon so you're not entirely out of luck.
