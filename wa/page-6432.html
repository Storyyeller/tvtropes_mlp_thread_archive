Someone mentioned that general Equestrian tech is pre-Industrial Revolution, with hints of our modern stuff shining through. They certainly know enough to make powerful telescopes (and thus SLR-class cameras like the ones Photo Finish uses!) and train engine-sized food processors, but they have no radio/television/Internet, still being dependant on Dragon-fax, couriers (literal pony express ), broadsheet/tabloid newspapers, and telegrams.

They certainly have access to glass and rubber and ceramics and at least some form of metallurgy too. I wonder what kind of leather (or Pleather) they use. Given the hazmat suits, they may well have some knowledge of plastics as well.

They don't have much of our war-born technology.
