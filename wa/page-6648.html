I liked this two-parter a lot! I went into it without spoilers this time since they weren't posted yet, and so I was kept guessing as to what was going to happen next. While I was correct with my WMG (not so "wild" after all) that Cadance seeming to be "evil" is because she was being controlled/brainwashed (or at least impersonated), I did not expect the villain to be impersonating her and have her captured in a prison with Twilight! Or the whole plot involving the changelings, either, and the fight with them reminded me of the Powerpuff Girls. And they didn't just use the Elements to save the day, they ended up needing something else, which was Cadance's love powers. At first I was a little annoyed that Luna didn't show up to help her sister, but then I realized after she appeared at the end that she was sleeping because she's nocturnal (one can't expect her and Celestia to be awake ALL the time). So of course she wouldn't be there. Ginger's theory of her being put into a deeper sleep by a spell also could be a good reason for her not to have appeared. It makes sense to me, because if Chrysalis did that then she would only have to deal with Celestia and not have both sisters to contend with.

As to how Pinkie could sneeze confetti, perhaps she got it up her nose because she was checking her party cannon (which she used in the fight) and did the stupid thing where a character sticks their head in the barrel of the cannon to see if it works. And it fired in her face and she got confetti up her nose as a result.
