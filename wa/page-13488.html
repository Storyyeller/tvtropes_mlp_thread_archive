#337196 Rainbow Sat, 9th Feb '13 6:49:18 AM
On a completely different (but still pony-related) topic, I was thinking about Twinkleshine, and the disparity between the doll called Twinkleshine and the character on the show who fans call Twinkleshine. While I know that many, probably most, fans think of the two as being the same character, I'm not quite so sure. The biggest difference is the fact that the doll's coloring doesn't look much like the character on the show at all, with a bigger difference than most ponies who appear in both the brushable toyline and the show (aside from Celestia, and even she was changed to be white like she's supposed to be). Also, in the case of other background ponies who also have brushable dolls, such as Lyra and Cherry Berry, the boxart resembles the character on the show more, while Twinkleshine the doll's boxart looks very different from the white unicorn background pony on the show. However, it is true that the two ponies have the same cutie mark and pink manes (although the doll's mane is a darker pink), although that isn't always a sure thing because there are sometimes other ponies in the series who have identical cutie marks, such as Minuette and Time Turner.

I'm not saying that it's wrong to call the white unicorn on the show "Twinkleshine, " since we don't have a canon name for her otherwise, and there are enough similarities to make it ambiguous. I can see how many fans would have come to the conclusion that the two are supposed to be the same pony, just that one is pink and the other is white. I just don't personally see them as being the same character necessarily. The main reason I brought this up is that I might start a fanfic that focuses on the toyline ponies (including ones who are also background ponies, such as Lyra and Cherry Berry), and I will probably base Twinkleshine in my story off of the toy version rather than the white background pony, unless something happens in canon to contradict this idea.
