#391542 Rainbow Sat, 17th Aug '13 11:03:51 AM
@Sydxelia: Your comment about heavy metal artists sounding like Cookie Monster gave me a funny mental image of Cookie Monster as a rock star. (I like pretty much all kinds of music, myself, I can't say that I dislike any specific genre, more like there might be individual songs I wouldn't like but not an entire genre)

I've been watching through all the pony episodes on weekends on a schedule that will put Magical Mystery Cure as the one I would watch on the Saturday before November 23 (which is when season 4 starts) and today I've watched Call of the Cutie and I noticed some interesting things about it.

One is that Amethyst Star is also at Diamond Tiara's party with Dinky. Given that I'm pretty sure this was the episode that started the fanon idea that Dinky is Derpy's daughter, I thought it was interesting to note that Dinky's canon sister was at the party with her too, although it's probably a coincidence because I doubt that they would have been planning the specific background sister pairs for Sisterhooves Social that far ahead (unless they were and it was an early hint).

The other thing is that at first I thought "Well, now we know Rainbow Dash is to blame for the CMC thinking that you can make your cutie mark appear by trying random activities" but then I realized that it makes sense for Rainbow Dash to think that way. Not necessarily that she got her own cutie mark that way (I don't think she ever said she was engaged in trying to make it happen at the time) but that Rainbow Dash isn't a very introspective pony who would want to do any self-reflective thinking about her talents and passions. She also would know very well what it feels like to be impatient, and is definitely the type who would think "just get out there and make it happen" because that's how she lives life in general.
