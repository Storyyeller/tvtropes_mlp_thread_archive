@Computer Sherpa, and Gingerninja after that: You raise a good point. The people of Equestria don't worship Celestia (in a religious sense; I bet hundreds of young fillies idolize her), and she's open and warm even by regular, non-magical royalty standards - it's hard to believe in a person as an ideal when you encounter them occasionally as, well, a person. And yes, being a god or goddess doesn't necessarily mean you're omni-anything. There are many more polytheistic pantheons than monotheistic... um, pantheons, after all, and most gods tend to oversee certain specific domains rather than the whole of time and space.
