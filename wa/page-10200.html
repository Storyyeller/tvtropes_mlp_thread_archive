I've got an idea on a future episode of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. An idea that is so crazy, it might work. That idea is...a funeral episode with Granny Smith!

Here's how I think it would go: Apple Bloom, Applejack, and Big Mac are doing their usual apple-picking, and they find it weird how Granny Smith has been taking longer naps than usual. Big Mac goes and checks on her. He lets out a cry that brings the other two over to find...Granny Smith in her rocking chair, dead!

Then you have the news spread quickly throughout Ponyville and beyond. A funeral is held for Granny Smith, and you have her family members, and citizens of Ponyville attending it. Celestia even appears there, too. Big Mac is uncharacteristically breaking down in huge tears, Applejack is very stoic, and Apple Bloom is in shock. You then have a number of ponies remembering their interactions with Granny Smith, like Rainbow Dash getting whacked in the face by Granny's purse. Her story from "Family Appreciation Day" gets a mention, and the fact that she spoke in mostly unintelligible gibberish before speaking coherently gets mentioned too.

Apple Bloom is receiving condolences from several of her peers...then Diamond Tiara comes up. DT makes rude comments and says that Equestria is a better place without that "kooky old lady". This causes AB to come out of her shock, and get into a fight with DT in front of everypony. AB then gives her "The Reason You Suck" Speech, saying that DT is only rich and living here in Ponyville because of Granny Smith, and GS has more courage, decency, style and class than DT can ever hope to get. Filthy Rich ends up having to take DT out of there. Silver Spoon decides not to go with Diamond Tiara, because she wants to pay her respects to GS.

You could even have another episode that focuses on the implications that result from GS's death. Namely, Apple Bloom will have to handle the rituals with the Zap Apple Jam, and that is going to take some practice!
