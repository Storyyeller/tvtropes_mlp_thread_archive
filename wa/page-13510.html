okay, long wall of text incoming, it's just, something I want to get out of my head.

That, is pretty much how I feel. With all this fear and anxiety over what might be coming. Of what Hasbro might be doing to this show we love. I think it's time we all jus stopped, took a deep breathe, and looked around. Sure, the show is AMAZING, it is what brought us all together. BUT! It is not, by this point the end all and be all of who we, as a community are. Becuase, to me and I know so so many others, we are not just a fandom anymore, we are a community. A place where so many people, for the first time, feel like they belong.

Will I be sad to see the show end, either through cancellation, or being twisted by Executive Meddling into a pale shell of it's former glory? Oh hell yeah. But it need not be the end. While the show is important. Nothing that may come will change what has already been given to us. The joy, the laughter, the message of friendship that has touched so many, change so many peoples lives for the better, will still be there. We, as a community, quite honestly do not need the show to continue to keep it's spirit alive. What is it that kept us going during the long droughts between seasons? Each other. Our art, music, stories. And, as cliched as this sounds, our friendship.

I have made so SO many more close, dear friends since becoming a brony then I have ever had before. And they are not predicated solely upon us watching MLP. But on us simply being friends. We produce so much on our own, have such creativity, and such a bond to each other (For the most part). That while loss of the show would be a blow, it needs not be a mortal one. We can choose to stay, to preserve the lessons we've learned, to keep the fires of friendship burning.

I know I'm rambling and going on to long, so let me just sum it up. To me, and to so so many more, this fandom is more then just, well fans, it is a group, a shining beacon of love, acceptance, friendship. A place to belong, to be welcomed whole heartedly. That, is what, to me, makes us special. not simply loving a show, even one like MLP. But being dedicated to each other. To being a bastion of hope, creativity, and caring where all are welcomed and accepted. And I hope, beg, ask, and pray. That when the time comes, when the show that started this movement ceases to be. Either fully, or as it once was. That we do not put out that beacon. But stay together. Keep it burning.

Though FIM my end, it's lessons, it's impacts, the lives it has touched and changed will remain. Please, everyone. Calm down, look around, and see what this fandom and this show has given us so far. Nothing can take that away. Only you can choose to abandon them. We can survive, as long as we stick together. No matter what happens to the show, I will awalys be proud to be a brony. And I hope others feel the same

To all the bronies out there. I love you all

Seraphem.
