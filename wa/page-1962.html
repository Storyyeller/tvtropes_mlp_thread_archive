@Fallen: Japanese culture values stoicism and maintaining one's poise and composure a lot more than most western cultures do. They'd typically place a pretty high medium on keeping to oneself and not being overly emotional. Something like crashing a party by acting wild would be an even more enormous faux pas for them than it would be in the west.

Things like talking over your feelings at length, which is pretty common in show, would be kinda weird to them. Given the situations in the show, they'd probably edge more to the side of "let everypony work out their own issues rather than burden others with them", which is probably why they think the ponies are "too" forward.
