#114561 Perpetual Lurker Sun, 4th Mar '12 12:53:24 AM
So I just rewatched the episode, and man, the production values just seem to be way better than normal on it (and they're pretty damn good normally, too). My favorite bit is when Fluttershy is tied up in her house, and they show a close up on her face. They really nailed the lighting there, which set the mood perfectly. As if that wasn't enough, they even shift the focus on the background to indicate when Pinkie Pie and Rarity are talking, even though they aren't in the shot. And then there's the music, which is above and beyond even for this show. First you have all the direct references, which are awesome. Then you have some really cool original stuff, like the music when Fluttershy leaves her house to confront the mailpony. That's a wicked theme for Flutterbitch. There's just so much cool I'd be gushing forever if I listed all the neat little things I noticed.
