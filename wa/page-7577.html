Has anyone ever noticed the similarities between the Mane 6 and the four Desperate Housewives?

Bree Hodge (formerly Van De Kamp) is so much like Rarity that she actually kind of looks like her. She's proper, is a homemaking and cooking guru (similar to Rarity's talent in fashion design), has a bit of a more "affected" accent from her friends considering she's still an American. Plus, although she's not really a drama queen, she's pulled into high stakes drama a lot.

Susan Del Fino (formerly Myers) is sort of like Pinkie Pie. She's a klutz, very cheerful, and she hates it when people are sad or angry with her and will usually do anything in her power to make others happy. (She could also be like Fluttershy, especially after *spoiler* her husband was killed this season. She's softer and kinder, to a point, and she's helping out her pregnant daughter more.)

Lynette Scavo resembles Applejack - a workaholic mother (used to have a high profile job before she had kids) and is more sensible and has a knack for business and working in general.

Gabrielle Solis is sort of like Rainbow Dash, if you replace "cool" with "sexy." She's the more outgoing firecracker of the group and always wants to look and act her best. She's stubborn and can be a bit lazy. (She could also be like Rarity.)
