There are some good patterns you can buy for fairly cheap. The one I am using is from Valley Violet on Deviant Art and Etsy. I think I paid 15 USD for the mare pattern.

This Derpy is made out of Flannel... I couldn't find any Fleece in the right color or I would have used that. The fleece is much more forgiving and stretches a little more.

Her wings are floppy cuz I didnt wire them or put any stabilizer in them. I wanted to see how they came out just plain. Not very impressive. Next plush will have a pipe cleaner or some kind of iron on stabilizer to give them more shape. This plush is prettymuch an Experiment for me... I want to work on the head a bit since I dont like how pointy her muzzle is.

That is what Im doing to make the mane and tail... its rather tedious.

Next pony Im going to work on will be a Bat-Pony. Im going to do his mane in fabric though since Im afraid to try and style the yarn too much. Maybe once I get better at hiding the knots you get when you make the tufts.

Third plushie... I'm thinking about doing Firefly from G1 since she was my favorite. She's going to have a yarn mane too... or Mohair if I can find the right color!
