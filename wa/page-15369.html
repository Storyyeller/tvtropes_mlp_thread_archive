The great thing about BL is, despite being the same character...they are different people.

The first BL, Prince Edmund Plantagenet, Duke Of Edinburgh, was a complete idiot and a simpering, cowardly, sneaky little creep.

The second one, his great-grandson Lord Blackadder, was clever, witty, snarky, and still devious and sneaky...but also very impulsive, reckless, arrogant and prone to digging himself into holes.

The third one, Mr Blackadder esquire, was cold, calculating, and utterly ruthless...and much smarter than the previous two.

The fourth one, Captain Blackadder, was much more weary and defeated than his ancestors, and just wanted to survive, never mind gaining power or influence.

Rule of thumb was, as time went on, BL got poorer but smarter...and Boldrick got stupider and less hygienic.
