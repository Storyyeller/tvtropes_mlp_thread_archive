I just rewatched A Dog and a Pony Show. We need more episodes like that one, I think it's my 3rd favorite of the series.

Anyway: Applejack. Applejack is low on my favorites, possibly lowest now, for a simple reason. The good thing about her is that she is the non-crazy one, the smart and dependable straight-pony who gets things done and doesn't flip out all the time. I like those sorts of characters, and as such, I'd say she has potential to be among my favorite.

The issue for me is her dialogue. Practically everything she says is an absolutely ridiculous, made up old timey wild west expression that noone would ever use in real life. It turns her from a real character into a caricature. It especially makes me cringe since I myself am Texan. Imagine you are British, and one of the main characters spoke only in ridiculous stereotypical British expressions. "Hullo, guvner, hows yer bobby today, anyone up for a spot of tea, cheerio, what what?" Cringeworthy.

Honestly, it's a testament to the show's writing that I can still like her at all.
