About Fluttershy being scared of Twilight levitating the animals but being fine with them flying through Ghastly Gorge, two things I thought of. One is that she may simply be afraid of the fact that the animals are left to Twilight's mercy and magic, meaning that Twilight is magically moving them, whereas with the Gorge race, the animals in that part were doing it all on their own. That is, they were in control of their bodies and their own movements. Also, if I remember correctly, the animals Twilight was moving with TK were generally smaller and probably ones that Fluttershy would worry about, whereas the ones in the Gorge race, aside from probably Tank and the bat, were physically sturdier in general. Also, I could be forgetting something, but weren't all the animals Twilight was levitating all animals that couldn't fly? Perhaps that factored into Fluttershy's worries as well, like if Twilight's TK suddenly stopped for some reason and they fell.

And about her being afraid to see Zecora, she probably wasn't afraid of going to Twilight so much that she was afraid of what Trixie might do to her if she was caught, perhaps? Or what Trixie might do to her animals as a threat?
