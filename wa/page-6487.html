@Lurker: But even at the best of time she still pretty obsessively schedules things - the checklists have come up several times. You'd probably be fair in saying there are certain things that kick it into overdrive, and it's probably an anxiety issue - she likely schedules and organizes the way she does out of some desire for control. When things start going against her she falls back on it desperately, trying to regain her 'control' of the situation.

It likely comes from being the princesses protege. That's a hefty title to give a kid of any age, and she probably assumed a good deal of responsibility came with it. Not wanting to disappoint her teacher she fell into the mentality of needing to be perfect, and always in control, which she just couldn't do. The lists came as the natural result of needing to be perfect - if she could organize it, it would be easier for her to be 'perfect'.
