#49020 Cyberbanjo Sat, 3rd Dec '11 6:39:12 PM
Took me way longer than I would have liked to see this episode, since there seemed to be a hiccup in getting an HQ version up this morning, and I was busy for the rest of the day. Some impressions:

  • This was actually one of my favorite episodes in a while. Which is odd because my opinion was a little lukewarm as I watched it, but it was the first one since Lesson Zero that I wanted to rewatch immediately upon finishing.
  • I'm glad I didn't watch it this morning when my roommate was around. I was thinking of coming out of the brony closet, but as I was watching, I kept thinking that this certainly wasn't the episode to do it with.
  • Meghan Mc Carthy has finally surpassed M.A. Larson as my favorite writer. They've always been neck-and-neck, but while I love M.A. Larson's adventures, he seems to occasionally sacrifice characterization for the story. Meghan, meanwhile, draws upon it to make the episode better.
  • Woo! The Gala ponies are back!
  • Woah, Luna's body type on a regular unicorn?
  • Octavia, Bruce Mane and Blueblood? Me gusta.
    • I was more excited by Blueblood's reappearance than I thought I would be. From a Doylist perspective, they were obviously just re-using assets to fill the song, but I'd love to hear the story behind his and Rarity's apparently cordial relations. I'm sure a fanfic will show up one of these days.
  • DAT MUSIC. Both the song and the score were particularly on-point today. For some reason, I heard a bit of Return of Harmony's leitmotif when Twilight explained she moved her party. I guess they could have reused the music like they sometimes do, but it seemed like a new composition to me.
  • Yay Continuity Nods!
  • Oh hey, the Fifth Doctor is back!
  • ELEVENTH DOCTOR PONY WAEDBGTAKEBASTDSADG
  • I actually sympathized with Rarity for pretty much all of the episode, even when she's lying and especially the Two Timer Date. Twilight's dancing was adorkable, but it made me really embarrassed for Rarity. I'm not sure I like what all that says about me.
    • Even so, why doesn't Rarity just tell the truth about her friends early on? I mean, even ignoring the saving-the-world-twice thing, Twilight is Celestia's prized pupil, Fluttershy was one of Photo Finish's top models who one day just vanished out of the limelight with no explanation, Dash is the Best Young Flier, Applejack runs a fairly prominent apple-supply company, and Pinkie is probably infamous for jumping onstage at last year's Grand Galloping Gala right before all hell broke loose.
