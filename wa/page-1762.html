I still think that AJ is the Boring, but Practical pony. Yeah, she doesn't have much flash (and I don't think big flashy moves fit her character very well anyway), but she's by far the most reliable pony. Yeah, she broke down a bit in Applebuck Season, but it took several straight days of hard work with no sleep to cause trouble. Pretty much all the other ponies have rather crippling issues:

  • Twilight's social skills suck, and her magic backfires massively on several occasions
  • Rainbow Dash's stunts fail almost as often as they work, and she tends breakdown immediately the second something goes wrong
  • Pinkie Pie has no clue how to communicate well, and if the singing telegram thing is anything to go by, she has very little stamina
  • Fluttershy is cripplingly shy in most circumstances, and she totally loses the ability to fly if she's too stressed
  • Rarity isn't exactly the most empathetic pony; and saying that she's a drama queen is a massive understatement
  • Applejack's main flaw is that she works too hard. As far as character flaws go, that's a pretty good one to have.

And as far as the hoof wrestling: it uses the forelegs. Applejack's hind legs get all the workout from bucking apples. They're probably several orders of a magnitude stronger than her forelegs.
