I haven't read MLD, so I have no idea what the quality of it is, but it's an entirely valid approach for an author to take an existing premise and rewrite it in a different manner. Granted, if you're only doing so because you think the initial attempt was bad, it does come across as kind of dickish, but even so different people like different things and rewriting a story in a manner that appeals to one's own tastes can turn out a work of fine quality.

Heck, my own most popular story is basically every "x has a crush on y" plot, only applied in a specific way.

And for what it's worth, MLD is kind of infamous for the Unfortunate Implications of the protagonist basically isolating himself from the rest of the world; so a take on the story that attempts to avert those would be interesting.
