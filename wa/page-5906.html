#147644 kegisak Thu, 5th Apr '12 11:53:12 PM
It's a french cartoon that's being made in conjunction with an MMO(Though the MMO itself is in Development Hell - I believe it's still in Beta about 4 or 5 years after I first found it). The story follows a small band of adventurers, whose goal varies from season to season - in season 1 the princess of a kingdom of druids has a vision of danger, so she sets off on a quest and picks up the main character Yugo along the way, who turns out to be a member of a ancient race of hyper-powerful beings. One of thier artifacts fell into the hands of an insane time wizard who wants to use it to turn back time and save his family, which would require him to drain the life-force of the planet.

In the second season Yugo finds out more about his race, and finds out there's a way to save a fair chunk of them. He sets out to find a dofus - the eggs of his people which contain one of what he is - the eliatropes - and one dragon, the eliatropes natural brother. the dofus would give him enough power to save his people. The plot of that season is a bit more obscure for most of it; the major antagonist is the equivalent of satan, but he doesn't show up until about 6 episodes from the end of the season.

I figure Ginger would like it for a single reason: The villains are hyper competent, and they stay that way. It takes a god to defeat Season 2's [[theDragon dragon]]. It also has insanely good animation, especially near the final episodes of each season. The fights scenes in it are amazingly choreographed, the characters are all likeable, it's funny... it's a really, really damn good show on the whole.
