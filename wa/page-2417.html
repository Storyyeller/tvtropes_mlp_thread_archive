@Pony Abridged Series: I'd say that none of the Abridged Series out there are perfect. If you put them together, however, you'd have a pretty awesome product. My favorite bits are (purely from memory):

Friendship Is Witchcraft's songs, great and varied voicecast, quotable lines and sometimes clever plot deviations (like turning "Look Before You Sleep" into Twi forcing her friends to reenact a horrible shipping fanfic written by her).

The Mentally Advanced Series' Dashie is pretty lovable. So is Apple Bloom, a great Only Sane Man. Scootaloo is great. The dialogue often is super-fast-paced and cleverly constructed. Also THRAKERZOD.

Camaraderie Is Supernatural also has a nice cast. Applejack changing accents with every line is funny, but Pinkie as an actual Fangirl of the show within the show is genius. Also, the meta message of the pilot episodes is really cleverly made, showing what an Abridged Series should be.
