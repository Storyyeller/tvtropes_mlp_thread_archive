How funny that the one who most wanted to help Rainbow was the one who lost track of her original mission so easily (but, to be fair, those were dangerously fabulous wings.

In spite of her jealousy, she kept herself from telling Fluttershy that, so that she could let her keep doing what she was sure would make her happy. There are quite a few shows where someone in that situation wouldn't be that self-conscious until the end of the episode/aesop moment.
