That was a pretty amusing story, on various levels. First of all, it was rather funny in itself (ponies seem to be at their goofiest when they're sleep-deprived), but I think I was most amused by the irony of Twilight scaring herself because she was trying to tell herself NOT to be scared. It made me think about how there are at least several stories that were intended to be set in the far future when they were first created, but are now either in the present or have turned into the past (like that one story in The Simpsons set in the year 2010).
