I think you would like a lot of things. The problem is that your tastes are so narrow that even with shows/books/whatever that would fit your criteria, you can't stand to read/watch/play past the first few episodes/chapters/levels thanks to normal storytelling conventions. Most stories take their time in establishing the villains/threats because A: if you bring out the big guns right off the bat, then there's no room for the hero to grow, and B: the story needs time to establish the characters/setting before the villain rips it all to shreds. It's just the way that stories work, and you seem to be completely incompatible with them.
