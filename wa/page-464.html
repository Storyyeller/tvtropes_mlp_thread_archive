On the note of darkfic... I don't have an issue with character death if it's handled well (and that includes Anyone Can Die-style deaths, up to and including Killed Mid-Sentence - for most fandoms; I'm still feeling out my limits for FiM, though it's not really something I care to explore that closely).

For instance, the two-parter "Centerpiece of My Collection" is about Spike as a young adult, and later as an elder dragon - and the centerpiece of his collecttion is (spoilers aren't shocking, just a bit sad): Rarity, who's elected to have herself petrified so that she can be beautiful forever. At the end of the second part, Spike frees her - which she's upset about, in true Rarity fashion - and they lie down together and die peacefully when the spirits of the other girls come to them. I have no issue with that, especially given that it was beautifully written. But getting gory about it, or killing ponies randomly...
