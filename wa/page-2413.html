Here's my take on those:

  • Twilight is primarily intellectual in her knowledge; give her a book on something and she'll memorize the crap out of it and be able to comprehend it, no problem. Thing is, most of her intellect is theoretical; she has difficulty applying it properly.

  • Applejack is basically the opposite of Twilight; she probably has very little patience for book knowledge (she'd probably find a textbook on botany horribly boring), but she can pick up practical that she's interested in and needs to know really quickly. Like she has no idea why crop rotation works, but knows exactly when to do the rotating, and for how long, and so forth.

  • Pinkie Pie is more a repository or random trivia than anything else. On occasion it turns out to be usefully. Outside of that, I agree with Red's assessment, she's not stupid, but her brain is so different from all the other ponies that they have no clue how she gets from point A to point B.
