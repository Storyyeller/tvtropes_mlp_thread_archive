Butting heads is a game. In the most genuine situations, those attacks and humiliations are harmless jabs. The people involved know each other well enough to understand that they are not genuine attacks. Not only that, it's an excellent way to air out petty grievances while they are still petty, instead of letting them foster.

It's also a demonstration of trust. The difference between a genuine attack and play is the intention behind it. You can accept ribbing and hard truths from someone you care about that, if it came from anybody else, would be fighting words because you trust their intentions and their judgement.
