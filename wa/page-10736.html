#268382 Japanese Teeth Sun, 12th Aug '12 3:32:31 PM from Meinong's jungle
I tend to lean on the side of geocentricity for the setting, mainly because I think it's more consistent to have the ponies controlling the seemingly natural occurrences. It just fits the setting more, and Celestia would have to be stupidly powerful to move the entire planet around a full sized son. It's possible, but I think it just makes more sense to say that the sun and moon are just heavenly bodies that Celestia and Luna can move around because they're smaller than in reality. That and I think that it's a lot easier to just explain the meteor shower as occurring some other way rather than to assume it works just like it would in reality and then extrapolate Celestia's and Luna's abilities from it.
