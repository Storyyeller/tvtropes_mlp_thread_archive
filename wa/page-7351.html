Hurricane and Pansy were in an army similar to the sacred band of Thebes.

For the unawares, the sacred band of Thebes was the elite Thebian fighting force. It was one of the most feared armies of its day, able to fight off even the legendary spartan warriors despite being only 300 strong. In it's final battles, the sacred band fought to the last man, some even choosing to commit suicide rather than be captured. The sacred band of Thebes was comprised of 150 homosexual couples.

The idea is that the couples would fight more ferociously to protect their lover, and never run for fear of looking cowardly to their love. It worked amazingly. It would explain why Pansy and Hurricane operated together in spite of Pansy not being really inclined to move through the ranks, although it does have a bit of unfortunate implications regarding their relationship itself.
