@Kegisak: Basically, somebody puts down an adjective (Sexy, enormous, scary, whatever), and everybody else has to play the noun card from their hand that they think fits it the best and who ever put down the adjective picks the one they like most. Given that each person only has like 7 cards, it inevitably results in most of the words being mostly unrelated. It isn't uncommon to draw a word like "Cute" and end up choosing between "My Bathroom", "Mushrooms", "Helen Keller", "Pocket Lint", and "The New York Times".
