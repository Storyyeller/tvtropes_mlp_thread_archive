Speaking of Derpy, this is the first time I've seen her with the weird eyes since the first episode. Been looking out for her.

Basically, she appears that way starting in FPK. The initial appearance in episode 1 was originally supposed to be a one time joke by the background animators. However, they started putting it in on purpose starting with FPK, once they heard about the fandom popularity. Because it's only a minor background detail done in the last stage of production, they were able to do it while season 1 was still going. Most of the fandom references don't start until season 2 though.
