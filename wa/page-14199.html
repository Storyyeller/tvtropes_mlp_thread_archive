It's mostly controversial because its first draft was really, really rough, the OC was a ridiculous Sue, and most of the major plot developments happened when characters did some very uncharacteristic things ‚Äî its initial fame was mostly from the My Little Dashie I WANT FEELS NAO crowd. Since then it's been extensively rewritten twice now, it's considerably more believable for the effort, and most of the drama over it is leftover from people who didn't know/care that the author actually responded to feedback and fixed most of its issues over time (certainly the most grating ones).

IMO it's a good example of an author finding his feet over time. It was actually really cool to watch a cringeworthy "look at my OC!" story gradually morph into an honestly poignant "look how messed up everyone is without really being anyone's fault!"
