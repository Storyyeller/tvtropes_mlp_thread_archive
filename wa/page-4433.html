#110824 Delvarian Wed, 29th Feb '12 9:45:34 AM
Well, I believe that ponies heal faster and have more defense because of their innate magic. To clarify, they have regenerating health, innate/constant magic shields, and cure/heal spells - all courtesy of magic. The doctors probably used a cure spell to heal the flesh damage, and some regents and other spells to mend the broken bones.

The reason why Dashie stayed in the hospital for 6 days (instead of just going out after a few hours of the healing) was because the mend bone spell was more of a basic-level one, and just slightly repaired the bone - making it easy to fracture yet, her body could repair it via regeneration in a few days. They probably used the basic one as the more complicated and stronger spells would require much more magic power and more expensive regents.
