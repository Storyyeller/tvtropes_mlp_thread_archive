Not sure exactly. To be perfectly honest, the idea of the Elements of Harmony strikes me as kind of silly in the first place, so making shadow counterparts for them is silliness on top of silliness. That said, Sideboard of Harmony's depiction of the Elements of Discord makes sense.
"Charisma, kindness as a means."
"Vanity, generosity towards oneself."
"Mischief, laughter at the expense of others."
"Ambition, loyalty until inconvenient."
"Cunning, honesty at its most selective."
"Chaos, the heart of magic."
As the story itself points out, none of these elements are inherently evil, but they have no training wheels to prevent being misused for evil. (At the end, Discord's last joke is that he names Celestia as the Bearer of the Element of Cunning.)

ANYWAY, Lightning Dust is definitely Ambition. And a good case could be made for Trixie being chaos, even aside from her being Twilight's darker foil.
