Another interesting thought: if the three pony tribes each have their own innate magic, what do the other hooved animals have? I would think that Donkies have much longer lifespans than Ponies. In Cranky's flashback, the black and white filter would imply that it took place quite along time ago, especially when compared to the sepia filter on Granny Smith's flashback. Add to that the fact that Cranky and Matilda were adults back then, and while they looked older in the present, they certainly we're that old, and I would guess that Donkeys might have lifespans at least twice as long as Ponies, if not more. Also, I'm fairly sure donkeys live quite a bit longer than horses in real life, too, so there's some more precedent.

That's just my overanalytical look at it, and I have no idea what kind of magic other races could have.
