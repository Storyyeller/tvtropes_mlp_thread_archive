Beginning as separate kingdoms hostile to one another, the fledgling civilizations struggled in the frozen landscape, but as time past the lands thawed, and the griffons flourished. Eventually, the four kingdoms (expies of France, Spain, Austria and Italy) united into the Griffon Empire. Possessing no real magic of their own, they instead turned to technology.
