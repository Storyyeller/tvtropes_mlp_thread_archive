I know I missed my opportunity to do this last nigt, but my two cents on the whole tabletop discussion. I do enjoy some of the stuff Pathfinder did to make some of the weaker classes more powerful. For example, Rangers get a decent boost since combat styles now give you a choice of feats at combat feat levels rather than it being a linear progression. Balance issues still persist though. That's one reason I enjoy Savage Worlds. Purely mundane characters may not be as flashy as those with supernatural ability, but can generally perform comparably and don't have to worry about running out of power points. And depending how they are built, the difference between a character in the seasoned tier compared with someone in a higher tier isn't necessarily all that drastic.
