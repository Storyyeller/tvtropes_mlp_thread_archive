#280730 ATC Sat, 8th Sep '12 5:39:38 PM
The main character, Luffy, sacrificed his ability to swim for the ability to have a rubber body.

He can use himself like a slingshot or trampoline, he can stretch very far, he doesn't feel cold because Rubber doesn't conduct heat (thus, it doesn't conduct the absence of heat), he will squeeze himself into small spaces, and he can do some pretty cool feats of contortionism. If you punch him in the stomach really hard, it won't break his ribs, it will just stretch out his stomach, and then the force of your punch will rebound back on you.

That's just the tip of the iceberg, there are other characters with one special ability who find dozens of ways to apply it.

The only cost of getting one of those abilities is the ability to swim.
