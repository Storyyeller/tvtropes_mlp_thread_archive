Namely, the murders. Coming up with nine or ten G-Rated ways to represent the ponies “dying” is pretty tough. I’ve got a few ideas, such as getting caught by a trapdoor or apparently falling off of a cliff (including, of course, a miniature figurine of that character being mysteriously broken as they vanish), but I’m largely drawing blanks.
