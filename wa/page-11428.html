Arguably my favorite episode of the whole show. I think it was also the pivot that made me adore Twilight personality wise. I had watched a random batch of episodes at that point and kinda saw her as the blander "normal" addition to the cast. Then I saw this episode, not only did it have Twilight display a huge defining flaw and go COMPLETELY APESHIT with it, it did so via what was actually a rather relatable motive and fear as a kid (terrified of angering peer). In a sense it made Twilight both more vibrant and goofy AND sympathetic at the same time.

It's funny how a lot of the time, I think a character gets more depthful the kookier they act up.
