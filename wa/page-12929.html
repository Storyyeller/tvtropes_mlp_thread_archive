ok, time for an episode day fanfic report!

Dear Thread Manor...

only 5 fics today, but the really cover the spectrum as far as story types go! Lets start with a first for my fanfic reports, a fanfic that I would not recommend to anyone.

Day One. A human winds up in Equestria, except it's not not a very realistic Equestria, it's 100% cartoon Equestria. All the Animals Lack Attributes, Nobody Poops, and to top it all off, the world itself doesn't seem to appreciate the human seeing just how far he can bend the limits of this cartoon world. Will he ever be able to get home?

This was the strangest story. I was really enjoying reading it, right up until the final chapter. Suddenly, a giant Deus ex Machina comes out of nowhere out and sorta kinda resolves the plot(it's left unclear exactly what happens) in a most unsatisfactory manner. I do not recommend this story, it's just setting you up for disappointment.

Yesterday Is Today -in progress. Discord has been defeated, and Canterlot is in full celebration mode. That is, until Celestia and Twilight set off a trap left by Discord when they return the Le3memtns of Harmony to their special storage room. The trap levels Canterlot Castle, and would have consumed all of Canterlot had Celestia not managed to contain it from the ceneter of the destruction, where she was trapped. When Twilight re-enters the ruins of Canterlot to find Celestia, but she doesn't find the loving, caring mentor that she remembers. She instead finds a battle hardened warrior of blood and iron. he instead finds the Celestia that originally fought Discord over 1000 years ago. How will this warscarred Celestia adapt to modern day Equestria?

This one looks like it will be real interesting, but it's still a bit too early to tell. I'm definitely keeping an eye on it though.

More Than Her Mane. When Spike greet Pinkie at the Library, he's shocked and worried to see her mane and tail are flat and straight. Pinkie assures him that it's just a new look she's trying out and that there's nothing wrong, and is eventually able to convince him of the fact. However, when the rest of Pinkies friends see her, they're not so easy to convince...

a short one shot slice of life type story. It's certainly worth a read.

Apples and Wheat- in progress. The Apple Family is, for the first time in years, planting some wheat on the farm. Granny Smith performs an old ritual that summons a familiar wise old wolf spirit, Holo, to help them with their wheat farming. Much shenanigans and Apple family backstory ensue.

I've never read/seen Spice And Wolf, but I enjoyed what's there so far. Will be following this. and maybe watch a few episodes of spice and wolf, the concept of the show seems pretty neat.

Interview With A Princess. On a whim, and not really expecting anything to happen, a man writes a letter to Princess Celestia requesting an interview with her and burns it in a firepit in his backyard. Imagine his surprise then, when Celestia shows and up agrees to do an interview! Much exposition on Earth/Equestria ensues.

This seemed mostly a way for the author to throw out some headcannon of his without having to really make a plot to fit it, but it's really well written, so I'd recommend it.

That's all for today all, see you next time!
