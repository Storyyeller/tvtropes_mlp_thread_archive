#362138 darkabomination Mon, 29th Apr '13 11:10:13 PM from deep within the Time Vortex
Way back when in the topic about languages, I had the idea to translate the main theme into Quenya after considering what a dub of it might sound like. It's a completely pointless activity and I wasted a few hours on this thing, but I finally finished it. It's more than likely rough in spots, but speakers should at least get the jist of it.

  • Tyenya titta olompo,

  • Tyenya titta olompo,

  • Ai Ai Ai

  • Nyë elmendë mana nilmë naecë?

  • Tennatye hyandanyë sina †ingolnë óni.

  • Hoa raxë lendai,

  • úvë i alassë.

  • Vanya fëa,

  • voronda nar tulca.

  • Hyandauva, aseaië,

  • Nas mótalë carda,

  • Nar †ingolë ilya.

  • Tyenya titta olompo,

  • Nai nyëhanya? tyet alli #meldër.

And here's the literal translation:

  • My little pony,

  • My little pony,

  • I used to imagine what friendship might be?

  • Before you all came and showed this magic to me.

  • Exciting journeys,

  • bursting with joy.

  • A shining spirit,

  • steadfast and brave.

  • Sharing compassion,

  • no difficult feat.

  • And magic completes it all.

  • And did you all know? You're my cherished sisters.

OK, so Quenya's ambiguousness can make for some interesting back translation, but it's the closest I could get with the lyrics being so open to interpretation which Quenya doesn't express very well.

Anyway, there goes another wasted chunk of my life to ponies.
