#134840 kegisak Sat, 24th Mar '12 2:10:48 PM
Okay... so.

That episode was AWESOME. Like seriously, probably competing for my favourite, now. I loved Dash's relationship with Fluttershy, how she genuinely cared about her, not just winning the record. I think it was probably one of the first times we've genuinely seen that famous loyalty of hers, and it was beautiful to see. I loved the moral of the episode, too - the letter even had me tearing up a bit.

The gags were all pretty great - I loved the beef-colt hopping in and screaming, I almost chocked on my lunch when he first showed up. Fluttertree was great, as was the paltry training montage.

The incidental characters were great - Pegasi are my favourite tribe, really, and there were a lot of really cute ones here. Thunderlane, those two with the cool hair, plus that blue pegasus who got the 11.0 power. I think I'll be keeping an eye on him...

The only thing that gets me about the episode is the whole record thing. If they lost 8 pegasi and just barely had enough to actually raise the water, they wouldn't of come even close to the record - they would have hit about 880 at the minimum, a hundred from the last record. There's no way 8 pegasi could combine to total 180 WP. And the record seems sort of unfair. Pitting a small town up against a city? Gee, I wonder who wins...

But yeah, absolutely amazing episode, to be sure. It made me just wanna give Shy a great big hug.
