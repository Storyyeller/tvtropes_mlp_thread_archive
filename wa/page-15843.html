#396070 Kuroi Tsubasa Tenshi Fri, 6th Sep '13 11:41:38 AM from Canada
The higher quality plushies tend to go fro $300-400 for main six, about half as much for stallions or popular background ponies and possibly throw on another $100 if it's a princess. Top tier plushie makers can generally get $800-1200, depending on the pony (consider that the top tier is that way because theses are pretty much sewing masters, though).

Unique one-of-a-kinds tend to go for a lot more too. Like the nearly 5 foot tall Rainbow Dash (the size was massive novelty boost to the price tag) that went for $4900 or the Gala!Shy and Gala!Dash, which went for somewhere around $2800 and $2600, respectively.

EDIT: Should add that with decent quality plushies coming from companies like 4DE en mass and with a much cheaper price tag, I think the non-top tier plushie makers are going to see people willing to pay less and less.
