#262079 Perpetual Lurker Mon, 30th Jul '12 1:54:14 PM
AU's in general are rather dangerous, because the immediate and obvious use for them is to "fix" what you didn't like/can't use from the base universe in order to tell the story you want, or to drastically alter characterizations for your own purposes. They also have huge potential for interesting scenarios, especially when contrasted with the main universe. For that reason, I much prefer the Multiverse approach to AU's than simply using a standalone AU.
