Chad, could I recommend The Chronicles of Amber by Roger Zelazny? It is a ten novel series focusing on war and familial rivalry among the princes of Amber. The initial novel focuses on the third son of a seriously screwed-up family trying to reclaim his birth-right as well as revenge on the brother who left him for dead. What makes this different from most other fantasy is that the only "actual" reality is that of Amber, with our own reality and countless parallel worlds existing as shadows cast by Amber.
