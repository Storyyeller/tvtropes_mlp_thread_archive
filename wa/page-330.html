@Fallen Hehe, actually, I think trying to relate them too much to real life equines IS my problem. I have a guide book with a basic skeletal type of guidelines that I just take a quick glance at whenever I'm feeling confused (I had to look at it a lot when I first started drawing ponies, which shows that the training grounds have taken me quite far), but surprisingly (for a manga book) the proportions are closer to real and on top of that, it's actually a guide for regular horses. So when trying to draw in the show's style, there's a good chance I need to stop and correct myself because I made the head a bit too small (seriously, ML Ps have massive heads) and I make the torso too long. You probably have all noticed that I still tend to make the heads a little smaller and the torsoes a little longer than the show, which has become a stylistic choice now.
