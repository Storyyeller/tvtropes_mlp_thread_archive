The inability to erase is a bit daunting, but that's why you just have to get it right in pencil first. :P In fact, since you're going to erase all the pencil anyway, you don't need to worry about the quality of your pencils or erasing lines while you're working. Just get the shape you want defined with your pencil lines, then only ink over the lines you want to keep.
