I liked it for the most part, it's just that there are one or two flaws that need to be addressed. The big one is the one Canonier brought up, specifically the "weaponized annoyance" thing. While I like the fact that Pinkie is finally being acknowledged as a bit annoying in-universe, I think the mane cast went too far when trying to get AJ to talk to them. I mean, trying to convince her to tell them what's going on is one thing, but trying to coerce her into telling them about it is overkill.
