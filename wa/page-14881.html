The bit about Gilda got me thinking. As a filly at Flight Camp, she seemed to be the kind of person who was perfectly fine with sticking up for someone she probably didn't know very well. And head-on against 3 (probably athletic) boys, no less.

So how did Rainbow Dash go from that to the selfish jerk we see in season 1? My hypothesis is that Gilda was a bad influence on her. During that time that they were close friends in school, Dash became someone who cared more about glory and accomplishment than about the well-being of others. If her sense of loyalty came into play at all, it was for the wrong person.

I don't know what happened that would make them not stay together. Possibly when RD dropped out of flight school. Anyway, continuing on the hypothesis, this would be the time when Dash becomes a little less insensitive. Without Gilda's influence, Dash starts to shift ever so slightly back to her old (or should I say young?) persona.
