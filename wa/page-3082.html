So, I was thinking about the ways Applejack and Rainbow Dash acted in this episode, and I don't think they were running contrary to their elements.

Appljack's entire problem here was that she felt that she let everyone down. She gave her word to come back with the money and prizes and all that, and when she didn't do as well as she'd promised, she felt that she couldn't come back.
