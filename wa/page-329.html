I've only really written two chapters of content, and both of those have the characters in rather atypical situations. So I'm probably not the best person to ask. My only advice is to keep in mind that Twilight is the most "academic" of the ponies, so sometimes her dialogue sounds a bit more stilted then everyone elses. Rainbow Dash is just a cause of capturing her constant sense of overconfidence. She's very brash and speaks her mind about everything.

Other than that, I can't really give you any more pointers. I haven't really written a whole lot of MLP fics, so I haven't really had time to develop my style for these characters. I could use some pointers myself, especially when it comes to writing Fluttershy. I'm not the best at writing soft-spoken characters.
