Whenever Pegasi pull things, such as chariots, they don't sag. They remain in the same plane as the Pegasus pulling it. This occurs even when the object being pulled isn't in motion (see Fluttershy's cart in Feeling Pinkie Keen). Physically this shouldn't happen, but if Pegasi have passive gravity manipulation, that could explain it.

Also, if the tank was falling very slowly, such as like a feather, then there'd only be a small amount of net force pushing it down ergo, a pegasus would need to impart a minuscule amount of force to keep it afloat.
