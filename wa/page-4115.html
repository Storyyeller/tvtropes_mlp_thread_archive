#102862 Japanese Teeth Mon, 20th Feb '12 6:20:16 PM from Meinong's jungle
I think the main issue with Celestia is that her role in the show basically requires her to be more or less infallible most of the time. She basically has to be the Reasonable Authority Figure, and introducing too many flaws could throw off the cast dynamic. I'd love to see an episode that gets inside Celestia's head, but the writers are rather limited in how far they can go. It's why I think something like a "Celestia goes on vacation" episode would be a good idea; it allows a sort of conflict to take place (if ponies don't recognize that she's the Princess) without removing an important aspect of her character.

A nice way of doing it would be to have Celestia try to resist that aspect of her character, she goes on vacation so she doesn't have to act as a peacemaker or whatnot, and finds it difficult not to get involved.
