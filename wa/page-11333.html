Who's your top 7 list of Meanest Ponies In The Show? My list goes like this:

  1. Diamond Dazzle Tiara
  2. Bon Bon
  3. Shoeshine
  4. Prince Blueblood
  5. The Valley Girl Ponies
  6. Photo Finish
  7. Jet Set and Upper Crust

DDT has started off as an Alpha Bitch, and she has only gotten worse. Bon Bon acted all pretentious to Rarity, and she considered telling a joke more important than pulling a garbage cart and letting Fluttershy cross the bridge. Shoeshine yelled at Spike to move on, and spitefully cut in front of Fluttershy, acted all "Maybe" when Fluttershy called her on it. Prince Blueblood is a disgrace to the Canterlot royal family, and it's a wonder that Princess Celestia keeps him around. The Valley Girl Ponies cut in front of Fluttershy, rudely told her to get out of their space, and they - worst of all - pulled a Karma Houdini. Photo Finish chose Fluttershy, displayed no concern for Fluttershy's feelings, and dismissed Rarity. Jet Set and Upper Crust are total snobs and Yes Man, and it was satisfying seeing them fall victim to their lack of independent thought.

I understand that the message of the show is to Love And Tolerate (why have those three words never been said in the show?) But those 7 ponies...I want to give them a Rising Pony Punch out of Equestria!
