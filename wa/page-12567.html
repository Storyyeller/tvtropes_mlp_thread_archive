#314156 Psi 001 Sun, 9th Dec '12 3:14:47 AM
Mc Carthy admitedly made my favorite episode, otherwise Morrow and Rogers tend to make a lot of my top episodes.

I think it's a common problem getting the balance with main characters. They either lack enough flaws and suffer Designated Protagonist Syndrome or they have them exacerbated and spend every episode making an ass of things (Sonic the Hedgehog is a particular character who often suffers from both problems depending on enterpretation). When it comes to developing supporting characters, it's often having the problem of the main character being the only flawed character, or the only one who isn't.

This is why Ensemble Cast shows tend to work better, since they take the time to develop a load of other characters equally and moderate their roles suitably. Twilight is a well balanced character that gets rather vivid moments of competence and flaws, albeit partly due to the fact she is a very marginal "lead" character (in which case she still kinda suffers Designated Protagonist Syndrome but for different more forgiveable reasons).
