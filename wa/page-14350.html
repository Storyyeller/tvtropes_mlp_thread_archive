#358746 kegisak Fri, 19th Apr '13 10:04:47 AM
I doubt he's 'redeemed'. Just like I doubt he was ever truly evil in the first place - he's Chaotic Neutral more than anything, really. He did what he did because he found pleasure in seeing the chaos he wrought, and he stopped doing it because he took more pleasure in being able to have a personal acquaintance in it. Notice how in Return of Harmony he immediately latched onto Twilight as his audience, and seemed buddy-buddy enough with Celestia that it's fair to assume he may have done the same with her in the past. He forced them to be his audience, so he couldn't really gratify himself from it, but he still needed that audience to some greater or lesser degree, if only to have the eventual pleasure of breaking them - in a way you could say their reaction was his end game. He got pretty bored-looking when Twilight stopped playing along. I imagine that Fluttershy simply happened to be an easier, perhaps more potent form of that gratification.
