For the record, I personally don't think Dash was that bad in MTBPW; I was just using it as an example of how a lot people got a negative impression of Dash from it.

As for MMDW, I think the basic idea (Dash's ego goes out of control, and the rest of the cast tries to show her how annoying it is) is good, but it wasn't executed well. Dash's popularity goes to her head way too fast, and the rest of the cast is too quick to go with the extreme plan. If they had made some other effort at talking her down first ("Dash, you're going to hurt somepony if you keep showboating like this"), the whole "superhero" plan would have been more justified. As is, they just jumped right to the most extreme method.

And that's all I'm going to say about that, because discussions about this never end well.

I think the self-aggrandizement is also part of it. There's a difference between "I'm good at this" and "Don't you think I'm good at this?" RD's issue isn't that she's claiming to be awesome, she's letting her attempts to get other ponies to tell her that she's awesome get in the way of her ability to actually be awesome.
