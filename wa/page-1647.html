1.) Favourite Pony?

It's hard for me to choose but if I had to, it would be a toss-up between Twilight and Pinkie. However, I really like the others, too.

2.) Favourite Episode?

Once again, it's hard for me to choose, but despite Rarity not usually being a favorite of mine, Suited for Success and Sisterhooves Social are probably my favorites right now. I also really like the moral in A Dog and Pony Show because of the way it pointed out that being classically feminine doesn't mean someone is an automatic Distressed Damsel who can't do anything for herself. It's just a good Take That to Real Women Never Wear Dresses and the general idea that being feminine is a sign of weakness or inferiority. I also generally like the Pity the Kidnapper kind of plot, too. It's because of this episode that I'd hoped to find a version of Cupcakes where Rarity is the victim and she gets free by annoying Pinkie with her whining but I was disappointed that the only version I found was one where she was just mutilated like the others and didn't even bother using her whining as a weapon.

3.) What do you think of Pinkie?

I like her a lot as a character because of how funny she is and can identify with the way she's rather literal at times (ex. telling Twilight she should just read her books rather than "hitting" them) and how she's the type to think of things outside of the norm. However, I can see how she can be annoying to some viewers.

4.) Favourite Background Pony?

Probably Derpy/Ditzy, at least I like spotting her in scenes and her little gags (like draining the water). I find her to be rather fascinating at least for the way she has quite the fandom following and various interpretations.

5.) Would you hug the Great And Powerful Trixie?

No because arrogant people/ponies like her annoy me. I'll let Snips and Snails hug her, they'd probably try if she'd let them.
