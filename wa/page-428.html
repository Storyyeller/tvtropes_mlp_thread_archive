Slightly off-topic, both from the current discussion and from the fandom, but here's how I see the different levels of fanon:

  • Canon: Anything explicitly stated in an episode, or stated by a creator to be canon. Inarguable fact. Example: Applejack is Applebloom's older sister.
  • Canonverse fanon: Speculation on the canonverse, using evidence from canon. Disputes should be decided by strength of evidence. Example: Numerical ages for the mane cast.
  • Wouldn't It Be Cool fanon: Essentially WMGs. Unsupported by canon, but tends to build on widely-accepted canonverse fanon. Examples: Tyrant Celestia, most of Luna's personality... and that Applejack's parents were killed by Bloomberg.
  • Fanfiction canon: Absolute truth for a given fanfiction that is explicitly stated to differ from canon.

Et cetera, et cetera. Sorry to get so preachy; just felt we should be more clear when discussing fanon whether we mean WMG fanon or canonverse fanon.
