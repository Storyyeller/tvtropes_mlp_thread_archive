Getting back to the question of "what would be good starter episodes?" Personally I'm gonna start my friends off with:

Swarm of the Century - For reasons stated earlier.
A Dog and Pony Show - Some might argue that you need to know Rarity, but I think the basic idea of "horse annoys her captors" is basic enough to understand.
