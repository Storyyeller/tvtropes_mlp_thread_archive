Good morning, Equestria. I have another new hypothesis.

When she was a little filly (even littler than y'all, CMC), she ran away to Manehattan to find her special talent. At another point in her life, she ran away to Dodge Junction because she couldn't win any prize money at a competition, one that she particularly considers herself to be really frickin' good at.

Could it be that Applejack has a history and/or a tendency to (literally) run away from her problems instead of facing them head-on?
