Season 4 will have a full set of episodes? That's good news! That leaves room for a variety of characters to get focus episodes and also for episodes focusing on pairs or ensembles as well. I personally would like to see some stories that combine characters that haven't really interacted much yet, just to give some variety in character interactions. For example, Fluttershy is usually paired with Rainbow Dash, and that's understandable because they're both pegasi and they knew each other when they were fillies. But it might be interesting to see Fluttershy get an episode where she is paired with someone like Applejack or Pinkie Pie. She and Pinkie would be quite a pair of opposites, since, when it comes to interacting with other ponies, they are at opposite extremes of the extrovert/introvert spectrum.

Concerning Pinkie and Fluttershy, does anyone else think that they both have similar problems with and reactions to being rejected by those they are desperate to befriend? Pinkie chases her friends all over in Party Of One when she is afraid that they are rejecting her, and the same applies to when she chases Cranky Doodle Donkey in A Friend In Deed. But in The Best Night Ever, Fluttershy acts in a similar obsessive manner towards the animals in the Canterlot gardens.
