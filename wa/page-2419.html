#60470 Fell Deeds Awake Mon, 26th Dec '11 12:43:23 PM from Augsburg, Germany
One last post before I go to sleep:

The "Cutie Mark is of you doing something So Bad, It's Good" thing does play a part in the last Fan Fic I read (and which I recommend): "The Worst Baker in Equestria". The premise is that Bad Baking Magic is one of the magic forces flowing round Equestria (like the Earth Ponies' growing stuff) and allows/forces you to spectacularly and entertainingly fail at baking. Sweet Soufflé, a cool old lady, has a broken measuring cup (I think) as her CM, which signifies that she's pretty much the master of Bad Baking, which she uses for entertainment purposes. She then expands the concept into a huge yearly competition, which she greatly enjoys hosting. So you could absolutely have a happy life with a So Bad, It's Good CM .

(If you like really nonsensical stories which nevertheless still take the characters seriously and have heart, give it a try . It has Trixie fighting a giant dough monster and a Phantom of the Opera-esque musical number!)
