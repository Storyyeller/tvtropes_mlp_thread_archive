#61743 Japanese Teeth Thu, 29th Dec '11 1:29:44 PM from Meinong's jungle
@Super Gex: Welcome to the herd. Fair warning: this thread moves uber fast. I'm sure there's some sort of fanon take on that pony someplace, but I can't remember it off the top of my head.

Anyway, about Trixie and Luna. I think there's a difference in that Trixie has an established personality, while Luna was basically a blank slate. I have no doubt that it might turn out okay if Trixie returns, but I'm not too sure. With Luna, I think the fandom was subconsciously prepared; we all knew that she had no canon personality, and that with her being Celestia's sister there was a good chance that she'd pop up again. With Trixie, the fanon has more basis, and thus there could be a greater uproar if it gets disproven. The other thing is that Trixie is pretty obviously written to be a one-shot character, so if she does show up again, it's almost definitely going to be in response to her popularity with the fans, which also means a greater outcry if she isn't characterized to fan specifications. Don't get me wrong, I think it could work, I just think that if the fandom can get butthurt over stuff like the Heartstrings toy not being named "Lyra", it isn't a stretch to think that they'd complain about something like this. Hopefully they won't, though. By an large they've been pretty good about stuff like that.

Either that or it's a flower garden. Maybe non-EP's can grow ornamental stuff. Or they can grow plants, just not nearly as well.
