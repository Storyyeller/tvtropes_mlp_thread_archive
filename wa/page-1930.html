I'm not sure if this would go better in the "pre-FIM" thread, but I was wondering, what would happen if the current ponies met the old ones? I know there was at least one fanfic like that, but I just thought it might be an interesting discussion question. I know that there are some fanarts of the characters meeting their older counterparts, usually having the new ones insult the old ones (which I personally disagree with since I actually like the older ponies, even if Fi M is my favorite pony series, it doesn't mean I can't also enjoy the older series). Some ideas I had:

  • Windwhistler and Twilight would get along VERY well.
  • Rarity would get along very well with not only the G3 Rainbow Dash, but also with Heart Throb, who even has a somewhat similar accent.
  • If they did meet the ones that have the same names, it could get pretty confusing unless there were codenames used like in A Pony and A Portal (the fanfic I mentioned above).
  • Pinkie Pie and Cupcake (from G1) might get along well because they both love baking, but given what I've noticed of Cupcake's personality she seems like she'd be easily weirded out by Pinkie's wackiness.

I have never seen the "My Little Pony Tales" series and don't know much about it outside what I've read on this wiki, so someone who has seen that series might be able to give a better opinion on how those ponies would interact with the ones from Friendship is Magic.
