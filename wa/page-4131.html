This looks like a job for OVER-ANALYSIS!!!

Ponies are herd creatures, and so social trends, such as celebrities, tend to be an all-or-nothing deal. The mane cast probably got plenty of attention immediately after their two instances of saving the world, but with nothing new happening, the crowd loses interest and moves on to the next big thing. You can see this happen in the span of one episode in MMDW, where the whole town switches its attention from Dash to Mare-Do-Well at the drop of a hat. This is different from human pop culture because while our trends shift, there will always be lingering fans stalking celebrities regardless of how relevant they are at the time. Famous ponies like Photo Finish or Sapphire Shores have to keep producing new material to stay in the pubic eye.
