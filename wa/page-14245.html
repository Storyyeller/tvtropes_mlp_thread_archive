#356122 Sereg Thu, 11th Apr '13 2:13:16 PM from South Africa
The Immortal Game: Before the reign of Discord, the world was ruled by the alicorns Titan and Terra. Their rule was as horrible as Discord's would become and they were sealed in an act of trickery by their daughters, Celestia and Luna as, considering the fact that an alicorn's power is determined by their age, they would never have stood a chance in a straight fight without the corrupting power of the S Liver of Darkness (which would later create Nightmare Moon). Now, The seal has been broken, Titan and Terra are back and want to rule once again, . Their pawn has also driven the Sliver of Darkness into Twilight. Celestia knows that she has no chance of escaping her parents alive, so she sets up Luna's escape as her first move in the plan to overthrow her parents once again.

No, none of the Mane six get Killed Off for Real, but it is a very dark story and some very horrible stuff happens to some of them.
