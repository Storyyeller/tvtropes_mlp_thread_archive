Horse-kind - three basic types (keeping with the theme of three that exists in MLP: Draft Horses, Thoroughbreds, and Hanoverians. (although I might change the names.)

Note - hands and "Apples" are used interchangeably, given that apples average 4 inches in diameter.

Drafts are mild-tempered gentle giants and well-suited for working and pulling. They're analogous to Earth Ponies. they range from 16 to 20 hands tall (at the withers).

Thoroughbreds are agressive, spirited, bold, fast and agile. They're also the lightest of the three. Analogous to Pegasi. They average 14-16 hands tall (at the withers).

Hanoverians are a mix of the above. They're militant, intelligent, curious, graceful, athletic and of a more even-temperment that the Thoroughbreds, but with greater agility than a Draft. A jack-of-all-trades Horse. Most average 16 hands tall (at the withers). Hanoverians almost invariably are found in leadership roles over the other horse-kind, making them roughly analogous to Unicorns.
