@The discussion of healing spells: Well, I like to take a combination of the three. To clarify, regeneration-enhancing-spells, instant-healing spells, and vitality-sacrificial spells (reverse-necromancy?). However, the costs are quite different, and that's not factoring in that I don't consider either of the three kinds of spells to have built-in anesthesia - that's a separate spell. So, the quicker the healing (and the less experienced the pony is to injury), the more the pain he or she will be in.

In my opinion/(self-canon), regeneration spells cost less than vitality sacrificing spells, which cost less than instant-heal spells. And of course, the regeneration spells are the least painful (need less painkillers), and the instant-heal spells are the most painful (a huge burst of pain - need a lot more painkillers and such). However, each requires a different discipline of magic to master. They have relations yes but, several of the techniques are different.
