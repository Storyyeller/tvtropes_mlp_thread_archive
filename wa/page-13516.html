#337882 Bluespade Sun, 10th Feb '13 1:11:06 PM from Fort Worth, Texas
I finally got to see Games Ponies Play.

I really liked it, one of the top episodes of the season at least. I really like that they're setting things up for future episodes. The Equestrian Games have to take place in Season 4, I bet they originally planned for it to be in this season but when they shortened it to 13 episodes they changed it up. Actually, this episode really felt to me like part 1 of a 2 part finale, with the focus on the entire cast, the higher than usual production values, the Big Event set up, and the guest star characters (Shiny was great).

I didn't like the green haired ponies accent or manner of speaking, I find that sort of bad fake southern affectation really grating. But the character herself I found interesting. I didn't expect claustrophobia to show up in this show. I assumed with the way they she kept going on about wanting to run and stretch her legs that they were leading up to the reveal that she was actually an athlete competing in the games, but that didn't happen. I wonder if it was initially planned but they decided the plot was complicated enough.

Best part: Twilight's legitimate character development. She was put into a position where she was in charge of a job that went bad... and she didn't go crazy. She even realized that this was unusual herself when she was talking to her brother, and it was really good to see her practicing the relaxation technique Cadance taught her throughout the episode. It leads very well into the season finale. It would be a bad idea to have a princess who can't cope with stress, and she's now shown that she's gotten good at handling it.

BTW Do we learned new town/city name in this episode? Mustangia?

I thought it was a province/country but maybe it's a town.
