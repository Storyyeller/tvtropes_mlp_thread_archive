#157262 Drenius Sun, 15th Apr '12 8:10:12 AM from Northern Virginia
crow, I seem to have remembered incorrectly, her being completely resentful of the people of Appleloosa was more due to a rift with her family who had been responsible for moving her to Appleloosa in the first place. After looking, I found some of my notes on her.

Part of what lead to her decision to disassociate herself from the rest of her family was the fact that they didn't respect what she felt that she was called to do and kept trying to push her into work they thought was more appropriate for a young lady of her age. Perhaps they were merely grooming her for marriage, which she had no interest for at the time. Most of all, they didn't care for her tendency to carouse with the "roughs" around town. All of these combined could have led to a fracas, after which Goldy felt the best choice was to simply leave. Obviously, time has brought her to question her decision on occasion, but part of her pride won't let her return until she's proved her point as it were.

She's not really mean-spirited, but a bit rough and living out on frontier have made other ponies look at her a bit cock-eyed. Prolonged periods of isolation and rumours that have spread around the town have made Gold Dust a bit inapproachable by the average pony and the tendency of others to whisper in hushed tones when she's around sets her a bit on edge.
