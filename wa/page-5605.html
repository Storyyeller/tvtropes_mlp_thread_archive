#140125 Seraphem Thu, 29th Mar '12 5:31:18 PM from Delamare
no it's not Grimdark. Grimdark is about taking hope and burying it, crushing it beneath tons of doubt, despair, cruelty, apathy, and death. Grimdark is about reveling in darkness, making it clear that no matter what, everyone is screwed, best you can do is stave it off a bit longer.

FO:E is the exact opposite, it is about hope, about how no matter how deep the darkness may be, there is always the chance that it could be better, and that no matter how long a shot it seems, it's worth fighting for.
Build a fool proof system, and the world will create a better fool.
