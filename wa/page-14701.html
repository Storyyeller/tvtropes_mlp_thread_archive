Dear Thread Manor...

It's been so long since I've written! I'm so sorry! But for a while, I just kind of stopped reading pony fics, and no new fics means no new fanfic reports...

You may be wondering why it is I stopped reading, and the reason is, I started writing instead! I created a whole ton of headcannon on changelings, and now have a fanfic of my own! You should all totally check out A Novel Tale! Follow Twilight as she learns all about how changelings live and operate!

But enough about me. I wouldn't have made this if I didn't have some new fics to report on! Today, I've got a crossover, a pony on earth fic, and two slice of life stories!

Mass Effect: Continuum -in progress. Shepard and Anderson thought themselves dead when the Crucible activated. Imagine their surprise then, when they wake up to find themselves on a strangely Earth like world, with Anderson still himself but Shepard as some kind of equine creature. Did the Crucible work? How did they end up here? How was Shepard changed? Cut off from all support and with only each other, this is going to be one of the hardest missions they've had to face yet...

This is notable for a few reasons. First, it's femshep. second, this story has a slightly renegade PTSD Shepard, which makes for some amusing situations. and it's nice to see some Shepard/Anderson interaction as well, even if one is still a human and the other is a pony.

A Twilight Landing -in progress. Jo is up late one night, watching a particularly violent storm. She is taken of guard, however, when the storm starts displaying some very strange behavior, culminating in a massive shock wave on par with a small earthquake that leaves her house is disarray. Upon investigating the damage done, she finds a mysterious, naked woman with blue and purple hair who clearly isn't in her right mind. Jo has always been something of a thrill seeker, but this takes the cake on strange and new experiences...

I like this because a) it's a humanized pony on earth, something you really don't see enough of, and b) Twilight has some major PTSD over the whole suddenly being a different species thing. It's still early on, but I am eager to see where this story goes.

Good Thing I'm So Organized. Twilight has always had some slight OCD tendencies, but ever since the changeling attack on Canterlot, she's gone into full blown organizing mode, going so far as to invade Rarity's shop to clean up her creativity inducing clutter. What is it that could have possibly set her off like this? Just why is Twilight such an organized pony?

A little one shot of 5k words, there's not much reason not to give it a try. and there's an epic pillowfight, too.

The Numbers Don't Lie. The CMC, while trying to get their gold finding cutie marks, uncover an ancient magical relic and accidentally activate it. Now everypony in Ponyville is stuck with a magic counter over their heads that ticks up every time they tell a lie. Only Celestia can fix it, but she's stuck in Baltimare for a few days. Surely Ponyville can survive that long with everypony's falsehoods brought to light, right?

It was an enjoyable story, though honestly, the moral kind of lost me towards the end.

Well, that's all for today. I'm still no reading nowhere near as much as I used to be, so i can't say when I might get around to doing another fanfic report, but I have in fact started reading again!

Your Faithful Fanfic Reader
