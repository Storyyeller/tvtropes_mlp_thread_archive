#355777 Psyga 315 Wed, 10th Apr '13 2:32:07 PM
An Accusation Fic is when a fanfic, or events in the fanfic, call out someone for their actions. It became popular to the fandom after Season 2 since its finale is ripe with ponies to call out. Basically, it's What the Hell, Hero? in fanfic format.

Unfortunately, the direction the fandom took it in made the Accusation Fic genre more of a "SHE WAS RIGHT, THEY WERE WRONG" kind of story. The first Accusation Fic about the Wedding, Post Nuptials, took no sides and called out everyone, victim included. Faith And Doubt did it a bit differently, adding drastic consequences to the mix. Families, the sequel to Nuptials, began to focus on some other consequences the invasion brought on.

Bitterness and This Day Should Have Been Just Perfect are just the same. Twilight decides that she's no longer upset with herself but rather pissed off with her friends, has a shitfit, then Rage Quits.
