#49256 Mio Sun, 4th Dec '11 9:53:44 AM
The mane cast was acting fairly strange. More uncouth than usual if you ask me. I don't remeber AJ doing any gardening at the Gala. It's like Machiavelli's Prince, twisting and slightly misrepresenting the characters' actions for the sake of argument, telling a story differently for the sake of an Aesop. Something there has been too much of in this season, as of now. The first season served to define characters and fill in the blanks left by the opening. This should constrain this season to only work on the blanks that are left, or build upon what was drawn, not twist and bleach it for the sake of one-off jokes and lessons. Twilight dancing horribly was very heartwarming though. She's really outgrown herself

You should expect some variation between character depictions, if only because different writers are bound to have different ideas of how certain characters work.

As far as characters being OOC for the sake of jokes/lessons this ranks pretty low on the list. I can honestly see the Mane six being a little thoughtless about going to a more formal party, especially if they just came from an incredibly informal party. Also this is a comedic cartoon, you should expect some exaggeration when ever a joke or any kind of humor is involved (though I admit that even that has its limits).

And yes Twilight dancing is adorable.
