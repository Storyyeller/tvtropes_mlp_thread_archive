#288259 Bluespade Thu, 27th Sep '12 3:41:21 PM from Fort Worth, Texas
Applelight: Hey, serious question,

what if I went for a very simplified look for this comic, rather than this current detailed style?

I suggest a simplified look. You'll draw faster, more often, and as you draw you will naturally improve, eventually evolving into a more detailed style. One of the absolute worst things fledgling artists can do is try to use to much detail. Every additional line you lay down on a drawing increases the chance that it will look wrong. Since you're just starting out, I think you should keep it very simple.

And simplified styles can still look really good.
