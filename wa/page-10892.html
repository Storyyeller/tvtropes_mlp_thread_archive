Well, you guys may already have some familiarity with my crew from my ATG submissions, but here they are again.◊

  • The University of Manehattan Department of Mythozoology
  • Top left: Professor Stachensturtz has spent his entire life in academia. He's never even had a job that wasn't for the school, and when he was finally made head of the Mythozoology department after Professor Buckbeaker retired last year he went just a little mad with power. Most of the department left after that. His cutie mark is a graduation cap, signifying his dedication to academia and his focus on the end result. His coat is pale yellow and his mane is pale blue.
  • Top right: Most ponies are shocked to learn that Wild Shot's first job was as a foal fashion model. Wild comes from a well-to-do family of Canterlot unicorns with connections to the fashion industry who expected him to grow up into the business. Instead, Wild became fascinated with the cameras and grew up to be a professional wildlife photographer with an adrenaline-seeking streak. He met his Heterosexual Life Partner, Brownstone, in a class on animal behavioral studies at UM while working on his photography degree and the two clicked instantly. His cutie mark is a rocket-propelled camera. His coat is brown, his mane is orange, and he often wears a red vest with lots of pockets for spare camera equipment.
  • Bottom left: Songbreeze is a chemical biologist with a tendency to skip straight from first thought to experimentation without waiting for her brain to analyze, let alone get her colleagues' opinions (not that it would matter in most cases; Wild thinks she's a genius and the Professor just says she's got moxie). Songbreeze got into chemical biology thanks to a childhood belief that butterflies were made from rainbows. Her cutie mark is a microscope. Her coat is sky blue with dappled black on her rump, and her mane is two-toned beige and pink.
  • Bottom right: Brownstone is a grad student, Professor Stachensturtz's chief research assistant, and commonly regarded by the other departments as the Only Sane Pony in Mythozoology. He is also commonly the bait when the team needs to study predator behavior, thanks a childhood spent in the foal scouts and having gone through his first few years of school on a track scholarship. Though prone to anxiety, Brownstone is more-or-less the reason the team actually gets anything done on a daily basis. His cutie mark is a magnifying glass with crosshairs, signifying his hunt for knowledge. His coat is two-toned light and dark grey, his mane is dark brown, and he almost always wears a pair of orange sunglasses.
