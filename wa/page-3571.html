@L Mage: Well I don't really recall it all that well, but there would be Prestige Points to show how well you were respected in your local society. You'd spend a lot of time finding out secret information which you would then be able to apply to put pressure on others, getting them to do what you wanted. I was planning on a sort of conversation system much like rpg combat systems, where all of the participants got a turn every round, and you could use all sorts of different ways of influencing others: charm, intimidation, bluffing, etc. There would be methods of planting subtle meaning into apparently ordinary conversation, allowing you to convey threats or promises to perceptive people while stating nothing you could possibly be incriminated for. There'd be different reputations towards different social orders, reputation based on your actions and reputation based on your personality, all sorts of complex stuff.
