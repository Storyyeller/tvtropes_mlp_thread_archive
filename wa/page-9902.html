#247540 Kuroi Tsubasa Tenshi Thu, 12th Jul '12 8:38:26 AM from Canada
Re: Discord fight: If you jump at him such that you'd land right in front of him, making sure you get as much hang time as possible, the fireballs are likely to miss (I think the odd angle makes him think I'm still directly in front of him because that's where the fireballs tend to go). Using this technique I only lost 1 life (it's a bit harder to get away after rushing him like that, but it's worth getting the hits in, especially with the beam spam characters who can get 5-6 hits) and that was with him only having half a heart left. Fluttershy I took him down without losing any lives (although I only had a quarter of a heart left). Dashie was a little rougher, but not by much. AJ was a battle of attrition for me, though.

And for anyone other than Dashie, Gilda is so easy to camp. Ranged can just sit on the initial cloud and beam spam whenever she approaches. Same thing for AJ and Flutters, except Gilda needs to get into melee range (which she will) and should end in her getting utterly clobbered. Dashie is stuck jousting her, though and Gilda has priority if they both use their dash at the same time.
