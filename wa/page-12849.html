Concerning, potential episodes, I think I'd like plots concerning RD and AJ's elements of Harmony. They seem the least developed of the cast. We've seen hints of how they are connected but not how they far they really define the character.

I kinda like the idea of AJ fearing hurting someone with Brutal Honesty and trying (probably badly) to use 'little white lies' giving an Aesop about moderating the truth so it isn't too harsh while still being honest.

As for RD, hard to think of a idea concerning her loyalty. A Taking Advantage of Generosity episode may be interesting to see for her endurance wise, though it would hard to see RD of all people being submissive to someone.

As for AJ being hard to develop, I think the main problem is one of her key flaws is now dissappeared. In early episodes she was rather temperamental, which often lead to her being overdefensive when she felt someone was being unjust or insulting her. This was also arguably a key drive in her stubborness (nearly ALL the cast have stubborn moments, just caused by their key flaw, if anything AJ's pig headedness is the least developed now since it isn't working off any other personality trait). What may have caused her lack of versatility is that rather than developing and maturing this flaw like with the other cast she just sort of 'grew out of it' and it was no longer an issue anymore and, as Japanese Teeth's review pointed out, she can't really take plots to high level due to her now very mellowed personality.
