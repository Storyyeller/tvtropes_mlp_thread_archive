#108388 kegisak Sun, 26th Feb '12 3:38:12 PM
@Mage: See, everyone says that, but season 4 was always my favourite.

I never tended to like any mon series that much, because I have a very internalized definition of strength. That is, it's all well and good to call the kids of digimon strong, which they were, but they still never did any fighting. I was so attached to the kids and I hated seeing them useless next to the super-powered monsters. As a kid I didn't really make the connection that it was the kids inner strength that fueled the mons outer strength - I sort of missed the metaphor on that one. Then comes season 4, where the kids are actually doing something on their own, and I loved it. Of course it has serious pacing issues, but I forgave those as a kid.

In essence my issue was that if they were going to save the world, they should save the world, not get their pet monster to do it for them.
