Sunsets. Poetry. Movies. Video games. But I'm talking about knowing that somewhere out there, right now, there is a team of people so passionate about making a good product that they can produce something like this. 22 minutes that can completely sap away any foul mood, any sad or angry thoughts. All because a team of people dedicated themselves to making. us. happy. I don't care how much bad there is in the world. That, right there? That more than makes up for it. Yeah, you know what? Everything is amazing. If you don't think so, you're not looking hard enough. I want you to walk outside, and find a flower. Look at it, real hard. Study the colours. Smell it.
