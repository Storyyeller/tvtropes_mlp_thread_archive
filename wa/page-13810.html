Speaking of fanfics, are their any longish ones of good quality I can read? Having read Fo E, I guess I can take just about anything at this point. My internet over here isn't very great so it'll be easier downloading chapters I can read between Wi Fi crashes. So if anypony could, that would be great.

  • Eternal (The first few chapters are rather misleading—they give the impression that the story is going to angst about mortality. Then, Twilight and Celestia both freak the hell out, and the real meat of the story involves a Journey To The Center Of The Mind to find out why this happened.)
  • Earth And Sky (A few years in the future, Twilight, Rarity, and Apple Bloom invent mechanical wings. Shenanigans and a huge pegasus race ensue. It’s a sequel to a shorter fic, Windfall, so you may want to read that one first.)
  • The Final Quest of Star Swirl the Bearded (An earth pony rock farmer, a unicorn wizard, and a troupe of pegasus soldiers set off to reclaim a mountain from a dragon. It’s The Hobbit set in ancient Equestria, and it works.)
  • The Flight Of The Alicorn (Rarity participates in an airship race, which quickly gets derailed by political conspiracies and an ancient superweapon. Rarity becomes an action heroine, and Prince Blueblood becomes tolerable.)
  • Mendacity (Possibly the best “Bon Bon is a changeling” fic yet.)
  • Project Sunflower (Possibly the best “human in Equestria” fic yet.)
  • The Best Night Ever (FIM meets Groundhog Day. Prince Blueblood gets stuck in a time loop until he learns to not be a jerk.)
  • Hard Reset (FIM meets Groundhog Day meets Die Hard. Twilight Sparkle gets stuck in a time loop until she figures out how to stop dying and save Canterlot from a changeling invasion.)
  • Infernal Machines and Contraptionology! (It starts with Mad Science coming to Ponyville, and the craziness just escalates.)

Also, I’ll second the rec for Its A Dangerous Business Going Out Your Door.
