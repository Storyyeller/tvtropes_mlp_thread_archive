So, question for anyone who's actually familiar with the franchise, as opposed to those of us (me) who just hopped on the bandwagon - how does this compare, in quality, to the other earlier series'? Because I'll never live it down if I like this and it turns out that consensus is it's not as good as the original, because there is no way I'm ever watching the original.

Original: Pretty good...by the standards of 1980s cartoons, which weren't exactly high. Science-fiction had Transformers, fantasy had My Little Pony. The show was more epic than FIM (lots of villains, ranging from silly ones like the Diamond Dogs to epic ones like Night Mare Moon), and the world was less civilized and more dangerous, but the characterization was fairly shallow.

My Little Pony Tales: Ditched the fantasy elements, and instead had anthropomorphic ponies going to school in an equivalent to modern suburbia. Not awful, from what little I've seen, but altogether forgettable.

G3: An atrocity committed against the franchise. Vapid, stupid, and insulting to the intelligence of anyone above the age of three months. Decent theme song, though.
