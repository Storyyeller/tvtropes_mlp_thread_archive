#268042 Japanese Teeth Sat, 11th Aug '12 8:28:29 PM from Meinong's jungle
Okay, here's the list. I divided the S1 episodes into 5 groups. Note that these are all based solely on my personal take on them and aren't objective in any way. I guarantee people are going to wonder WTF I was thinking for some of these.

  • Epic: Cream of the crop episodes; ones that I don't really have any substantial complaints against.
    • A Dog and Pony Show
    • Green Isn't Your Color
    • Party of One
  • Near-Epic: Solid episodes that don't quite hit epic tier, but are still very good.
    • Applebuck Season
    • Dragonshy
    • Look Before You Sleep
    • Bridle Gossip
    • Fall Weather Friends
    • Suited for Success
    • A Bird in the Hoof
  • Good: Episodes that are above average, but have evident flaws.
    • Friendship is Magic, parts 1 and 2.
    • Griffon the Brush Off
    • Boast Busters
    • Swarm of the Century
    • Feeling Pinkie Keen
    • Sonic Rainboom
    • Stare Master
  • Average: Decent, but not particularly outstanding or memorable.
    • The Ticket Master
    • Winter Wrap Up
    • Call of the Cutie
    • The Show Stoppers
    • Cutie Mark Chronicles
    • Owl's Well that Ends Well
    • The Best Night Ever
  • Subpar: Should have gone through another round of editing before airing.
    • Over a Barrel
