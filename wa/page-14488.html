#362185 kegisak Tue, 30th Apr '13 8:32:12 AM
I have to say, evidence is certainly stacked in favour of Scoots being unable to fly. We've seen infants fly with relative ease, and we know that Rainbow Dash, and therefore presumably Fluttershy and several other pegasi at Scoots' age were able to fly at various levels of proficiency(Cutie Mark Chronicles, if you're curious - Dash admits to getting her CM 'first in her class', meaning she'd likely be younger than Scoots. Depending on how flight camp is divided Fluttershy, Hoops and Dumbbell are probably around her age).

We've also seen that her lack of flight capabilities are a point of embarrassment for her, since she A) looked humiliated after her failed attempt to keep herself aloft in the Gabby Gums episode, and the incident was also used as blackmail against her in the same episode.

I don't think it's impossible she could fly in the future, but even if she could she'll likely be a very weak flyer. That said, her wings seem quite capable of providing horizontal thrust, so using them as a motor in various situation is very plausible. She just doesn't seem to be able to generate sufficient lift, for whatever reason.
