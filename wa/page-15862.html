@Ace, King: I just want to point out that once you get past college or so, numerical age as precisely nothing to do with maturity level. The fact that, say, Rarity acts more mature than Pinkie doesn't necessarily imply anything about their ages. Rarity could be a very mature 22 and Pinkie could be a very immature 25. Simply put, it doesn't matter who's the oldest because it doesn't really affect their personalities any.

Case in point, the Fluttershy/Pinkie gag: exactly how does it change their relationship that Fluttershy is older than Pinkie? It's not like either of them would act differently if their ages would be reversed. The only reason it was mentioned is because Pinkie was treating Fluttershy like a child, and Fluttershy was noting the irony. Outside of that specific circumstance, it really makes no difference.
