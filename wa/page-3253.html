I had... probably the most absurd dream ever last night. It was a pony related nightmare. And not in the Cupcakes sort of vein of pony nightmare. If anything it sort of smacked of Dark City.

I wasn't actually involved in it at all, it was basically revolving around Big Mac and Caramel, who were couple in this context(GEE, I WONDER WHAT MY SUBCONSCIOUS IS TRYING TO TELL ME?). There was this sort of subtle alien invasion going on, where the aliens would go around abducting lone ponies for research purposes. They would analyze how they reacted to various stimuli, and try to create empty pony bodies - they had some way of separating a consciousness from a body, and trying to build a new body around a consciousness. Except that the consciousness could reject the body... somehow. It had something to do with intense emotions, because a Caramel consciousness rejected a body because he was pissed that he got separated from BM. The rejected bodies were called Blank Checks, or Bad Checks, for some reason.

Anyways, they were trying to recreate pony bodies so that they could stick their own consciousnesses into them and become ponies, I guess. Their motivations were either for plain old study from a hands-on perspective, or that they were an immortal species that wanted to take on mortality so that they would be so bored. Both explanations were given at some point. Most of the studies focused around emotion - their motivations for abduction Mac and Caramel were to study love, for example - and stuff like that.

The aliens were actually sort of sympathetic. The nightmare part comes from the fact that A: In the vein of Dark City they were really fucking creepy and B: a part where, from Big Mac's perspective, I witnessed him waking up to three of the aliens standing over top of him (and they are tall) staring down, only to grab him and collect genetic material by Sticking a needle into his eye. So yeah. Kinda freaky as balls, that. But it was actually kinda cool. Might make for a good fanfiction.
