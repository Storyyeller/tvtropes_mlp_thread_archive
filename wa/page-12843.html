#321064 Morning Star 1337 Sun, 23rd Dec '12 3:25:34 PM
Let's see some of the Character Development for the mane 6 and how easy it is to make an plot or idea with them in mind.

  • Twilight Sparkle: no longer required to send letter every week, advance to a next level of magic. Seems pretty content with her life so far.
  • Rarity: gained a benefactor of sorts in the form of Fancy Pants. also seems happy so far but haven't became a big-name celebrity yet.
  • Fluttershy: Gotten some self-help with her assertiveness, However still acts meek in spite of that.
  • Pinkie Pie: It would be harder to make her sad the it would be to make her happy, Though she did gain some lessons on responsibility and not need to be everywhere at once (I'm sorry that I couldn't state the aesop form Too many Pinkie Pies better)
  • Rainbow Dash: slowly but surly becoming closer to achieving her dream of being a Wonderbolt, but like Rarity is not there yet.
  • Applejack: Arguably the hardest to write an episode for unless it involved her sister or her stubborn streak. She's usually more laid back otherwise.
  • Spike: Not sure about him yet but he seems to be happy. and there still a chance that the writers could squeeze a plot or two out of the fact that he's a dragon or his crush.

Now then with this in mind. Twilight, Rarity, and Rainbow Dash have the most obvious choices for plots ideas while the writers may have to be creative with the others at some point.
