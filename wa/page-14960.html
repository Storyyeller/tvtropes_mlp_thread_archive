#373994 Meta Four Sat, 8th Jun '13 6:28:34 PM from riding the wave
Anyone found any good music lately?
If you’re asking specifically about pony music, nah.

Otherwise, I blew some money at Best Buy and a used CD store earlier this week. The highlights of my haul:
  • Kishi Bashi: 151a. It sounds like Mr. Ishibashi compiled a checklist of Baroque Pop tropes and set out to include every single one of them in this album. But originality is overrated; even if 151a is a derivative album, it’s still a great listen.
  • King Crimson: Starless and Bible Black. Reminds me of Mahavishnu Orchestra’s The Inner Mounting Flame, just with the jazz/rock ration tipped a little more towards rock.
  • King Crimson: Thrak. It’s very 90s, in a way I can’t quite put my finger on.
  • UB40: Signing Off. Holy freaking crap, is this really the same band that recorded “Red Red Wine”? This is dark and bloody amazing.
  • Antonio Carlos Jobim: Wave. “Meta Four, is that bossa nova you’re listening to?” “I know, isn’t it so out of character for me?”
  • Isao Tomita: Snowflakes Are Dancing. Selections of Claude Debussy’s music, rearranged for Moog synthesizers. Lovely stuff.
