If you frequent Equestria Daily, you might've seen this game already. If not...

Basically... Someone made an 8-bit NES-style MLP game, starring Apple Bloom. Despite only using one button, it's surprisingly good, and faithfully replicates the limitations of the NES hardware right down to the palettes and music. It's short, but sweet.

And It Gets Worse. Just... Just play it. The effect is best if you're not spoiled. I kid you not. The FEAR games never came close to scaring me like this game did.

Oh, you think it's all sunshine and rainbows? Haha. Cupcakes ain't got shit on this.

Don't say I didn't warn ya. Sweet dreams.

It's amazing how scary it is despite being 8-bit, the visuals for the zombie ponies and the corpse remind me of the visual style of the Chzo Mythos games, especially Trilby's Notes - it's basically that, plus Silent Hill meets My Little Pony. It's minimalistic, but creepy as FUCK. Less is more, indeed.
