I played that one. It was when I first learned that ponies are pretty nasty when pushed. The author then went to add three cave pony races (which were kinda cool, btw), three evil pony races, and then he went overboard with the three race counterparts in other races. After he vanished without a trace, two of his fans decided to continue his legacy with mods of their own, with only one keeping the cave ponies but being kinda weird (tyranids all over the place) and the other one being only show canon, but pretty decent.

And that wasn't the only weird thing. Pegasi could wrestle with their wings, and would often disassemble their enemies by wing rather than use their weapons. And unicorns went stab happy with their steel-piercing horns with alarming frequency. And telekinesis is still kinda wonky. The former kept it like that, and the latter made telekinesis another organ in the horn. It feels weird to pinch the telekinesis off their horn using your own, leaving a severed telekinesis lying on the floor and one very unhappy unicorn.
