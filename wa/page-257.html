#6410 Violet Lashes Tue, 7th Jun '11 3:28:39 PM
I remember Rarity being my least favorite in the beginning of the season. She just seemed so "Wow, girly character that likes fashion" to me. The episode I started to like her a lot more in was "Look Before You Sleep". I really liked how she wasn't a snotty bitch or anything, she's just the OCD prim and proper type of character. Then she autimatically became my favorite pony in "Suited For Success", because she worked so damn hard just to satisfy her friends when they were being extremely picky and kind of inconsiderate for a moment. They weren't even paying her, and she worked days and nights to get them perfect for them, and they shot her down. It really displayed her generosity (her element of harmony, of course) that I had never seen so fiercely until this episode. And she's one of the only characters on TV these days that show that true beauty comes from the heart, and Rarity isn't just beautiful on the outside. She's generous and a workoholic that tries her best to make her dream come true by being a perfectionist, and that shows her inner beauty as well.

And I'm not a trendy girly girl at all, I just like wearing comfy nice clothing. And I'm usually not even into girly characters in TV shows, but the 3 girliest ponies of the mane cast are my top 3 faves. Go figure.

Also, Rarity's laugh makes me laugh my ass off every time. Also her funny facial expressions. And "How coooooooulllddd yooouuu!"
