@badwolfwho: Actually he can upgrade up to Magic +5 from the start, but you'll need blue titanite to do it. You'll find it around, also the crystal golems in the forest drop them but I'd suggest just playing through normally until you find them. Once you get to Magic +5 though, you need the Magic Ember to go higher, which is found deep in the Darkroot Forest. You can access the forest almost immediately, but the area the Ember is in is a pretty high leveled spot so I wouldn't suggest going in it right now unless you just try and run past everyone. There's really no need for you to get it yet anyway since you won't have enough blue titanite to upgrade that high.

You should definitely make sure to upgrade your weapons and armor when you can though, doing so can actually make a bigger difference than leveling up your character.
