#58318 kegisak Thu, 22nd Dec '11 12:27:33 PM
Yes and no. A Foil is a character that's meant to contrast with another, in order to make them both look much more distinct. This is usually done by making them opposites, but there is often a similar trait connecting the two. An example is twins who are Different as Night and Day: they may have many similar qualities, even beyond their appearance - they might both enjoy sports, but they like it in different ways. One might be a very aggressive, a football quarterback, whereas the other might be very defensive, a lineback. The similar parts give them grounds to be compared, while the opposites play up the traits more powerfully. In essence in order fro someone to be considered a foil they must both at the same time be the same, but opposites.
