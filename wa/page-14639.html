So, quick theory/rambling. there are actually a good many unicorns who, like Twilight, hve a aptitude for magic in all its forms. However, they can only acquire spell knowledge through focused study, as while the knack of spellcasting comes naturally, they have not the instinctual focus to create spells naturally. It's the sorcerer vs wizard thing. Rarity is the former, and Twilight is the latter.
