I think it's because, at least judging by Derpy's canon personality in The Last Round-Up, I just plain think she would get along quite well with Pinkie. Pinkie also seems like someone who would be pretty tolerant of Derpy's clumsiness. The subject of how Pinkie would handle being around someone else being very clumsy made me wonder, as an alternate universe kind of option, what if Spike had been trying to be Pinkie's servant instead of Applejack's? I guess I was wondering how she would handle having someone who was trying to help her but kept making more messes in the process like Spike did in the Apple family's kitchen.
