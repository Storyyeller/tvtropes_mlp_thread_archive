#395562 jennytablina Tue, 3rd Sep '13 4:55:01 PM from Cambridgeshire
Rainbow: Derpy was portayed in fan interpretations as a mailmare quite early on. I used to lurk the old /co/ generals and she was already being drawn by the fanartists in the threads as such around the time of Dragonshy and Winter Wrap Up's first airings. I recall debates on how ditzy/random/dumb she might actually be in those times too, I think most people settled for her being a bit ditzy and incredibly random.

"Feeling Pinkie Keen" was one of the first episodes to finish production after the show staff became aware of the older fandom developing and the memes surrounding it - so that little addition to Twilight having various objects dropped on her was a sort of nod to Derpy's fanon developed persona(even if it isn't quite the same thing). Something they massively expanded on in season 2 - however the whole "The Last Roundup" business sort of stopped that all in it's tracks.

It's sad really because I think Derpygate really brought an end up to heady-heights of the fandom for a bit. It caused so much fractions and in-fighting - it kind of changed the relationship between the fans and showrunners. Derpy is still my favourite pony in a lot of ways, seems that way with a lot of other people too considering merch but...it's a little sad seeing she isn't even allowed to have a name and likely is discouraged from showing up in the show much going forward.
