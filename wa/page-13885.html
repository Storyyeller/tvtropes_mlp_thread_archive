#347104 Pykrete Mon, 11th Mar '13 1:43:37 PM from Viridian Forest
Gameloft is kind of the Malt-o-Meal of games. They deal in ripoffs, albeit ones that actually tend to have effort put in, and are almost entirely takes on stuff whose original hadn't been ported to IOS yet for some reason.

They're not exactly a last resort company, but they're a bandwagon one sought out by companies looking to capitalize.

The scary thing is, this already was an example of a freemium game that had an unusual amount of effort put in. Most of them are just an incredibly rudimentary menu interface with an energy bar that proceeds to throw unbalanced pay-to-win crap in your face, and there are code templates floating around (mainly in China) that "developers" just plug in artwork (mainly stolen) and ship it. That's the sort of thing Kongregate started shilling right after they were bought out by Gamestop.
