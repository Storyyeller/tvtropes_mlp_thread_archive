When I think about musical numbers, I shake my head at how people automatically think that musical numbers stink.

I would point out to those people that not all musical numbers are bad. Sure, there are numbers in The Land Before Time that were not all that great, as an example. But Disney has a lot of numbers that were totally good quality stuff, like the "Hell Fire" song in Disney's The Hunchback of Notre Dame.

The way I see it, some of the musical numbers in MLP: FIM are totally on par with Disney's stuff, with "This Day Aria" standing out. Pinkie's songs are pretty silly, but at least the other characters acknowledge their silliness. Fluttershy sings some pretty impressive songs.
