#319525 Pykrete Thu, 20th Dec '12 5:05:06 PM from Viridian Forest
Next question: How do you actually use the button on the spot in the GPC? I've tried F, space, clicking, and nothing works.

Hit 1 to bring up the inventory (2 for spell hotkeys once you start getting them). Interact uses a held item on whatever's glowing blue, X puts it away.

What is the point of the arrowheads, since they respawn every time you change areas? The only limitation to collecting them is boredom.

Once you get the dowsing rod it speeds up considerably (you get like 50 at a time). The only thing that's really required to buy is the cobweb duster (though I REALLY recommend picking up the magnet), and you'll only absolutely need it in the last 2-3 levels in the game. If you're like me and go binging on hidden stuff, just keep the dowsing rod out while combing the camp at first and you'll have as much as you need.
