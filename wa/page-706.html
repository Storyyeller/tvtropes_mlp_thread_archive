As a note, the entire franchise goes Lighter and Softer from that pilot up until Friendship Is Magic, and one could argue that Fi M is still not quite as "dark" as the original pilot (though that's not a bad thing).
Sure, The Movie proved it was willing to treat its antagonists like complete jokes, but the TV series bounced back and forth on the level of danger the ponies faced, from Tirac-style threats that could capture everyone and wipe out Ponyland, to more incompetent villains whose plans practically unraveled themselves; it's just that they no longer had the "Dungeons & Dragons" animation style to help sell you on the serious ones the way the first special did.
