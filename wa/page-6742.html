#168539 Bluespade Sun, 22nd Apr '12 4:08:48 PM from Texas
Well I don't really have anything to say that other people haven't said a few dozen times. I loved the finale, especially the Queen and the epic nature of it. I loved how the Elements were defied as the plot-resolution-of-the-day. I liked Cadance and Shining Armor despite the limited amount of time we got to know them and I hope they'll show up again someday (they'd better, considering how Twilight is supposed to be so close to Shiny).

There are a few little details that people seemed to have an issue with (Remember the New Guy, the story focusing on new characters we had no reason to care about, the non-Twilight Mane 6 getting Demoted to Extra). While I understand the complaints and I agree that all of these points look bad on paper, I think the presentation of the episodes made up for all of them. Tropes are never automatically bad, and I think the show kept these from becoming big time failures.

I only have 2 legitimate complaints. Weak conflict resolution, just like with Discord. But again, I don't think it was bad enough to seriously weaken the episodes, although a better, more climactic defeat for the Queen (involving Shiny, Cadance, AND the Mane 6) would have bumped the finale up to another level of greatness. My other complaint is the BBBFF song, which I explained last night is cringe-worthily cheesy and awful to me, but other people seem to like it so I'm willing to forgive. This Day Aria more than makes up for it anyway.

One thing I never thought I'd say, I actually enjoyed the finale more than the episode of Legend Of Korra that aired yesterday. Considering Avatar The Last Airbender is pretty close to my favorite show of all time and so far Korra looks to be even better, that says a lot about the quality of MLP:FIM.
