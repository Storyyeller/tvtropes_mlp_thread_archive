More obviously, I'm not a big fan of Over a Barrel either; it relied too much on new characters and the mane cast didn't really do a whole lot.

I actually didn't mind Ticket Master that much; the plot has been done before, but I think that they way they did it made sense for the characters (of course Twilight doesn't know what to do, she's new at this) and the ways that the other ponies were sucking up to her was pretty entertaining.

The overall plot of Owl's Well that Ends Well wasn't that great, but between Spikely Whiplash and the quest for the Quill, I thought that it was funny enough to overlook the problems.
