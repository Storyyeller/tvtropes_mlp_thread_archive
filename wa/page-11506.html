#287642 Perpetual Lurker Wed, 26th Sep '12 9:23:54 AM
I actually frequent the forum where the creator of that fan MTG set posts, and he detailed the legal situation there. It sucks, but he really doesn't have any say in the matter. The set falls under neither fair use nor parody, as he made a whole set incorporating the entirety of both properties rather than just taking a small portion for a joke. The main gripe here is the fact that playing with this fully-functional and freely-distributed fan set is a substitute for actually buying the cards, which is cause for legal action in order to protect the copyrights of both properties.
