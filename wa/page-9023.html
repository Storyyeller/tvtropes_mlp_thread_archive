The other thing I would suggest Ginger... is to check out some autism community sites and see if anyone there has ways to help you cope.

http://www.wrongplanet.net/ is a good one to check. You might find people who share your quirks and they may have coping mechanisms that you could use.

My quirks are mostly social and procedural. I get REALLY stresed when people dont follow a procedure, to the point of wanting to hurt them for not doing it right. And dont take this as an insult. Im borderline Aspie myself and reading about other people with quirks and how they deal with them helped me deal with my own a LOT.
