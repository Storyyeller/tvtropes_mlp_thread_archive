To respond to something that was mentioned earlier, I don't think that pony society is ranked by race, like having unicorns be a higher class than pegasi and then having Earth ponies at the bottom like second-class citizens. It may have been somewhat like that in the time period that the Hearth's Warming Eve play is set in, but that play was showing how bad things USED to be, and that the three types of ponies worked together as equals to form what is current Equestria.

Anyway, if Earth ponies were treated in a similar way to marginalized races in real life, we wouldn't have cases like Cheerilee and the Mayor both being Earth ponies in positions of authority. And discounting the Canterlot snobs (which is pretty much a city of unicorns anyway, so they've got unicorns in manual labor jobs like the poor bellhop in that same episode), most of the wealthy snob ponies in the show are Earth ponies such as Diamond Tiara, Silver Spoon and Hoity Toity. I looked it up, and Photo Finish is also an Earth pony. So not all Earth ponies are manual laborers, farmers and bakers anymore, nor are all unicorns necessarily on Twilight's level (Snips and Snails are another example).
