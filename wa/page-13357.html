Dear Thread Manor...

I've got 4 new stories today, one a somewhat dark story with twilight, and 3 crossovers of sorts.

Hard Reset. Twilight is not having a very good day. First, an experimental time manipulation spell blew up in her face and seemingly didn't do anything. Then, when she was getting lunch with Spike, changelings invaded Canterlot. Then she was killed. Then she found herself back in the castle library right after an experimental time manipulation spell blew up in her face and seemingly didn't do anything. Twilight is stuck in a time loop, and she's going to fix all of this, the changeling invasion, the time loop, everything. She's going to fix it all ever if it kills her, again. And again. And again...

This was more entertaining than I thought it would be. The dark tag is because Twilight dies, a lot.

A Day at the Quest Hub- in progress, side story to World of Ponycraft. In this new post-Deathwing Equestria, Carrot Top is having a hard time making ends meet. She wonders how her long time friendly rival Applejack is managing to keep afloat financially, and discovers that she has taken up adventuring. There's lots of cash to be made questing! Carrot Top decides to try it out too. It can't be that hard, right?...

This is the last story I was expecting to see a side story for, since it's such an old one. It's pretty good so far.

The Rise of Iron Mare- in progress. 20 years after inventing the first magical power cell, Twilight Sparkle, CEO and founder of Sparkle Industries, is one of the most famous and wealthy ponies in Equestria. Her life is thrown upside down however, when she is kidnapped by some griffon terrorists who want her to make weapons for them.

Yep, Twilight as Iron Mare. Fairly standard fare so far.

Cuatro- in progress. A strange Alicorn with bat wings and a whip like tail wakes up in the middle of a blizzard in the Everfree. He manages to stumble to the door of Sweet Apple Acres before collapsing. When he wakes, AJ and AB discover that he has amnesia and can't remember anything about himself, not even his own name. AJ decides to take him to Twilight to see if she can't help him out. Who was he? Where did he come from? And why is his Cutie mark just a simple "4"?

It's a crossover with Bleach. I've only ever seen a few episodes of Bleach, so I have no idea how well the author is doing keeping in character, but it's a fairly enjoyable read so far.

That's all for now. I hope you found something of interest! Till next time!
