#327694 Ace Of Scarabs Thu, 10th Jan '13 6:46:50 AM from Singapore
@Lurker: Like I said, I don't suscribe to stable time loops. There's always a source timeline where the change was originally made, followed by X recursions with gradually accruing differences (like when Homura embarked on her timeline-tampering spree to save Madoka), followed by a final, stable timeline which cannot be reconciled with the original source timeline. So there'll be an original timeline where the original "you" creates a gen-1 clone, after X timelines it results in a 1+x clone with x generations of degradations, which will ultimately result in a final timeline where "you" don't exist because your 1+x clone died of degenerative illness from clone degradation before he could create one more generation. Simple math.

At best, you perceive one/some of the transitionary looplike generations of clones cloning each other.
