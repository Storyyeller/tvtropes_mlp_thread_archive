I think all three 'sisterly' bonds are dysfunctional in their own way (four if you count Twilight and Spike). Rainbow's would likely be dysfunctional full time since at heart, she is rather self absorbed and not the most perceptive of someone around her in trouble. She often knows how to handle things when handed to her however and is at the very least a good friend. Twilight's is arguably all of the above to a lighter degree, in both a negative and positive sense.
