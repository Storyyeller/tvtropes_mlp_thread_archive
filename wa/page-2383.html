#59572 Perpetual Lurker Sat, 24th Dec '11 12:34:51 AM
So, as my name here suggests, I've been lurking. A lot. I've finally decided to join in the pony discussion for once, as there's just something about this show that clicks with me. It's probably the fact that it's very character driven, even given it's typical episodic nature. Most cartoons nowadays have a "1) Put static silly character in silly situation. 2) have silly things happen. 3) ??? 4) Profit." approach to their storytelling. Fi M manages to make it the characters' own attributes that create the situations, and they have to solve it with the help of the other characters. Even if a plot contrivance creates a situation, the character themselves causes the plot to develop or advance, not just a natural progression that the character is reacting to. The fact that the humor is also top notch and harkens back to my childhood also has a lot to do with how awesome this show is.

While most anything I watch or read that interests me has me shooting ideas around in my head that could potentially be "fanfiction", the character driven nature of the show here lets me actually figure out a plot in a much more coherent manner, so much so that I'm tempted to actually write something. I just need to get over my internet-bred dislike of fanfics in general first, as I've been creeped out/disappointed by all of the chaff on the internet that I really want to avoid becoming "one of them", so to speak. So, that's my long winded introduction, hopefully I won't return to lurking forever again.
