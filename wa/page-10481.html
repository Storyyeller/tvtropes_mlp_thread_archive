Another important point in that theory for me is that those "connections" are incredibly vague. An earth pony would find it easiest to be good at growing a plant because of their connection to the soil, which supports and feeds the plant. A unicorn, on the other hand, could theoretically assist a plant's growth through a connection to the sun, which provides a plant with its energy, but those that would be capable of doing so would be rarer. The more abstract the connection, the less common it is, but it is still possible.
