I never thought much of it until AJ said "[They] normally don't wear clothes" and Rarity said "some have standards."

Second is word of god and merchandise; if cannon hasn't made mention of something, this comes next. Ever since the trading cards came out, we've learned that the "time lord pony" is called Doctor Hooves. To those who noticed, I have stopped calling him Doctor Whooves. I will not call him that again unless cannon says otherwise.

Lastly is head cannon and fannon*; this is when the first two have yet to make any statement. Basically, I have free range on how I can interpret Orion. Because of this, we've established the conditioner gag, his immortality, and his cutie mark origins. If cannon, word of god, or merchandise ever make a statement on this character, then that WILL take precedence over my head cannon. So if this character gets a name, I will have to make do with that. However, if such a thing does happen, I will have a field day with this thread.
