#9588 Japanese Teeth Sun, 10th Jul '11 10:49:53 AM from Meinong's jungle from Meinong's jungle
The problem with the character's ages is that their social positions and personalities don't really match. They're mostly living on their own, and they all have jobs, so they'd probably be roughly college age by that standard. But then if you based it solely on personality, they act a lot younger. I'm pretty sure the age is ambiguous on purpose.

As for the whole "Rainbow Dash is a lesbian" thing, the issue wasn't really that people thought she was a lesbian, it was the fact that they were making the assumption that she was based on the rainbow thing and the fact that she was a tomboy; basically they envisioned her as not just a lesbian, but a lesbian stereotype. I'm pretty sure Faust said somewhere along the line that making the assumption that RD is a lesbian because she's a tomboy is unfair for both heterosexual tomboys and non-butch lesbians.

About shipping: it doesn't bother me too much, and as an author I actually find it interesting to think of stuff like "what would happen if these characters were a couple". But when people start getting militant about it or trying to claim their fan pairings as canon, it's incredibly annoying.

Also, season 2 needs more Big Macintosh, cause he's awesome.
