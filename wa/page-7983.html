#199557 Perpetual Lurker Sun, 20th May '12 3:28:41 PM
@JT It's really more a problem with Pinkie's character type than how she's actually written. Everyone seems to agree that Pinkie's character is at her best when she is shown to be vulnerable, distressed, or otherwise not happy and silly all the time. The problem is, though, that you simply can't do that to her when she's not the focus of the episode, so your options for her in those situations are to either have her crack jokes on the side (which is exactly what she would do in most any situation), have something distress her directly (only PYHD has done this, to my knowledge) or not have her be involved at all.
