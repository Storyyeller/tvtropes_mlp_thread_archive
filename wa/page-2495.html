So, the results of a recently concluded Russian brony poll.

Most things are pretty standard, but several things of note:

  1. "How do people you know react to MLP?" got barely any "Negative" answers
  2. People tend to find out about MLP almost exclusively via image boards
  3. Rarity is astoundingly unpopular, ranking noticeably lower than the entire mane cast and the princesses. Spike is basically on background pony level.
  4. Half the people admit to clopping.
  5. Episode popularity: Winter Wrap Up, Luna Eclipsed, Sonic Rainboom, Best Night Ever. All CMC episodes, sans Sisterhooves, rank very low, bottom three by far are A Dog and Pony Show, Owl's Well, and Griffon the Brush-off, followed by MMDW and Cutie Pox.

Also? Lesson Zero is also very low in this fandom. Really really low. On par with Over a Barrel.

I think this is interesting. I am really surprised at some of the results. I guess that indicates just how different my mentality is that of a random Russian geek. Perhaps because I was raised by the Internet.
