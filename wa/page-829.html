#20719 Japanese Teeth Wed, 21st Sep '11 8:53:40 AM from Meinong's jungle from Meinong's jungle
I'm honestly willing to forgive his "Rage Quit" on Fluttershy because I think it was one of the funniest moments of the episodes. I agree that I would have liked to see him exploiting the cast's personalities a bit more, but we have to keep two things in mind:

1. The show's primary demographic is little girls, so the manipulation has to be simple, and it can't be too subtle or it'll go over their heads. Yes, it could have been more complex, but doing it that way would be ignoring the primary demographic in favor of the secondary one.

2. They only had about 20 minutes to work with. They couldn't really dedicate more than a minute or two to each pony being corrupted, so they had to use a simplified version.
