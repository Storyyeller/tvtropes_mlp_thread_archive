@FL: I love T. S. Eliot, but if you don't know much about poetry, he might be a little too dense. I love Shakespeare's sonnets, too, but I know a lot of people have trouble with them. (My fav of his is 130.)

I love the major Romantic Poets, and they are not typically very difficult.

I love Lord Tennyson, too. Read The Lady of Shalott, if you haven't already. It is a Narrative Poem for the most part, and it is beautiful.

I am a big fan of Pablo Neruda. He encouraged me to learn Spanish more on my own.

I also love Epic Poetry, but it takes a long time to read.
