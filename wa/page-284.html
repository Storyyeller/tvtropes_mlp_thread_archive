#7100 Eventua Thu, 16th Jun '11 11:24:44 AM from onboard the Citadel from onboard the Citadel
So a while ago I made a game!
Specifically, a Collectible Card Game about a bunch of teenagers armed with alien tech to summon a friendly variation of The Heartless using a mixture of random crap that's just lieing around and emotional energy.

And I decided to make a Fluttershy card for it. Unfortunately, limited file size is limited.

Basically, a quick low-down about stuff on the card:

  • Pale green background shows the card is Phlegmatic
  • Top red number = strength (how well the card does during contests)
  • Other red number = support (how well the card helps out neighbouring cards during their contests)
  • Green number = intellect (how many points (called data) you get for activating (flipping it face up) the card)
  • Blue number = cost (how much energy it costs to activate the card)
