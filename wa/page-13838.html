Re: Order of ascension, here's how I see it:

  • Logically, Twilight was first. Element of Magic, de facto team leader, personal protege of the princess; it makes sense.

  • Applejack would be next. She's the most solid and least prone to freak out, and her biggest flaw is that she's too dedicated to helping those around her, which isn't that bad of a flaw for a leader to have. Not to mention that she's already used to acting as an authority figure to Apple Bloom, and she's basically the Team Mom anyway.

  • After that would be Rarity. She's responsible enough to run her own business, and while she's rather histrionic she's good enough at recognizing and correcting her own flaws that she'd be okay. She'd definitely have to overcome her Attention Whore tendencies, though.

  • Fluttershy's main problem would be her dislike for imposing herself on others. Her character as a leader would work, but she'd have to learn to give directions and orders to other ponies without clarifying it with an "um...if that's okay with you" afterward.

  • Pinkie or RD would be last. Pinkie has a lot of difficulty knowing when to reign in the insanity, so she'd have to learn when to be serious. RD has definitely leadership skills, but she's also a glory hound and would have to learn to reign in her "LOOK AT ME I'M AWESOME" tendencies when she's in a position where she'll be constantly lauded.
