Now as for your Persona question, Persona 3 and 4 are essentially a different series than the first 3 games. They're sort of a reboot, and more or less in a different canon. Persona 3 is great, nearly as good as Persona 4 in every way, but the original version of it had some seriously glaring issues that detract from the experience. If you can, get a hold of the PSP version, which fixed every major issue with the game and added a ton of new content, including the choice between a Male or Female main character. Overall, I liked number 4 better, but the PSP version of Persona 3 is technically as good as it, so it'll likely come down to personal preference. Persona 3 is much darker, and has the main cast as a group of comrades focused on their mission. Persona 4 is more lighthearted and much more focuses on the team as a group of FRIENDS, with their friendship being the center of the game.
