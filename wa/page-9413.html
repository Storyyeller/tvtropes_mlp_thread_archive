#235306 Pannic Tue, 26th Jun '12 11:11:54 AM
Allow me to explain something that happened on Glee.

There are two cheerleader characters, Brittany (The Ditz) and Santana (wisecracking bitch). In season one, there's a scene where all the characters are on cellphones, talking about some development (probably in regards to Finn's dating life, I forget). Eventually, this line comes up:

  • Santana: "Sex isn't dating."
  • Brittany: "If it were then Santana and I would be dating."

Cue funny facial expressions from everyone involved.

One-off gag. Then the shippers all decide to start pairing the two of them together, and you know what? The show runners listened and it became canon. Led to a Brittana storyline, a love triangle involving the wheelchair kid who ended up kinda getting shafted in a development that still leads me to cry "bullshit!", and a storyline about Santana coming out as a lesbian.

So the shippers actually had a direct effect on the direction of the show.
