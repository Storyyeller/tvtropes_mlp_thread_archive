Tentative Horses in Equestria:

They live in small villages, mostly agrarian-based, with little of the Earth Pony innate magic ability, (Earth Pony innate magic is a result of their distant relation to Alicorns) so they live simply and mostly off of the land. They are reliant on pony-kind for tools and technological things as a resut. However, they are powerful workers and near-tireless. They tend their simple farms and barter their wares with earth ponies for the things that they need, so they co-exist well.

In the distant past, they were used as soldiers, having the stamina and strength to go into battle as ground troops against predators and stuff. They have a strong sense of pride and stubborn nobility, remmnants of their past warrior traditions.

Aside from trade with other Earth Ponies, their views on those other ponies is a bit more complicated. They revere Alicorns as veritable dieties, more so than ponies do.
