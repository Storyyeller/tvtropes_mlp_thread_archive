#317835 Tiggers Are Great Sun, 16th Dec '12 3:19:31 PM
I don't know about you guys, but I hope King Sombra makes a comeback in some form.

Why? Well, I see him as the pony equivalent of Diablo. Consider this:

  1. In the first Diablo, Diablo the Lord of Terror was the Final Boss. But despite having his name as the title, he was never mentioned until close to the very end of the game. Unlike the other bosses and characters of the game, he doesn't say anything to you. He just attacks you instead, and it comes off as rather jarring. Indeed, so little information was given on the character at the time that he was no more than a Generic Doomsday Villain.
  2. In the second game Diablo II, Diablo is once again the final boss, until his brother Baal became this in Lord Of Destruction. A lot more information is revealed on the Lord of Terror, and it turns out that he played you like a violin in the first game, and he does the same in the second game. This time, he says one thing to you: "Not even death can save you from me!" This proves that he can talk, but he apparently prefers not to.
  3. In the third game Diablo III, Diablo does not appear until the very end. In fact, it turns out that he has a backup plan, one that would allow him to come back in 20 years even after getting killed off...and become more powerful than ever. He uses his daughter Leah as a host body along with six other powerful demons, and invades Heaven itself. It initially looks like he'll win again, just as he always has. But you'll notice that he is extremely chatty this time around, and it turns out that he, the Lord of Terror, is terrified of you. He'll end up flying into a Villainous Breakdown, and you will finally have the satisfaction of crushing him flat. However, there is the question of whether he is gone for good, or if he will return....

I'm pointing all this out, because King Sombra makes me think of Diablo. If Sombra comes back, I bet we'll find out quite a bit about him. <Nervous>
