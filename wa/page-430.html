Another week of reading fanfiction and it seems to me that "Celestia becomes the Nightmare Sun!" is the most common fanfic idea that doesn't NEED to involve shipping.

It seems a lot of fandom is showing something fandoms often show for something you start to like when you are a little kid and continue to like as you grow older - when you go older you somehow want the show to be the same, yet you want it to be more "adult and serious". Except here people START as twentysomething males who like the show for little girls. (I'm sure there some trope for the phenomenon... I just can't remember what it is called.)

This is not a diss on Grimdark fanfic. Fallout: Equestria is excellent, for instance. And for some reason I find a lot of the Cupcakes-based John Pinkie Gacy fanart hilarious...

It would be really fun to see more relatives and friends of the main cast next season! Not everyone need to have them alive or present, mind you. It's not unrealistic for there to be at least one parent who is absent among six twenty-year-olds in the real world, so that would be OK. And for some reason I would be OK if we met new friends/rivals/enemies of the main cast instead of bringing back old ones. The one exception is that I would like to learn more of Luna.
