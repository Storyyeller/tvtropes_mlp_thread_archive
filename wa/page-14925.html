I was watching the G3 Runaway Rainbow movie today and I noticed some interesting parallels and comparisons to the Fi M version of the series. First of all, maybe because that specific movie has a main character named Rarity in it, I kept having to remind myself to not get her confused with Rainbow Dash because G3 Rainbow Dash not only acts a lot like G4 Rarity, but they even have almost the same accent (not quite the same, but very similar). And G3 Rarity acts more similar to G4 Rainbow Dash in that they both like to have adventures and can be kind of irresponsible sometimes, and she also has some similar traits to G4 Pinkie. In fact, she even looks like a cross between G4 Pinkie and Rainbow Dash, since she has a pink body like Pinkie but a rainbow-colored mane.

Also, an interesting parallel between G3 Rainbow Dash and G4 Rarity is that G3 Dash went on the expedition to help Rarity find her home again, but like G4 Rarity, she packed a LOT of luggage in a cart and she wasn't the one who pulled the cart. In this case, it was G3 Spike who pulled it, which mirrors G4 Spike's role as often being eager to do chores and manual labor to assist Rarity, who he loves.
