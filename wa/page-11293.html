It's been around for years, although the third edition is comparatively recent. It's a pen and paper rpg in which you play a human (or other entity) bound to a metaphysical concept, so you might be the Power of Trousers, Power of Fire, Power of Ducks or Power of Hamburger. You then use the godlike power you have within this field to save the world from incredibly beautiful Humanoid Abominations that ride in from outside reality and cripple various concepts, sometimes including the ones the players are using. There's a lot more weirdness and poetry in there than this summary; I'm just obsessing over it at the moment thanks to two gentlemen named Strange and Norrell putting me in mind of it and sending me on the track to rpgnet spoiler threads and wondering which stats each pony would focus on and so on.
