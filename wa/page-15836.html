I was on my way back from my final exams, and for some reason I started thinking about this one time on Fimfiction I helped someone with algebra homework by framing it in terms of Applejack's farm and running costs and stuff.

And then I started thinking about a series of pony teaching aid clips. Like, old-school PBS style, starring Twilight and Cheerilee, with guest stars from the others. Rarity can teach area of a circle by cutting a crochet coaster (the kind made of rings) down the radius and unfolding it into an r-by-2πr triangle. Dash can talk about parabolics. And so on. Like, all the stuff schools try to teach as blank, abstract equations — let the ponies frame it in terms of concrete objects, because kids tend to learn better that way.

This is probably a good reason why you shouldn't ask me about good ideas after a hell week of projects and two exams, but my addled mind thought it was interesting.
