@Mio: One of the fun bits about the show is that how many chemistries are rather even handed. Even regarding the wavering sanity of each character, there is rarely an "all take no give" relationship where you wonder why one puts up with the other. All the characters foil each other well to some extent, maybe what fuels "debates" such as these.

Pinkie is childish and nutty, but her eccentric but warm hearted outlook is just as often right or wrong as Twilight's logical but cold one. Similarly RD is too aggressive, but works well with the too meek Fluttershy. There is no consistant "competent vs incompetent character" dynamic.
