Pretty good episode. Might argue the best of the individual CMC spotlight episodes (unless you count Bridle Gossip for AB).

I didn't mind the Luna appearance, nice establishment, plus I liked Luna being loud and hammy at the end of it (worried that part of her might have dissappeared).

I also like the good balance of personality for both Rarity and RD, showing them interchange well between being caring role models for the CM and Innocently Insensitive of the highest order. We finally got some real development between Scootaloo and RD, and they did well to show RD caring and playful towards Scootaloo while also showing she isn't most perceptive of the big sis role as the other two.

Will likely serve as good balance since I get the feeling the next episode will be RD at her most abrasive again.

The only major complaint I may have is that it was still largely developing a CMC based largely on the people they interact with rather than their individual personality. Both of the other two cutie marks are shown similarly terrified as Scoots for example, but the pivot for them not being part of the story is they don't have RD as a peer.

Was anyone else reminded a bit of Roo's role in The Tigger Movie with how Scoots wanted a big sister?
