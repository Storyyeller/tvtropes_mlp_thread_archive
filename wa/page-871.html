It's interesting how nitpicky people can get after they have had a few hours to digest an episode (myself included).

I guess some final thoughts from me. I actually liked the second half of the episode more then the first half, but I can see why most people would react the opposite. It seems that that Discord got hit with the idiot ball at the very end, but I always felt that he came across as a bit to powerful so that kind of ending almost felt inevitable to me.

And I guess ultimately this episode will stand as a point in favor of the "slice of life only" portion of the fandom. While the episode certainly isn't the worst by any means, it does demonstrate how the show at this point really can't handle these kinds of adventure without coming across awkward in some fashion (it certainly doesn't help that most of the writers don't seem to have all that much experience with these kinds of plots before this show).
