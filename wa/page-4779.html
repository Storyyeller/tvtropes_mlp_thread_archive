I notice that this season has been placing some of the cast in more stricter roles, eg. Rainbow Dash is more freqeuently the Commander Contrarian, Applejack is more The Straight Man, Twilight is more of a Nervous Wreck. Sympathetic can be termed in different ways, Rainbow is a bit jerkier now but she is also a more dominant Butt Monkey / Jerkass Woobie as a result of it. It's like comparing the versions of Daffy Duck, the later one was much more vindictive and selfish, but he was also more humanized and suffered so much abuse it was hard not to feel sorry for him. A nicer character isn't neccessarily more sympathetic and developed.
