If I might go on a POSITIVE rant for a second?

This was, without a doubt, the absolute MOST I've liked Pinkie Pie since Party of One. IMO this episode used her perfectly. She didn't steal the spotlight from others yet was still able to play her role as the comic relief. The comedy itself wasn't obnoxious or annoying, dare I say I found some of it... charming. (like I loved her little "s'allright" after she pushed RD out of the way of the falling plant ).

She was actually able to help the mane cast out in a decidedly non-insane manner, while still remaining true to her character. She utilized her ACTUAL skills to help, rather than using her Rule of Funny powers, which I've demonstrated my disdain for in the past. (as I've mentioned before, I greatly enjoyed the return of the Pinkie Sense)

But most importantly: she seemed human (er, pony). She wasn't as loud and bombastic as she normally was. She was more bubbly and sweet, which I honestly prefered. Plus, she was angry for a lot of the episode, and not in an over the top manner either. I honestly felt her frustration and her desire to help.

Yes, if nothing else I can say that this new writer writes Pinkie well (for ME anyway)
