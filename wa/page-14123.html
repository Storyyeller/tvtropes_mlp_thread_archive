#353059 Perpetual Lurker Tue, 2nd Apr '13 6:57:46 AM
So, The Crystal Heart Spell came out last night. It's a pretty good read, all things considered. The concessions to younger readers are really obvious in some places, and the author seems to be operating under the assumption that Trixie and Gilda either live in Ponyville or are there a lot of the time, but with those details aside, it really does good by the characters, as well as the world-building. Cadance in particular gets a lot of fleshing out.

Random fun facts that weren't given out in the first big leak:

  • Pound and Pumpkin Cake have learned to say "giddy-up", which is just adorable.
  • Cadance talks a little bit about friends she had when she lived in Canterlot. Buttercream, an Earth Pony with an affinity for chocolate, and Sky Chariot, a rather up-tight pegasus guard.
  • The "Crystal Heart Spell" itself seems to be a trait of alicorns. It's a manifestation of a fundimental, personal truth that serves as guidance for the one that invokes it, and invoking it requires that truth to be discovered in the first place. It's definitely not your typical "fix everything" spell.
