#302140 Perpetual Lurker Sun, 11th Nov '12 12:14:30 PM
So, upon rewatching the episodes, I've noticed that there is a lot more potential foreshadowing going on than just Celestia's plans for Twilight.

  • When Twilight triggers the Nightmare Door trap, notice that during the entire bit where she tries to chase down the door, her magic aura gets progressively darker as she gets angrier until she lashes out with black magic, which backfires. That could easily become a problem for her later on down the line.

  • When Cadance activates the Crystal Heart, she is identified as "The Crystal Princess" by a pony and her cutie mark glows. There's definitely something important about that, and since the episode doesn't touch on it again, it'll probably be important for the next time the Crystal Empire becomes important.

  • As I stated above, the focus on Sombra's regenerative abilities and the fact that his horn was intact in the end.

While some of these might just be neat details, they're certainly ripe for expansion and elaboration later in the season.
