#295783 Turtlebutter Wed, 24th Oct '12 7:01:41 PM from A Marshy Pond
I think from the front his hairstyle kinda looks like one of those 50's greaser hairstyles.

Its not a horrible learning curve, you just have to get used to the fact that you dont really have to press down hard to get a line. If you get a good tablet it will let you do variable pressures, light touch for thin line heavier for thicker, but you don't need to press nearly as hard as if you were using a pencil. It also has a weird texture that is more like drawing paper, but if you are using drawing paper instead of copy or notebook paper then that shouldn't bother you at all.

Positioning on the tablet is also something to get used to. You have this little tablet and a great big screen so judging distances and sizing takes getting used to.
