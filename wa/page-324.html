#8096 Moe Dantes Sun, 26th Jun '11 4:18:10 PM from the Land of Classics from the Land of Classics
Hmm, I just needed flashy, powerful elements for all 6 of them. Heart, while aswesome, doesn't tie into the forces of nature much.

Yeah I was being kind of silly about heart.

The problem with making "Nature" an element is that, Earth fire wind and water are all parts of nature, so its redundant (and a little weird, since you're saying its on equal standing with the things that are a part of it). Because of this, its also way too vague an element.

The four classical elements pretty much cover everything. I've seen some RP Gs that cheat by casting "Light" and "Dark" as elements. Problematically "Dark" usually has evil intonations, though this doesn't have to be the case (Secret of Mana is an example where it doesn't, or at least its not explicitly bound to the forces of evil). If you wanna go with that though, Light fits Fluttershy and Dark fits Twilight Sparkle.

I've seen some other "cheap" elements too. Here's a quick list:

  • Metal
  • Plastic
  • Technology
  • Spirit
  • "Pure" Magic (ie non-elemental)

Brownie is lost on the way home from school. A police officer pony helps her cross the street. She likes him. He helps her find her way home. Her parents thank him, and he goes on his way. She never sees him again, but remembers him. She'd like to be like him.

A pegasus couple is moving out of their home in the clouds. They have adopted an earth pony, and she won't be able to live there. They don't mind, though. They'll care for her like they should. They meet the filly. She sees their wings and wishes that she could fly.

A family of unicorns is packing up a wagon. The parents can't afford to live in the house anymore, and they need to find someplace else to work. They pack up some furniture, food, and a few spare belongings. The youngest one, Gold Standard, has a stuffed bear.

Ritardando enters into a music contest. His special talent is music. So is everypony else's. His hastily-constructed, under-rehearsed one-pony band show does not impress the judges. The contest is won by a unicorn who can magically produce the sound of a fifty-piece orchestra.
