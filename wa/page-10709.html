#267720 Japanese Teeth Fri, 10th Aug '12 10:28:30 PM from Meinong's jungle
I'm not claiming that it's a straight sugar bowl, or that there are no problems. It's just that I've run into a lot of people (not necessarily around here) who just assume that Equestria is really just the same as earth but with ponies. I.e. that it's plagued by stuff like xenophobia, racism, classism, etc. because we've seen "soft" versions of those problems appear in the show. To use a somewhat generic example, I find it kinda weird that some people assume that there's a whole upper/lower class struggle comparable to ones that occur in Real Life even though at the absolute worst nothing like that appears in the show. Take Blueblood for example. He's basically spoiled rotten prince, but a light and somewhat harmless version who's basically just a jerk. But I've run into people who basically assume that in "reality" he's a classist who uses his status abuse the peasants and that we just don't see that because it's a kids show. As opposed to just taking him at face value being a somewhat harmless jackass.

EDIT: Basically what Meta Four said. The issues in Equestria are evident enough that they're relateable, but I think it's better than earth by a fair margin.
