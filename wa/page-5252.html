I always assumed that pegasai magic was in making them aerodynamic, not in actually assisting in their propulsion, with the "bubble" that forms in front of Dash before she pulls off the Sonic Rainboom being the visual representation of that. It basically removes air resistance and shunts air across the wings to generate lift. As for hovering, I'd attribute that to manipulated air currents as well, but in a more enclosed and circular manner. It's just an extension of their weather manipulation abilities.

Their wings definitely aren't vestigial, though. They need to be large and powerful to generate thrust and direct the air forced over them by their magic.
