I'm fully aware. There's a reason that I usually avoid writing Time Travel but when I actually need to anyway, I'm going to have some fun with it. The spell Twilight used results in You Already Changed The Past and anypony affected by it becomes immune to future castings. The spell the Discord used is repeatable, but you are unable to interact with anything. Travel to the future allows you to interact with stuff, but it's only a possible future. When the timeline stabalises, only some copies of younger travelers will remain. That's why Doctor Whooves's younger self (of course, he wasn't named that yet) was in the battle for Appleloosa and Derpy's younger self witnessed the events of Cutie Pox and a later version of herself participated in the Best Young Fliers competition.
