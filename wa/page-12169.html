Uh, I'm pretty sure all of the positive aspects of their abilities have come up over the course of the show.

  • Twilight has Winter Wrap Up

  • Rarity has A Dog and Pony Show and Suited for Success

  • Fluttershy has Stare Master

  • Applejack has pretty much every episode aside from the ones centered around her (seriously, unless the episode is about one of her flaws, she's usually the most solid character, and even in her own episodes, the town basically reveres her; the fact that she screwed up in Applebuck Season and The Last Roundup are exceptions.)

  • Rainbow Dash has Sonic Rainboom and Hurricane Fluttershy

  • Pinkie has Swarm of the Century and Feeling Pinkie Keen.

Basically, the show does have lots of places where it shows the ponies at the top of their game, it's just that the plots are usually centered around character flaws. Which makes sense given that the show is intended to teach kids to deal with those flaws.
