Ultra-basic primer to make plushie:

  1. Get material
  2. Have a rough idea of the shapes you need
  3. Outline shapes on material
  4. Carefully cut out the shapes
  5. Stitch together the "wrong way", leave a gap, turn inside out so the stitches are on the inside
  6. Stuff with your soft plushie filling, close the gap
  7. Add details and stitch individual bits together
