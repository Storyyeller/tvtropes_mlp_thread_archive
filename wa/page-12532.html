I actually would like it if "weird" names were more common, because I think that a wider variety of names should be socially acceptable, including My Little Pony type names. This is because I like those kinds of names myself, ALONG with more "normal" names, and the divide between the two is rather arbitrary. One can't just say "no names that are common nouns" because then that would eliminate "normal" girls' names like Hope and Rose. And "the kid will be bullied!" argument just means that I think that people should be taught NOT to bully people for something they can't help anyway. My personal stance on bullying prevention is that the best way to do that is to encourage tolerance for differences in general anyway, and to focus on the bullies' behavior, not the victims.

The name thing is kind of a personal Berserk Button issue of mine, though.
