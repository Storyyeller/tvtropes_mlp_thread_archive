I think it could also be said that editing spells requires one to, you know, be able to keep what their editing intact to begin with, otherwise the spell fails(A reflexive anti-magic on the part of the object?). Which is probably why Gender-changing is harder than age-changing. Age changing alters what's already there, Gender swappign requires you to both alter some parts and fabricate others, while keeping them consistent and cohesive with the whole, without completely eradicating the other bits so that they can change back later.
