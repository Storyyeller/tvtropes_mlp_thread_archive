@ Apple: Firstly you'll need to decide on an update schedule, a reliable schedule will make you more appealing at least that's how it works with me. I'd recommend a large break between updates to start with, maybe try weekly. If it turns out you can update faster you can always change the schedule later. Also build up a buffer to start with between comics you've done and comics you've uploaded. And you'll need somewhere to put it.

Of course I've never done something like this so I'm just parroting information I've heard from other webcomics.

No wait first you should decide on content that's more important.

@Apple: Your best bet is to get a basic idea of the story you want as a whole - not necessary details, but just a skeleton idea. Maybe have the first, say, 10 comics planned out.
