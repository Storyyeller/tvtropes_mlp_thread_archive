#392080 Meta Four Mon, 19th Aug '13 3:26:31 PM from riding the wave
Much of my character-building comes from actually writing the story, rather than pre-planning. From the beginning, I had planned a scene with Ditzy Doo and Carrot Top eating lunch together. It wasn't until I actually wrote that scene and had to come up with real words to put in Carrot Top's mouth that I realized what her personality—and the sort of friendship she has with Ditzy—should actually be.

Much later, there's a scene where Constable Peeler has a run-in with Dr. Hooves. When I was planning the scene, I thought Peeler would be cowed into submission by Hooves. But when it came time to write the thing, that plan no longer felt right—it was suddenly plain as day that Peeler should go Obstructive Bureaucrat on Hooves instead.
