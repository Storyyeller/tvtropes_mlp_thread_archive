#290559 Ace Of Scarabs Wed, 3rd Oct '12 9:35:07 PM from Singapore
The modern Equestrian Royal Guard is composed of a mix of ponies.

Pegasi provide air tactics, weather manipulation (literal fog of war and weaponized gale-force winds/rain/lightning/hail), and pure mobility.

Earth Ponies are trap specialists, turning the land itself against hostiles. They also have unparalleled camo skills and superior stamina.

Unicorns are the mages and intel, providing support magic and offensive magic-based bombardment. Also Telekinesis gives them better leverage.

For more info about Equestria's Pre-classical history, the fanfic A Brief History of Equestria gives a pretty good glimpse.
