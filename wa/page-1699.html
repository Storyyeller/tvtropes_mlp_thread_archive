Hey, speaking of drawings, I had some free time today so I took a crack at drawing Peeler myself! Well, a few cracks, it would seem, but they were all just starting with the same base and adding on to it.

I couldn't decide if I wanted to give him facial hair, you see, so I did some experimenting.

He struck me as the clean-shaven◊ type first, but that looked too young.

so I tried a nice, solid mustache, but that just looked odd.

I finally went full-on beard, and I think that one looks really nice. It fits with the nice, Stout Strength sort of portliness I tried to give him. and honestly between that and the beard he was starting to look a little bit like my dad.

and then, having avoided it three times, I finally got the nerve to try drawing him with his helmet. There's another one where the helmet has a strap, but it's too small of a detail to bother with another link.

But yeah. I gave him a nice, portly sort of frame, to make him look a bit more friendly. The badge on the back of his coat is supposed to be his cutie mark, just printed on the coat. Was it standard issue, and that's where the mark come from? Does Trottingham let it's police force customize their uniform with their marks? Who knows!
