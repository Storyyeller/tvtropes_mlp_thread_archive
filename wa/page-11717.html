#292906 Japanese Teeth Fri, 12th Oct '12 9:56:37 AM from Meinong's jungle
@Sereg: It really depends on how the fic handles it. The difference is that once something appears in canon it's, well, canon. As in, it's just as much a part of the setting as everything else and becomes an irremovable aspect of the setting, which isn't true for fanfic unless I want to headcanon it. I.e. if canon introduces another Sealed Evil in a Can, it's something I can't get rid of unless I call Discontinuity on it, which I really don't like doing. Whereas if a fanfic does it it's just an AU and I can take or leave it.

But really it just comes down to the fact that I'd much rather see the cast have to deal with a contemporary villain rather some ancient evil or whatever.

As regards Griffons and Diamond Dogs, I'm assuming that the "only hoofed mammals can talk" rule only applies to real animals. Mythological creatures like Dragons and Griffons (and Diamond Dogs; they're really more like Trolls than anything else) are basically whatever the writers feel like.
