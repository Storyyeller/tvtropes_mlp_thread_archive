It's just I feel AJ is getting duller with each season.

By Season Three she just seems completely mellowed out, she has problems starting off stories and even as a Straight Man she seems too dull (compared to Twilight who can play both roles but use her neurotics to give it full potential).

I don't expect AJ to be completely over the top, but the thing is she needs at least some exagerrative points to keep on par with the rest of the cast or even just make for interesting roles. I likely wouldn't complain as much if she hadn't gotten the balance perfectly fine in Season One anyway.
