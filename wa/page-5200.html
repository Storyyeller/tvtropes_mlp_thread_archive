#129989 Perpetual Lurker Sun, 18th Mar '12 11:00:30 AM
Alchemy is one of those terms that can end up having almost completely different meanings in different fantasy settings. In classical mythology, Alchemy was the practice of turning various metals into gold. This "transmutation" is what is extrapolated into the "alchemy" used in Fullmetal Alchemist. It also ends up being a catch-all term for potion-making thanks to its strong relation to real life chemistry. In fact, a lot of breakthroughs in early chemistry came from attempting to study alchemy. The only thing that remains constant with fantasy alchemy is its strong blending of magic and science.
