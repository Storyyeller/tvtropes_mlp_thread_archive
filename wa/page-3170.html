Interesting...remember how the Heartstrings◊ toy and card have noticeably different colors? Well, if you look closely at the box◊ that holds the Wave 3 Blind Bags, you can see that it shows a Heartstrings toy that matches the card! (And this isn't what the actual toys look like; photos from people who've bought them from stores show that they're colored the same as the leaked ones.) I'm guessing it's a prototype, since apparently the original box for the Wave 1 Blind Bags shows a pegasus version of earth pony Minty (also shown as a pegasus on the back of her card). Furthermore, Wave 1 Blind Bag ponies Pepperdance & Lucky Swirl's original European cards have differently-colored hair which was corrected in the Australian and American releases, and Wave 2's special edition Applejack◊ has purple eyes on the card but show-matching green eyes on the toy.

TL;DR version: The Heartstrings◊ card artwork not matching the toy or the show is most likely just an error.
