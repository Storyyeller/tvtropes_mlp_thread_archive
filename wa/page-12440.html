@Guns: Holding a gun in the mouth is a terrible idea. Why? Because of the recoil. There's a reason you use both hands while firing a pistol and make damn sure the butt of the rifle is pressed to your shoulder before pulling the trigger. It kicks. Hard. Holding a gun in your mouth, or having it strapped to your head, no matter if you're a horse or a human, will not only throw off your aim, it has a good chance of snapping your neck, killing you instantly, or at the very least paralyzing for the rest of your short life.

And if you were lucky and the gun actually hopped out of your mouth instead of taking your head with it, you almost certainly loose quite a few teeth, and get your yaw dislodged.

In short: Don't do that.
