#335709 JM Qwilleran Tue, 5th Feb '13 7:04:58 AM
Regarding the toys, a couple things:

A.) Yes, the plan is to tie the toys much more in with the show. I know at least one person who is disappointed about this (for reasons of his own I won't go into), but on the whole this is good. It generally means stronger sales, and I know that I prefer toys that actually look more like the characters and match up more with the show. I have seen Pony merchandise at Odd Lots, but only the small figure sets that they manufactured en masse of just about every character, and even those eventually sell.
B.) Toy sales always drop off somewhat once the Christmas holiday season is over. It's inevitable. Trust me, Pony sales were huge during the last holiday season.
C.) A number of new toys / exclusives are coming out or have recently come out and those are going like hotcakes. Fluttershy recently came out for the first time as an official plush, in sizes from 5" to as large as 20", and those are selling like hotcakes.
