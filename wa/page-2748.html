As far as focus episodes go, I don't think it's necessary for a character to be the focus in order to be likeable, but the more screentime they get, the the more well rounded the character becomes. They can be entertaining as secondary characters, but they probably won't get a lot of characterization unless they have a fair amount of focus in the episode. A single episode won't make or break a character entirely, but it'll certainly influence the audience's perception of them a lot more than a non-focus episode would.
