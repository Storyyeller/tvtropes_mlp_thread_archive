Quite a few are, although more commonly in the form of Stella Bestia, or "star beasts" in common parlance, such as the infamous Ursas Major and Minor, rather than as ponies. In fact, several of the zodiac signs now assigned pony avatars have representation among the Stella Bestia.

Scorpios, for example, have long been a nuisance to Saddle Arabian trade routes, despite being one of the smaller Stella species at only twice the size of an adult pony. These fascinating nocturnal creatures sleep under the sand during the day, all but completely camouflaged right up until the moment an unfortunate caravan pony trots across a leg or thorax. It is impossible to plot trade routes around them, as they range over exceptionally wide tracts of the desert every night with no telling where they will bed down, and only the most experienced and watchful caravan ponies can pick out their shimmering carapaces from the sand before treading on them. It is only fortunate that they are solitary creatures rather than pack animals like my own constant nemesis/subject, the timberwolf.
