@Applelight: My headcanon is basically that a unicorn horn functions as a natural focus point; sort of like a build-in biological wand. The reason other races can't cast spells is because they don't have a way to focus the energy efficiently. Pegasi have a sort of semi-focus; they can't cast spells, but whenever they do a physical action they subconsciously shove magic out. It's what allows them to fly and move clouds around; whenever they flap their wings their subconsciously manipulating the air via magic to keep themselves aloft. Same for moving clouds.

Earth ponies don't have any focus at all magic just flows through them and into their surroundings constantly, giving them increased physical abilities as well as giving them a connection to their surroundings which allows them to influence it more. I.e. due to the constant flow of magic from an EP they're better able to farm food or manipulate rocks to form geodes for gem mining, etc.
