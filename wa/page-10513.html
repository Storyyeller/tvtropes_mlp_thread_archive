Speaking of store surprises, my story was a happy one, but I read one on Cake Wrecks that was just shocking in how negligent the store people (and whoever bought the "cake") could be. Basically, it's not a real cake, it's a cardboard box with icing on it:

http://cakewrecks.squarespace.com/home/2012/7/31/hey-this-cake-tastes-like-cardboard.html

And then I wondered, what would the MLP characters do if any one of them ordered a cake that was like the messed-up ones on Cake Wrecks? Or if they were given one at a party or something of that nature? I think it would probably depend on the situation and the circumstances of who made the cake and who was ordering it (and how the mistake came about), but I think that Twilight and definitely Rainbow Dash would be the most likely to be very vocal about criticizing the mistakes on it (or if it was funny, Rainbow would probably just make fun of it). Rarity might be upset too, but depending on the situation she might try to be polite and pretend like she liked it anyway (if the baker or the pony giving it to her was someone she didn't want to offend). Pinkie would probably just think it was funny and would probably take a bite and say "See girls, it's still yummy cake!" However, Pinkie would probably hate it if she thought she was getting cake and it was just a box with icing on it (unless it turned out to be a prank and there was a real cake after all).
