Oh God, how many times are people going to be asked to explain this?

I like My Little Pony because it's one of the cutest and funniest pieces of American animation I've seen in years. Which makes me happy because I'm usually off watching anime and hardly ever even feel like giving any stuff that would belong in this type of section any chance. MLP was just so awesome that it did that. I honestly don't see what people don't see in this show, because as soon as I saw it, I put down my anime and picked it up. Or at least, heard it was awesome. I mean, awesome new MLP series? I'm in! Of course, I grew up on the Toei G1 Anime of MLP anyway so I'm not most people.

It reminds me of my inner childhood with it's bright colours and things. I mean, it's no more for kids than Pokemon. It's a bright and beautiful place. Then all the characters are interesting. Then in the animation, everything is drawn really well, or at least most things such as landscape areas are simple, yet beautiful. And the character animation is really amazing, they pay a lot of attention to detail with the eyes and so forth. They really do almost everything that makes anyone who remotely enjoys cute things enjoy the show. I mean, it's a very lovingly crafted cutesy show with a lot of adult appeal and references. Nothing in the show is bland, there's a vibrant variation of personalities which all get their chance to sing and show off in the most subtle of ways in a bright cute world.

She's an all around fun loving character. I've been told lately that humans are pretty instinctual, and I'm thinking there a Pinkie Pie instinct. You just love Pinkie Pie, because you have a human brain. And if you'll notice, amazingly, just for a show originally aimed to sell products, Friendship is Magic has some of the best musical numbers in all of animation. Full stop.
