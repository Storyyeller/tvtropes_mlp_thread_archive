@Story: It depends on where you work. there are bad companies that work you like slaves, but for the most part the industry is really starting to level out. Most companies don't want you to work too much overtime now.

The reason you hear stuff like that is because a lot of beginning designers will work themselves stupid, and end up burning themselves out in a few years because they work like, 12 hour days. So they end up retiring after 5 years because they just can't work anymore.
