#269843 Japanese Teeth Tue, 14th Aug '12 12:31:18 PM from Meinong's jungle
Dredging up topic from 2 pages back: for me at least, I enjoy shipping as a way explore the characterizations more. I usually approach it as less of a "OMG X and Y are so cute together therefore they are OTP!" and more of "I wonder what would take to get X and Y into a romantic relationship and what would happen if they did". So I can say that I ship AJ and Twilight, but it's primarily because I find it really interesting to think about what circumstances would have to arise for them to get into a relationship (while remaining in character, of course) and how they would act once they were in one. That's the main reason I don't really care much about Background Pony ships; there isn't really any established character to play with.

@Perpetual: For Lyra and Bon Bon at least, we know for a fact that the animators are aware of their popularity and intentionally pair them up; the reason I find the Dr/Derpy connection tenuous is that there aren't any other cases where they've been seen interacting (that I can remember, anyway) so it seems less likely to be intentional.
