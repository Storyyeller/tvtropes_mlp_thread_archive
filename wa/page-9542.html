I thought of a pony topic. I know that Applejack's favorite fruit is probably apples, since she farms them and hasn't shown any signs of feeling like she's tired of them (the "I wanted to try cherries for a change" thing in The Last Roundup was more her excuse for working at the cherry orchard because she didn't want to admit she didn't get the prize money). But I was wondering, what would the others' favorite fruits be? This isn't just the Mane Six, this could include others too if you can think of favorite fruits for the others.

Strawberries are often associated with a pink color, at least in candies, so that might be Pinkie Pie's favorite. I kind of associate Rarity with raspberries because they're often seen as a "purple" fruit and because her name sounds a little like raspberry. And Rainbow Dash might like apples best because they come in so many different colors so they're kind of like a rainbow fruit, especially the zap apples they have in canon.

Speaking of the zap apples, I wonder if Equestria has any other unusual types of fruits?

Edit: About the fanon question, probably my least favorite is the Trollestia/Molestia/Tyrantlestia stuff. I don't dislike the idea of Dinky being Derpy's daughter, but I don't personally use it in my own planned fanfic (Derpy doesn't have any biological foals in my story but she's Pinkie's wife and Pinkie has three biological foals so they're also Derpy's kids too). I'm also ambivalent about the "Rainbow Dash is gay" thing. I don't mind stories that have her be a lesbian because of her relationship with another character, but I don't like the idea that she's the designated lesbian pony because of her personality and mane color.
