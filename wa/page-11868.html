#296696 Japanese Teeth Sun, 28th Oct '12 9:28:46 PM from Meinong's jungle
I think the main reason is that our perception of the races are kinda skewed. RD and Twilight are basically top tier in their races' respective abilities (flight and magic), and it tends to be rather important for them. RD is a huge showoff who's life revolves around flying, and Twilight is the personal pupil of the Princess.

The Earth Ponies don't get that sort of opportunity to show off; AJ isn't one to really brag, and Pinkie is explicitly considered weird by the other characters, so people tend to write off her abilities as not being race related. TL;DR pegasi and unicorn powers simply come up a lot more often in the show than earth pony special abilities. Doesn't mean that they aren't useful or strong, just that whatever their abilities are don't make for interesting plotlines.
