#10115 Computer Sherpa Wed, 13th Jul '11 10:34:24 AM from the future from the future
BTW, for some reason I feel that the various pony breeds would work very well in a bog-standard fantasy RPG as balanced "races", and unicorn magic is part of this.
Gonna have to disagree with you there. Unicorns are waaaay overpowered. If (God forbid) there was a war in Equestria, unicorns would curbstomp the other two races until Celestia intervened.

Earth ponies are the best at growing things; that translates into the best industrial capacity of the pony breeds. But without any defensive capability beyond kicking people really hard, their only way to survive would be to ally with one of the other sides and rely on them to keep the farms from burning down.

Pegasi would probably make a name for themselves; air power is a huge advantage in combat. But with telekinesis, unicorns could block/deflect any dropped projectiles, and we simply have no idea as to the limit of their offensive capabilities. As a sample, though, Twilight was able to mass-brainwash a town full of parasprites to change their intended diet, and that took just a few seconds. Granted, Twilight is an unusually powerful unicorn, but I doubt she's totally unique in her talent for magic. Unicorns can sense the location of things they can't see. Unicorns can fire energy beams out of their horns. Unicorns can use tools. In almost any conflict, brief or sustained, I'd bet heavily on unicorn power.

Although...I'm curious as to the degree of control pegasi have over the weather. If they can accurately target lightning bolts, that degree of raw power might give the unicorns a run for their money. Once the unicorns construct lightning rods, however, that ability is largely negated.

To be sure, all of the races will be best off cooperating; the other races will eventually starve without the food the Earth Ponies provide, and using pegasus transportation to deliver a unicorn to the right spot could turn the tide of a battle. Somebody needs to get to work on a strategy game so we can simulate battles between pony nations!
