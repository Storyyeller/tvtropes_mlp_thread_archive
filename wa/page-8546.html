@Psi That's EXACTLY what Golden Balls is all about

4 players. Each are given 4 balls from a pit. 2 on their front row. 2 on their back row. most of the balls have an amount of money written on the inside of them, but 4 of them have KILLER written on the inside instead. Each contestant opens up their front row and reveals what they have. They then take a look at their back row privately. They then all have an open discussion, claiming the amounts that are on their respective back rows (truthfully or not). They then all vote. The object is to try and deduce who has the most killer balls and vote that person out.

It's all about manipulation and deduction
