Trolling's definition is too wide to make a blanket statement. At its most basic, trolling is doing something for the sole purpose of getting a reaction. That reaction can be a pleasant one (i.e. Tara's "twolling", as she calls it) or an angry/disgusted one (i.e. the average internet troll). It's not inherently a negative thing, and it's not about who does it, but how they do it.
