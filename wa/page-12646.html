Might as well leave my feelings on it here:

MMDW: Some serious issues with the characters and setting being inconsistent with previous portrayals. I don't think any characters were horribly, blatantly OOC, but it just doesn't feel like it's on the same page as other episodes.

HWE: I don't really have any major issues with this one, even with the backstage stuff. It doesn't stick out to me as "OMG AWESOME", but there are no major issues either.
