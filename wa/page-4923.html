My Theory on the gods is that they have a sort of higher consciousness, but are not infallible to any degree. Whether that higher consciousness is due to their birth or a matter of experience is sort of out the window, since the youngest of them is still several thousand years old, so it's pretty much impossible to tell. The only thing known for sure is that their more tuned to magic than ordinary ponies. Possibly this is what helps them attain their higher consciousness.

For the sake of definition, what I mean by 'higher consciousness' is their brains are basically several times more advanced than another ponies. Celestia can effectively live out several weeks worth of actions, almost pinpoint-perfectly predicting other ponies based on what she's observed, in a matter of seconds. It's what allows for her batman gambitting - She's perceptive enoug that she literally can predict what other ponies will do. When gods try to out-plan another it comes down to who can be the least predictable and still do something constructive.
