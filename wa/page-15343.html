Taking a page from Twilight, Pinkie consults a schedule called the Pinkie Party Planner to see if there were any parties that day. She has a mini freak out that she couldn't find a reason to make one and goes outside for inspiration.

Noticing that the weather is fresh and lovely after a recent Winter Wrap Up, she decides to throw the Spring-Sproing-Spring Party to celebrate the season.

She brings a pair of bouncing shoes that let her touch the clouds and bounces down to the market. Defaulting to her normal method of getting attention, she improvises a song describing the party.

The CMC overhear her singing and offer to help, in hopes of getting a mark in party-planning. She pulls out a few pairs of bouncy shoes from nowhere and tasks them with spreading the word.

The shoes provide two things. An excuse as to how Pinkie could follow Rainbow up to the clouds. And it's a way for Scootaloo to reach Cloudsdale.
