Waitwaitwaitaminute.

There's a My Little Pony RPG?!?!?!?!!!!!111!

Holy Clop, I've so got to get one of my friends to run this thing.

There have been many Pony RPG attempts. I think we even have a thread over in Tabletop.

My major beef with most of them is that while they are all very cool systems, they are still focused on combat as the main source of conflict. (The old RPG curse.) RP Gs grew out of wargames and this is something most R Pers can't wrap their head around: there are types of storytelling where this becomes a drawback. To me, any game where I need to calculate how much injury my pony can take before she dies is clearly missing the point of Friendship Is Magic.
