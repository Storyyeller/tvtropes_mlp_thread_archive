Pros-

  • Animation improved in all areas, even in the backgrounds! Easter eggs were put in there for the fans!
  • Derpy had a scene with lines!
  • More new, varied, colorful characters debuted.
  • Stories did not solely concern one of the Main Six all the time, and focus was put more on the side characters. The CMC hardly ever sought to earn their Cutie Marks.
  • Music improved and was more varied too.
  • Continuity was preserved in this season, even while dodging Hasbro's insistence for the show to be episodic!
  • ***** DISCORD!!!

Cons-

  • No more Luna after Luna Eclipsed.
  • Lessons were told to the audience more conventionally as if the characters are reading from a script, rather than always writing to Celestia as justification.
  • Hasbro edited Derpy's scene after it premiered.
  • Writing has not always been good (all the ponies becoming Jerks for some reason in Putting Your Hoof Down, The Mysterious Mare Do Well, etc.), but for the most part, the quality matches that of the first season.
  • Not enough Fluttershy, although the next episode will focus on her.
  • Not a lot of memorable songs.
