YO FALLEN WHADDUP?

Here are some basic anatomy issues you can correct:

  • Ear is too low
  • Neck is too narrow (a common mistake when it comes to making animé-esque characters)
  • She only has one arm :P
  • The wing is not drawn properly. Dragon wings◊ Bird wings◊
  • Hand is too small
  • The dress seems artificially extended. Did you draw a body underneath the dress? That is always advised, so people don't look all wonky and misshapen. You can do this with pencil or pen
