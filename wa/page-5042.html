#126030 Cysma Thu, 15th Mar '12 12:32:09 AM
If anyone's been wondering where I've been, I've been doing an Archive Binge of Cracked's articles instead. Haven't read anything since January. Also...

https://www.youtube.com/user/BronyDubs4Life

You've probably seen a gag dub or an abridged series of FIM before, but The Brony Edition sets itself apart in three ways: it is not abridged because they dub entire episodes with male voices, the humor is improvised on the spot, often with hilarious results, and this series more or less keeps in the spirit of the show itself. As of this post they have dubbed the first 23 episodes of season 1 as well as both parts of Return of Harmony. The group also does a variety of other things: They have a miniseries called Sleepover at Fluttershy's, where we witness the ponies taking turns playing various video games and doing dramatic readings of bad fan fiction. We also have the beginnings of Twilight Sparkle doing a Let's Play of The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword, parodies of various songs performed by the cast, and more.

I've been following the crew since they only had 4 or 5 videos uploaded, and later on they started hosting livestreams every fortnight or so, wherein they do Q&A's as the characters in the dub, play various video games (including Mario Kart Wii and Super Smash Bros. Brawl so you can play with them online!), and stream various movies with a set theme each session. They've done a bad movie night, a Disney movie night, a Studio Ghibli movie night, a Back to the Future trilogy night...

They've got more lined up in the future as well. During one livestream, I was given the privilege of becoming an honorary crew member to help with their plans of creating a My Little Pony musical. I made a Pinkie Pie Promise not to divulge the details (my eye still hurts!), but from the summary and the script written so far, it's going to be SO AWESOME! When they get done with that, they plan on doing more dubs as well, including a not-just-audio play for a ponified version of Back to the Future, starring Scooty Mc Fly, Doc Pie, and Gilda Tannen. I'm not yet sure how I'll be contributing to the musical yet, but I figured that getting the word out is the least I can do for now.
