#295440 Japanese Teeth Tue, 23rd Oct '12 12:52:40 PM from Meinong's jungle
@Mio: That's not quite what I mean. That's exaggerating the attributes of the characters as they appear in the show. I'm thinking more of "how would the general character concepts have been mishandled by lesser writers"? Like Rarity is basically "The fashion pony". In MLP:FIM we get a designer who runs her own business, whereas in a show with less depth we'd probably have gotten a Rich Bitch Valley Girl Shopaholic. Rainbow Dash is like the "sports" pony. Here we get someone who has a dream and actively works to achieve it, whereas she could have just been a Jerk Jock, etc. Basically reducing the characters to stereotypes and seeing how the canon characters react to them. HWE was more taking the existing characters and exaggerating existing traits, whereas this would be more stripping traits out until only an archetype is left.

Pretty much every meme likely has somebody who's still utilizing it. It's a dead meme in the sense that it doesn't dominate the fandom anymore, but it's still out there.
