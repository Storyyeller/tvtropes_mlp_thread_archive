See, Spike wanted to go waterskiing in Australia (he found out about it while in the Equestria Girls human world) but he thought that because it was founded by convicts, he was supposed to wear a black and white striped stereotypical burglar outfit and stuck a toy iron ball to his leg. While waterskiing, Percival Mc Leach (the villain from the movie I mentioned, he's a poacher) wanted to capture the baby dragon for his collection. Spike fought him off with his fire breath, burning Mc Leach's hat and melting his gun. Mc Leach ran away to go get some other weapons, and dropped his hat, which Spike took for himself because he thought it was cool. Unfortunately, Mc Leach then used a bow and arrow and missed Spike but hit the hat. Spike then had to escape through a version of the portal that was in Australia, but he lost one of his water skis and ended up getting his inner tube stuck over his head. He then put the hat back on and Pinkie found him and he requested that she give him a snow boot for his other foot (the one that didn't have a water ski) because it was winter in Equestria. She thought his mismatched look was funny and added the lipstick mark because she thought it would be a funny face decoration (not from her own lips, but by drawing on his face with lipstick). And then he showed up to meet Twilight looking like he does at the end of the strip.

The main reason I thought of a crossover with The Rescuers Down Under is because I thought that the hat he was wearing looked like Mc Leach's hat.
