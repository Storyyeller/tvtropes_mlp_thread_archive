#351845 Calnos Fri, 29th Mar '13 10:50:41 AM from Nowhere
Lauren Faust once said they try not to hammer logic too hard in the show back when she was still in charge, and so I think its best we don't to think too hard either. The truth is this isn't a long running comic book, anime, or novel series with tons of hard rules with how the universe works. Its not realistic in the slightest. Its a fun and silly cartoon with the occasional heavier plot that delves into the mythology, and that's it.

Celestia moves the sun. How? Magic. It doesn't really matter how she does it or the science involved in making it happen because this isn't a hard sci-fi series where physics matter, except Cartoon Physics.

And that's all I really have to say about that. Its just a silly cartoon about ponies and FRIENDSHIP that we all enjoy.

Someone brought up the character pages a while back, and why we have several separate pages. Loads and Loads of Characters and Trope Overdosed are part of the reason, but I think it helps to understand the Characters page was split when all the subpages got hit real hard with vandalism during the first season break. What were folders became pages. We finally got around to rehauling and reorganizing the pages (we used to have a "Celebronies" page, which was ill-defined and axed in favor of a page for characters that only appear in one or two episodes).

We also seem to have more people who contribute to pony pages than other, bigger shows, and not all of contributors post in this thread. There's me, Whatshisgame, Dragon Ranger, and...who else is a regular Wiki Curator that posts here? Or am I just not checking the history pages enough?

The Mane Cast all having their own pages is a tad extreme, but if there's enough tropes... the only other character that has a page to themselves I can think of is Commander Shepard. Technically also The Doctor, but there's a reason for that.

I'm still wondering how Background Ponies page got to where it is, and that's probably the character section I've contributed the most to!

We do have a problem with trope shoehorning/misuse when it comes to pony pages, but there is a reason we have a bunch of character pages. Its just one of those shows.
