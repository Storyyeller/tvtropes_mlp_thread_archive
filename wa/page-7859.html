I was thinking about the G1 series and I realized something that might be a callback or at least an interesting similarity between the the "Escape from Catrina" movie and the G4 series. Near the beginning of "Escape from Catrina, " Megan sings a "lullaby" to the baby ponies that's basically saying "let's stay up and NOT go to sleep" which of course gets the baby ponies tired and sleepy anyway. Fast forward to the G4 series, and here we have Sweetie Belle singing a noisy song with lyrics like "Hush now, quiet now, it's time to go to bed" that definitely has some Lyrical Dissonance going on with the way she sings it so loudly. So in G1 we have a song that says "don't go to sleep" that puts ponies to sleep and in G4 we have a song that says "go to sleep" that wakes everyone up!
