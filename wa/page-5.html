#122 suntzu Tue, 8th Feb '11 1:43:44 AM
So while we're waiting for the next episode, I ask. What was the original "My Little Pony" like?

It was a semi-permanent Sugar Apocalypse - on the one hand, you had these cute, girly ponies residing in a pink manor and living the good life, but on the other hand they were constantly being threatened by half the cast of the Monster Manual.

It wasn't the deepest or the smartest thing on TV, but it certainly wasn't worse than most. All in all, I consider it one of the upper-tiers cartoons of the 80s, in the same category as GI Joe or Transformers.
