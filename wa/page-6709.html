#167721 Kyler Thatch Sun, 22nd Apr '12 7:34:48 AM from the bottom of my heart
Impressions...

the Cold Open waas kind of awkward. Her brother's getting married, the normal response would be either elation or surprise. Not looking like she's been deeply insulted. So Twilight's reaction being just that is kind of a jolt, without an immediate follow-up. Granted, we get an explanation as soon as the next scene starts, but... yeah.

Some of the song numbers start kind of awkwardly, I think. I mean, I'm a bit of a fan of musical numbers, so it's not like I have an aversion to characters suddenly breaking into song. But sometimes the way it starts up feels kind of weird. I don't know why.

The fight with the changelings was pretty well-done. First off, it puts the battle-of-the-clones scene in context, which is a relief considering the previews had me pretty worried. Fluttershy stays true to character and doesn't actually fight, instead opting for a pretty impressive Bluff check. Finally, I like the twist where instead of pulling some sort of Big Damn Heroes moment, they actually fail and get captured instead.

And then there's all the interaction between Twilight and Shining Armor, and Twilight and (fake) Cadence, and the falling out between Twilight and Shining Armor, and then Twilight finding the real Cadence. In other words, it's got all the character-based drama that I pretty much love to pieces.
