@Wryte:

Checkers is a fast-food chain known for its multitude of delicious and affordable items and its fries. ESPECIALLY its fries. They're the only fast-food company I know of whose largest size of fry order is a large cup. The only downside is that the burgers are very high in calories, but they are SO fucking delicious. My favorite from there is the Bacon Roadhouse Burger, where they toast roadhouse bread and stick two cheesy patties, deep-fried onions, bacon, and sauce all on it.

Five Guys is also getting fairly well-known recently. They're gaining recognition for using all-natural beef and potatoes, as well as the ridiculous amount of ways you can customize your burger (find me any other place that allows you to put A-1 sauce on a two-patty cheeseburger). Their fries are also notable in that they are prepared boardwalk-style (firm on the outside, soft on the inside) with peanut oil, and they also provide complimentary peanuts for all their customers. They also put a greater emphasis on fry servings than most others, in that they will put an extra scoop of fries in the bag with whatever fry order you got, AND complimentary ketchup and/or malt vinegar for them.
