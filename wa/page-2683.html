There are two basic systems of elements. The first was Greek in origin and probably the best well known. Fire, Earth, Water, Air. If you've ever watched W.I.T.C.H. Captain Planet or Avatar you're familiar with these. A fifth element is sometimes added, often something like Aether or Quintessence.

Then you have the eastern elemental system which has five elements. Water, Earth, Metal, Wood, Fire. These are much less common in TV land. I remember the five Foot mystics from Ninja Turtles had this and I think it applies to Sailor Moon as well.
