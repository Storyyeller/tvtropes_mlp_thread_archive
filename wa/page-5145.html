Well, let's have some context here.

Rainbow Dash can fly fast enough to break the sound barrier - she has been stated to be one of the only pegasi in history to do this, and she did it as a child.

In context, Twilight really isn't that overpowered. Everypony else is obscenely talented in one way or another, hers is just the flashiest.
