So, does anyone here play Magic: the Gathering? For those who do, what color of magic do you think the Mane 6 would represent? Here's my thoughts:

Twilight Sparkle: Definitely Blue, as she is all about knowledge and learning.

Rainbow Dash: Red. She's hasty, rash, impulsive, and hot headed. That covers most of Red ideals.

Pinkie Pie: Also Red. The other half of Red is Chaos and randomness, so Pinkie fits the bill.

Applejack: Green. She's strong, has earthly wisdom, and is very nurturing.

Fluttershy: Green/White. She also has a strong affinity for nature, but is very focused on getting along with the animals rather than being stronger than them and rounding them up.

Rarity: Black/White. She is very ambitious and materialistic, but overcomes that for the sake of her friends and wants to share her gifts and talents rather than hoarding them for herself.
