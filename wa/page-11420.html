#285488 kegisak Fri, 21st Sep '12 2:08:54 PM
My personal fanon on the finality of cutie marks is that, since cutie marks rely on the ponies personal sense of meaning, or their 'purpose' in life, it's possible for a mark to change, but it takes a HUGE epiphany, often accompanied by a big change in personality. But even then, it's also very likely that they could simply apply that meaning to their mark in a different way - ie, for those of you who follow the Hoops/Dumbbell blog Dumbbell before coming out of the closet determined his purpose is 'to be strong and cool'. After coming out it was 'to give children someone to look up to, and to lead them right. Both feature the dumbbell cutie mark because he still desires strength, he just changed his definition of strength. Most times the mark changes it was largely a 'filler' mark anyways; something they never really thought about, a sort of given purpose rather than obtained one.
