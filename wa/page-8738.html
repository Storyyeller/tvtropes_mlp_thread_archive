@story every member of both teams is given a total of 400 million yen each by the game officials at the start of the game. 300 is in the ATM for smuggling and 100 million is in their "third country" account. The "winning" team of the game is determined by which team has the most collective money in their "third country" accounts at the end of 50 rounds.
