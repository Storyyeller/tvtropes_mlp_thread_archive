I'm still with you there, Ginger... But I think the EP's may actually, on retrospect, have a bit of an advantage. I mean first of all, they have a huge boost out the start - Even a few extra stat points is a big bonus, as is a free skill out of the gates. Granted they don't start off with a racial like Pegasi and Unicorns, but they still have racial skills - they just get them later. So they have, in essence, everything the other races do and more, they just take a while to get started. Ironically, they're the Quadratic Wizard in a way. They have less opportunities than the other races, but they're much more likely to excel in their path of choice, particularly if they choose a path related to their racials.

Of course on the whole, it's very likely that the different racials are going to lean more towards one 'class' than others. Since the unicorns are the only magic race, of course a unicorn is more likely to become a magical class. Although a unicorn in a purely physical class would be pretty funny.
