#388866 Wryte Wed, 7th Aug '13 8:07:10 PM
I think EQG could have been massively improved by a few minor tweaks.

  • Sunset had never run for prom queen before because she never cared. Rarity was the reigning queen until Sunset needed to win to get the magic crown, giving Sunset clear motivation for sabotaging the alt!Six's relationships and dividing the school's cliques. Her scheme to break them up could also use some improvement.
  • Cut the romance angle from Twilight and Flash's relationship, and refocus it on his former relationship with Sunset.
  • Instead of trying to frame Twilight for trashing the gym, Sunset straight up tries to steal the crown during the dance. Flash catches her based on Flash's understanding of her from their relationship, and Sunset accuses him of falling for Twilight. He points out that he dumped her because she let her jealousy take over, and almost manages to talk her down, but she throws him aside when Twilight is crowned during their conversation.
  • At the end, it's left open-ended whether Flash might get back together with Sunset or really has fallen for Twilight.

The rest of the movie plays out more or less the same. These tweaks make Sunset a more focused and competent villain and give Flash an actual purpose for existing, which were two of my biggest problems with the movie. On the whole I think it's worth watching for fans, but I'm glad I didn't pay to see it in a theater.
