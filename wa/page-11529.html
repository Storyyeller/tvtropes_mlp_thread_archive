@Apple: Mm. Well, the first couple of things I can point out are that, at that angle, we wouldn't see any of his right side(left from out perspective). The line of the body would be flush with his neck and right from hoof. As it stand it appears like his body is facing us head on - and that he's very fat at that, since horses legs are on the side of their bodies, he'd have to be very large in order for his sides to go past them. And while real-world anatomy doesn't always apply to ponies, any horse that fat would be unable to stand up. Because it's legs would break.

Beyond that the only big problem is with his legs, front and back. The front legs I've already noted, they look like they're coming from the inside of his body, but the hind legs have other issues. The big one is that his left knee appears far too thin.

I also have a bit of trouble with his wings - you look like you've put them more on his shoulders, while in the show they tend to be around the center of the body. and this is more a personal preferences but I like my wings to be more realistic, which would mean their base spanning most of the length of the body in order to catch enough air. But as I said, that's a personal choice.

On the whole though, It looks very good! I really like his face, that sort of smug bastard look hes got going on, and the new hairstyle looks great! I have to do a bit of work right now, send a resume and a couple emails, but once I'm done that if you'd like I might be able to do a couple quick sketches of what his limbs might look like a bit better?
