On the topic of the rock farm:

Pinkie's parents were originally very successful farmers before the Parasprites came. After the Parasprites ate all of their crops, the Pies had to sell rocks until they could get enough money to sell seeds again. Pinkie is the only one of her siblings to remember before the Parasprites came, hence her depressed nature. Seeing the Rainboom gave her enough hope to try and plan a party, but she used the money that the family was saving to do it. When they found out, Pinkie was promptly exiled. This moment was very traumatic for her and comes back to her whenever she feels alone.
