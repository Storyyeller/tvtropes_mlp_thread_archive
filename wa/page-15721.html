#393011 kegisak Fri, 23rd Aug '13 4:27:47 PM
So, just read the CMC microcomic.

The story was nice enough. Nothing to write home about, really - the kids were cute enough, and the plot was fairly neat. The Mimick is a pretty cool addition to the MLP universe, and it'll be neat to see what's done with it in the fandom, is anything, but on the whole nothing about the story really jumped out. It was nice, and cute, but not terribly substantial.

The art though, was fantastic! The sketchiness really lends itself well to the dynamic style and poses of the comic, and I expecially loved the special effects of it - the glows and shines when Rarity used her magic, and when the Mimick changed forms. IT was absolutely gorgeous to look at, and the panels were extremely well arranged, which is a fairly small detail, but you can really notice when it's not done well(I'm looking at YOU, Suicide Squad).

On the whole, certainly not one of the better microseries comics, but still very enjoyable.
