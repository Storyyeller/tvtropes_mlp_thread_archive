#94197 kegisak Sun, 12th Feb '12 9:23:46 PM
So... by way of apology for an empty post, my ideas for The Wooden Mask and The Gardens of Equestria:

The Wooden Mask has an inscription on the inside that translates to, "The masks we wear show our true face". It's a mask of shadows - the true self. It allows the wearer to see the true self of those it beholds in the form of shadows, lurking behind them and speaking their thoughts at all times. However, the more you wear the mask the more your own shadow comes out - you start to act on basic impulses, losing you inhibitions. Once you're acting purely on your shadow it start to warp you, turning you into a caricature of yourself and even corrupting your body to resemble the shadow. Eventually, you become nothing more than a shadow yourself, and burn away in the light - because while a show is what is behind the mask, it is not the whole truth. It cannot exist without a mask to hide behind. The mask was created by a zebra priest at the command of a pony king who once conquered the marengeti in ancient times. He was commanded to create something that would allow the king to see any who opposed him. In the end, the kind was his own greatest foe. He acted only out of his own selfishness, with no regard for the rest of the world - no mask to hide his shadow. Though I may want to twist that to have something to do with the idea of The Gardens of Equestria...

In The Gardens of Equestria, there isn't any real treasure - the treasure is the gardens themselves. They're the fertile fields, the origin of the earth pony tribe. Possibly the origin of all pony kind, in fact. It's the wellspring from which all earth pony magic flows, the source of all earthly magic. The Gardener is a massive, immortal earth pony, with flesh like solid stone - Daring thinks he's a statue at first. He was the first acolyte of king steady, the first Earth Pony King. He charged the gardener with protecting the secrets of the garden before he died. The Garden of Equestria would theoretically be the first notion of a larger plot involving the four tribes and their kings - and the artifacts of their kings. Running with the idea I had before where each of the artifacts has a power and belonged to one of the four kings. Each of them would have their own home, I suppose. I'd give my opinion, but other people have claim and I don't want to tread on any toes. But anyways, yeah. The Gardens of Equestria are the home of the earth ponies, and the source of all earth magic. In fact the earth magic is so strong it interferes with other magic - including Do's ability to fly.
