Also, I suppose the fact that she gave him what he had been searching for would also be a major factor in him deciding he at least sort of likes her. It is rather odd to me that he suddenly said they were "friends, " however, given the enormous amount of ponies who Pinkie considers to be her "friends, " it may just be more what the average person would consider to be a friendly acquaintance.
