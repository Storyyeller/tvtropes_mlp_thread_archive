Custom ponies are pretty easy to do. You just buy a pony, pop the head off... pull out the mane and tail and then recolor the body using either RIT dye or paint. I have an airbrush but a can of spray paint will work about the same and it comes in a bazillion colors nowadays.

The hardest part is probably putting the hair back in, but there are tutorials on how to do that.

Does end up costing a little but if you want a pony version of yourself that isn't a drawing, that's the best way to go!
