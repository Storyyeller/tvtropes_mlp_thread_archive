Last Thursday, I adopted a new Pomeranian from a rescue shelter and named her Lily, after Harry's mother from Harry Potter, which makes her name kind of a pair with Luna the other Pomeranian. So what does this have to do with My Little Pony? Because Luna and Lily are ALSO names of pony characters. Interestingly enough, while Luna the dog doesn't act much like Princess Luna (she acts like a somewhat calmer version of Pinkie Pie—the "Smile" song would fit her perfectly!), Lily is rather shy and easily scared of new people and sudden noises, although she can be very sweet and playful when she gets to trust you. Now who in My Little Pony is easily frightened, other than Fluttershy? None other than Lily, one of the "Flower Trio." I just realized the similarity between the dog Lily and the pony Lily today, and thought I'd share it here.
