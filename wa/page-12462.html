Thinking of the bodyswapping "Pinkie with magic" idea made me think of something. As their characters currently are, Rainbow Dash and Twilight both are very invested in the powers they have as a pegasus and a unicorn, respectively. It made me wonder, what would the cast members lives' or characters be like if they were different pony types? I know that Fluttershy as an Earth pony and Pinkie as a pegasus were the original ideas for those characters, but what about the others? Applejack as a pegasus might not be that different, but what about her as a unicorn? Or Rainbow Dash as a unicorn/Earth pony? Twilight as a pegasus or an Earth pony? Some guesses:

  • Twilight would be very studious about gardening or something like that as an Earth pony, and she would still study a lot. As a pegasus, she would probably spend a lot of time calculating weather patterns and be somewhat of the record keeper or the mathematician of the weather team.
  • Rainbow Dash as a unicorn would probably be the type to focus on spells that look flashy rather than things that might be Boring, but Practical. Her as an Earth pony wouldn't be that different than the way she is already, except she would focus on running speed and agility rather than flying, of course.
  • Rarity as a pegasus might be the type to focus on being the most graceful flyer ever, and if she was ever asked to help with the weather making, she would probably take to that like she takes to making fashion in the show—she might end up focusing more on how the weather should look rather than its function. Applejack, of course, would be the opposite if she was a pegasus.
