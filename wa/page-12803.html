#320053 Perpetual Lurker Fri, 21st Dec '12 9:20:59 PM
So, random thought. Have you ever noticed that the adorable little buzzing Scootaloo does with her wings is entirely unique to her? Every other pegasus we've seen, including foals like Rumble, Featherweight, and even Pound Cake flap their wings at a much slower, steady pace when flying unless they're really straining themselves (like those slow pegasi in the obstacle course in Wonderbolts Academy).

Thinking about it, it actually gives a good bit of insight into just what her flight deficiency is. Basically, her attempts at flying have her pushing her wings as hard as they will go, and she still can't manage any lift. If there's any sort of evidence for her being disabled rather than simply unskilled, that would be it, since it implies a physical defect of her wings rather than a technical deficiency. Of course, it's just a tiny detail and I'm definitely over thinking it, but its food for thought regarding the possibilities for her future development.
