#51998 Kyler Thatch Sat, 10th Dec '11 10:57:51 AM
I kind of see where he might be coming from (and feel free to correct me if I'm wrong, ginger).

Obviously someone in the world has to be #1. But Rainbow Dash is a primary character in this series, someone we pay close attention to. Someone our stories center around. And often, given the premise of the show, our focus is one someone's personal growth. Once you've reached the top, there's nowhere higher to go, really.

It's not that Rainbow Dash should be physically unable to be the best flier in all of Equestria, it's just that you have fewer options for potentially interesting stories once she's already at the top.

That said, aiming to be the best flier is far from Rainbow Dash's sole source of character-driven conflict. Beyond that, there's still the fact that she's often rude and selfish, and we've seen her struck with insecurity.
