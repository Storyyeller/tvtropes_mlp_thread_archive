Storyyeller: Build-A-Bear Workshop not only makes bears. But they make plush toys like Rabbits, Dogs, Cats etc. They also do special limited plush toys to celebrate a new kids movie that came out. Like Alvin and the Chipmunks, and The Smurfs. During holidays such as christmas, they would do holiday theme plush toys by having the plush toys dressed up as Santa or an elf. But they also do plush toys like Rudolph the Red Nosed Reindeer and other characters from the Rankin Bass holiday special.

Edit: Build-A-Bear Workshop is mostly seen in Shopping Malls. But in some states or countries, they have their own store.
