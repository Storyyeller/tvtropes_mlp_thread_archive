#259684 Kuroi Tsubasa Tenshi Fri, 27th Jul '12 10:19:02 AM from Canada
Echo: That was a knowledge failure on Hasbro's part. Derpy in general is fairly non-politically correct (unsurprising given her origins started with a group on 4chan). I always predicted that if they made any sort of reference to fanon things would blow up. Honestly, it was a total publicity mess from the very start.

I think a big problem of the fandom is that we are overhyped. There's this big pedestal type of thing going on. We're held up as some kind of big open community that will always love and tolerate. Thing is, by nature of being a generalization, it's not true. Yes, our public face is noticeably more open than many other groups out there, but here's the caveat: we're still human. We have opinions, get angry, have disagreements, etc. I don't know where the expectation came from that this fandom is perfect, but that's an impossibility and it sets up newcomers for disappointment. On the flipside, just because there are counterexamples to this hype doesn't mean the opposite generalization is true. We're not all like the counterexamples.
