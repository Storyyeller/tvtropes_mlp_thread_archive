Apparently this was supposed to be the Season Finale. It was apparently written, storyboarded, animated, voiced, and printed during that season's production, but was held back in exchange for "Party of One" and "Best Night Ever."

Perhaps this was meant to be the Grand Finale?

The show begins with Twilight going to Ponyville, making some friends, and defeating a villain. The next twenty-two episodes focus on the building relationships between the new group of friends while handling smaller crises such as the Ursa Minor or the dragon, ending with the ponies going to the Gala. Then, an extremely powerful villain appears and uses the same character development against the ponies, turning them against each other by exploiting their weaknesses and breaking their friendship apart. Only in the end, they manage to get back together and defeat him, seal him back up, and go back to Ponyville closer than ever.

The last few My Little Pony shows were only marginal successes, with only the first G1 cartoon receiving any real acclaim from anyone. So, it was obvious going in that this show probably would receive the same lukewarm reception. When it became a huge hit, the executives ordered that a second season begin production. Thus, "Return to Harmony" was shelved as the Season Two premiere (giving the staff more of a buffer and allowing the season to start a few weeks earlier than expected) and added two "normal" episodes to fill out the twenty-six ordered for the first season.
