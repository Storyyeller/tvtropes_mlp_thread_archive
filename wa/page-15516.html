#387885 Wryte Sun, 4th Aug '13 4:41:26 PM
I liked EQG more than I expected to. I didn't like the character designs going into it, but they grew on me, though a few could have benefited greatly from some tweaking. Applejack wearing a miniskirt is probably the one that gets most under my skin.

The plot was nothing spectacular. The conflicts were each resolved almost as soon as they were introduced, and some of them never even should have been problems to begin with. Flash Sentry was completely pointless, and his screen time was badly needed for other aspects of the movie, like making Sunset a more competent villain or giving RD an actual part.

That said, most of the humanizations were pretty good, and it was fun trying to identify all the background characters as they went by. Most of the humor was solid, especially Twilight's attempts to understand the human world and Pinkie's casual fourth wall painting, and there were plenty of fandom nods to try and catch.

Overall, worth a rental, not a purchase.
