Well that episode was awesome. And the first episode of the entire series that left me in suspense, since it's not clear whether Rainbow is a Wonderbolt or not now. I think she's still in training but it was kind of vague.

Lightning Dust was a great character. It's too bad the show axed her at the end, just like Gilda. Kind of ridiculous the lengths they go to to make sure that the Mane 6 are Rainbow's only friends, but that's my only complaint.

Rainbow getting and explaining the point of the episode was great, and she got plenty of awesome moments to shine. Best move of the episode: wing slicing clouds, complete with anime-style delayed collapse. I actually thought the show was building up to a reveal that Spitfire made Rainbow a Wingpony specifically because she thought she'd do well to curb Lightning Dust's reckless tendencies, which I think would have been a better plot (and would have made Spitfire out to be a lot more intelligent) but whatever, what we got was great anyway.

This was one of those rare episodes where I didn't find Pinkie annoying at all. She was hilarious, she acted like an anxious girlfriend all episode (soooooooo much shipping fuel, I don't even care about it but I couldn't help but notice).

Basically, everything was good A+. I just wish there'd been a bit more time to clarify things at the end.

I want to know how long Dash is gonna be in training. Dare we hope that she'll become a full Wonderbolt in Season 4?
