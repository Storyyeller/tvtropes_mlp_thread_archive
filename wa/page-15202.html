@Apple: It's just a matter of finding the right length. I had this problem a lot where I would draw the legs shorter than the body, making it look all wrong.

Here's a pretty decent proportions guide. It uses the 8 head format for simplicity. The crotch is kind of the mid point, so if it's about 4 heads from the top of the head to the crotch, it should be about four from the crotch to the soles of the feet. You don't have to use exactly 8 heads once you're used to the idea.
