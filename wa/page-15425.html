Preset? There is no preset, only Constructed and Limited, and Constructed is only expensive once if you play Vintage/Legacy. Limited is perfectly affordable (a buncha boosters per Limited round) and you get some nice tradebait from it sometimes. The thing about Magic that makes it so flexible is the ever-evolving rules. I haven't even built any post-2008 decks with Planeswalkers in yet...

I kinda miss the old Extended, but I guess Modern is better. Built a cute Spellbomb Control deck (which had some serious old-school 3-Colour Control vibes straight out of Ancient Times circa pre-Urza's Brokenness) that actually shut out a bunch of Combo Decks I faced and even pinned a Control deck back in the Grand Prix. It also happens to be fast enough to kill milling decks somehow

As for decknames, either they go with the tribal theme (Goblins, Affinity, Elves), come up with a neat thematic name based on how the deck plays (Mill decks, Red Deck Wins, Stompy, Stax), or cute nicknames based on strategy (Omelette/Sunny Side Up, a fun recursion deck with Baubles galore that exploits Second Sunrise to pull them out of the Graveyard repeatedly; Prosbloom, the Prosperity/Cadevorous Bloom combo deck), or they're named for the Power Cards that make them work (Winter Orb, Tooth and Nail, Necro, Charbelcher).
