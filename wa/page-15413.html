@G3 discussion: Just to note, Star Catcher is not a Winged Unicorn, she's a pegasus who has some mystical qualities, but she doesn't have a horn.

I was thinking about how the three main movies would adapt to the G4 universe. I'm not really sure how the Runaway Rainbow story would adapt, considering that the way rainbows are made in both versions is different (the unicorns create them in G3 and the pegasi create them in the weather factory in G4), unless one simply kept the general idea of a young unicorn trying to learn how to use her powers who was easily distracted and ended up getting lost accidentally. This would probably work with Twilight or Rarity in Cheerilee's role and Sweetie Belle in G3 Rarity's role. Here's my ideas for the other two main movies:

  • For A Very Minty Christmas, Minty's role is taken by Derpy, and instead of it being a candy cane to guide Santa, it's something that's supposed to bring Celestia or a gift for her that Derpy breaks. Something where Celestia is coming to Ponyville for a special Hearth's Warming Eve visit, and a big present is made for her that Derpy accidentally breaks and then tries to bring a substitute to her or fix it. (although this might not work because Celestia wouldn't need to find Ponyville because she visits there already and thus wouldn't need a beacon to find it) And then the gift is that in the process of fixing the gift, the ponies of the three different races bond together and the gift to Celestia ends up being that she is very pleased to see them showing the true spirit of Hearth's Warming Eve. Or maybe they were going to put on a Ponyville play of the Hearth's Warming Eve story and Derpy breaks something that was supposed to represent an important artifact so she tries to go fly to Canterlot to get the original artifact to replace it.

  • A Princess Promenade: Twilight has just become an alicorn Princess, and Rarity takes on Heathspike's G3 role of trying to make Twilight be a proper princess. Two possible endings for this. One is that because Twilight actually IS more familiar with Celestia, Luna and Cadance, she points out to Rarity that the other princesses don't necessarily fit Rarity's fairytale idea of what a princess is like (such as "princesses don't raise their voice" but Luna used the booming Royal Canterlot Voice when she visited Ponyville). And then the message is that there is more than one way to be a princess (which would fit Lauren Faust's original idea for the show having the message that there are many different ways to be a girl). The other would be more like the original G3 special where Twilight creates some new magic using that Star Swirl the Bearded book and turns everyone in Ponyville into alicorns. Including Spike, who gains a magic horn and dragon wings.
