#210587 Ace Of Scarabs Fri, 1st Jun '12 10:14:06 AM from Singapore
@ccoa: Mostly it's popular because of a combination of factors. It begins with a bunch of article writers bashing Lauren for "selling out" and making the show for Hasbro, luring some Anons from /co/ to watch and see what the fuss is all about. They loved it, drew in more anons, got the attention of the Internet in general, and within a few months, bronies had practically become an Internet Demographic (and one a lot more tolerable than the rabid fandumbs seen elsewhere!) and ponies re-entered the mainstream. Between the refreshingly three-dimensional Mane Six and the astonishingly consistent world, the excellent dialogue and entertaining humor, the show also has a bunch of seriously better-than-so-called-kid's-shows plot points and jokes and plenty of life lessons that adults could do well to learn or re-learn.

Well, at least like their distant relatives the Transformers, ponies are also originally merch-driven, only now Lauren has made the show good on a level that rivals Beast Wars and Transformers Animated.
