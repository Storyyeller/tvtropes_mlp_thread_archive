Dream started out with me waking up in a house that looked kinda like one I used to live in but haven't seen in years. I turned on the TV in one room and FIM was coming on, so I turned it on in another room and started watching it.

First thing the episode showed was Discord's statue, which was now in a forest on the edge of a park or playground for some reason. First thing I want to point out is that all the characters—Discord and everyone—looked like they do in the show, but the setting and background looked different. Like at times it looked like cartoon characters in a real place, like the Roger Rabbit effect. And suddenly Ponyville was a more wooded environment, with every house being kind of isolated around parts of this forest (it kind of reminded me of Knothole Village), and all the houses looked real, too, not like libraries carved out of trees or anything like that. Also the episode gave the impression of taking place on an overcast day—the sky was gray but it wasn't raining or storming or anything (this is probably real life creeping in, because today really is overcast).

So anyway, first thing it showed was this park and Discord's statue was at the edge, and I was like "Oh god, another Discord episode, " but then he talked about a plan and the statue flashed and suddenly Discord was free, but in the form of a baby dragon (like Spike, but he was all-gray and had wings—and yes he could fly). And then for the first part of the episode, he visited Twilight Sparkle (who, again, didn't live in a tree anymore) pretending to be a messenger from some prestigious magic school. Here I didn't really follow the plot—he told her she had been rejected, but offered to teach her some unique tricks anyway, and they started to kind of get along.

Then somehow Discord got launched away from Twilight's place and landed on a road leading to some mansion with a huge yard. And he wound up meeting this pony who looked a lot like the "The Horror" girl from Applebuck Season. And she said something, and Discord had a crush on her, but there was a servant or maybe the girl's boyfriend or something who didn't like the look of him and was suspicious or something.

I woke up before seeing where the episode went.
