#352544 Shota Sun, 31st Mar '13 8:39:56 PM
The pacing. Good God.

This is the exact same problem The Looney Tunes Show has! Even if the jokes SHOULD WORK because they're just written well, a poorly-edited version of it in animation can completely kill it. Characters say a line, then stop, then stand, then an action occurs, then another line is said! Guess what? Thats boring to watch! Lines aren't supposed to be separated by 10-second-long cold spaces! Scenes need to flow into one another!

I love this fan episode, but purely for the whole endeavor. Audiences expect to see a visual language in film, and that language is extremely hard to understand and get right. Japaneseteeth's reviews proves that even the canon show isnt perfect with their pacing, but at least it's better than THIS!
