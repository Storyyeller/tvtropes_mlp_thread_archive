#18394 Ever 9 Wed, 14th Sep '11 3:27:00 AM
People suggesting that maybe Discord is not evil, just Chaotic Neutral, and doing it for the lulz, probably mixed up some of their term definitions.

For the record, "discord" is not a synonym of "chaos".

Discord means, by definition, tension and fighting between people. It can be the synonym of "disharmony", "strife", "conflict", or "infighting".

Assuming that the creators didn't commit the same mistake, and Discord's name isn't a misnomer but he actually represents the ideal of discord, it's impossible that he is not evil. He is literally the embodiment of all hostility, and thus totally incompatible with the magic of friendship.

#18398 Ever 9 Wed, 14th Sep '11 4:27:03 AM

Technically, that is inaccurate, for example the Slice of Life genre often lacks Conflict.

And anyways, while the word "conflict" can be a synonym of discord, (eg. as it is used in "armed conflict"), the Conflict trope is more broad than that. "Man vs. Nature" is one type of Conflict, "Man vs. Self" is another, even "Male and Female" is a form of literary Conflict.

Conflict can happen without an individual character intentionally trying to spread dissent between the main characters.

Just because Discord's character fulfills that role in the plot, it doesn't exempt him from being evil. The Conflict could be coming from misunderstandings, or natural barriers, etc, but so far, it looks like it's coming from a character who is setting himself up as the opponent of the mane cast's harmony.
