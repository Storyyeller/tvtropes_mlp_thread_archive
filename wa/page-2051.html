#51257 Ace Of Scarabs Fri, 9th Dec '11 7:13:17 AM from Singapore from Singapore
Simple explanation: It's a kind of Uncertainty principle, a thought experiment that proves you can't know what the state of something is until you go and look.

It involves a cat in a box. You won't know if the cat is alive or dead until you go and look. Some physicist wag raised the idea that as long as you don't know the cat's condition, it is both alive and dead. Terry Pratchett countered that there is a third state for the cat, that is absolutely furious.
