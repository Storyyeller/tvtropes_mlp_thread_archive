I finally got started watching through My Little Pony Tales, which is so far the only incarnation of the series that I have not yet seen (until yesterday). I watched "And the Winner Is..." today and I noticed that it's a lot like The Ticket Master in the basic plot of "someone gets a ticket for something and they have an extra one and don't know which friend to invite" but replace the Grand Galloping Gala with a rock concert. It's even the purple one of the group who has to make the decision! (Clover is the lavender/purple one) The resolution is quite different, though. And it makes sense that it would be different, because Clover won the tickets through a random drawing while Twilight was personally given the tickets by Celestia and Clover probably wouldn't be able to get away with asking for 5 more tickets so all her friends could go. Clover's reaction is also very different from Twilight's since she separately tells all her friends they can go with her because she couldn't say no to them (and gets them angry at her for that) while Twilight refuses to choose, nor does she let a third party randomly choose like Clover does.

So basically the two stories have similar plots but they play out differently because of the differences with Twilight and Clover's personalities and the nature of how they obtained the tickets in the first place.
