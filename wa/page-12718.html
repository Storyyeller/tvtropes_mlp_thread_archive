#317936 Perpetual Lurker Sun, 16th Dec '12 7:26:35 PM
I actually developed a good bit of backstory for Trottingham for a fic I'm writing. Like Kegi, I place it across the sea as an England analog, but it's just a city, not a country.

When Nightmare Moon refused to allow the sun to rise, it was not an open and shut affair, and the night lasted for many months before she was defeated. Fearing that the sun may never rise again, a group of enterprising ponies set off across the sea to the east to find the sun themselves. Eventually, the did find it, and settled on the western coast of the continent they had come across where the sun was frozen in a perpetual dawn. Thus they founded Trottingham, The City on the Edge of Tomorrow. Eventually, the sun and moon began to move again, but the city remained and flourished, and was peacefully annexed by the Griffin Empire as it expanded across the continent.

The culture of the city is one of advancement and cooperation, due to the unique mix of races inhabiting it, with magic and technology being developed and used for the purposes of accommodation and adaptation to citizens of any race that chooses to visit or take up residence there.
