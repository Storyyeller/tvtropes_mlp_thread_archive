#289107 kegisak Sat, 29th Sep '12 11:50:38 PM
Er... best not to rely on that, really.

I've written more than a few fics in my time, and though I rarely use EQD these days it's simple enough. They have a submit button right along the top bar, and in there they'll have a link to a submission form that has everything you need.

the opinions of the pre-readers tend to dominate what gets put up, though, so just be aware of that. I had an m/m shipping fic be completely ignored for nigh on a month before some back-door(heh) skulking around finally got it looked at and posted, but it's inevitably a HUGE boost to have it up there. If you're doing a serial fic though, get a few chapters ready before you post it, because only first and last updates get features. the rest just go in the story updates posts, and it's easy for a story to get lost in those. So build up a nice hook for the readers before giving it to them there.
