All I'm going to say on the Power Level discussion is this:

It's based very heavily on headcanon. If you personally believe that Celestia is that powerful, fine, but don't try to argue that canon proves it. We don't have any hard evidence that the sun is in any way comparable in size or mass to our own. I can't take stuff like constellations as evidence because there are tons of similarities between real life and Equestria that don't make any sense when you make those kinds of assumptions.

I don't think that Celestia has ever been portrayed as that much more powerful in the show. Literally the only evidence for that is "She can move the sun", and we have zero frame of reference for how much effort that actually takes, so extrapolating her overall power level from that seems impossible to me.
