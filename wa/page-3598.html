The other Phantasm Films are basically one part After the End and one part Cosmic Horror Story. They get really weird and insanely over-the-top. I'm not really sure how to describe them as weird. Basically in the 2nd the lead manages to escape the Tall man briefly and at the end he gets captured because his love interest was actually a silver sphere piloting a person puppet. In the 3rd, the ice cream man saves him and they go after the Tall Man who has basically done the same thing he did in the first movie to towns all over the world. The lead starts having visions of his brother who is now a sphere. He then gets captured AGAIN and we learn that he's got a golden sphere in his head. Then there's some time travel and attempts to stop the creation of the Tall Man which only result in his creation. Basically the movies continue in fever dream fashion.
