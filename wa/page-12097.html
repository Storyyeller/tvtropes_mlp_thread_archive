Of course, these are ponies far tougher than the soft, entitled G3 ponies. They've had to survive in a world that's so hostile and full of megafauna that they had to manually control the climate to make things habitable. Also the Talents of Equestrian ponies are a racial power, it'd take world-changing magic that alters the fundamental rules of that particular existance to completely negate their Talents. Suppress, maybe. Cancel out, naaaaaah.

Between an angry Night Goddess, a Chaos God, a Fae Queen leading her hive on a feeding invasion, and a brutal and cunning dictator who may be as bad as Weil, they're more than prepared for otherworldly invaders they'd have much less qualms about destroying since they aren't natives or ponies and are clearly a threat.

After two seasons of dealing with their insecurities and fears, they'd find it much, much harder to drive them to despair. Heck, KYUBEY would be more successful because the wily little fluffball could easily take advantage of his cute animal appearance to deceive them with omitted truths and trick them into making Contracts with him.

...Besides, you're taking the characters at their face value. By attacking their friends, you drive the other ponies to stand up and protect them, drawing out Harmony's latent power in the protection of friends.

You also take Fluttershy at her face value, which means you discount her well-hidden force-of-will and courage.

By targeting the other ponies, you target Dashie's friends, awakening her latent Loyalty and driving her to face and overpower her fears and insecurities, reaffirming her ability and strengthening her.

Rarity would willingly be broken rather than let her friends suffer too, demonstrating her Generosity.

Pinkie would realise her friends need their spirits to be bolstered despite her sadness and would find a way to cheer things up somehow.

Applejack has learnt to face harsh truths, and would steel herself because she knows her friends wouldn't fault her for trying her best since she's done it before.

Twilight has gone through the breaking game once, without her magic to boot. Round II would be less successful especially if her friends step up to support her. After all, Friendship is worth fighting for.

With all the Harmony combined, the Sha will enjoy the Skittles-flavored taste of the Final Harmony Blaster.
