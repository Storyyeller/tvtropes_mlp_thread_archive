#26182 Kerrah Sun, 9th Oct '11 2:10:33 AM from Helsinki, Finland from Helsinki, Finland
Since I assume that there are people who read this thread, but not the one on Yack Fest, I'll repost this info here:

The Helsinki Brony meetup was held yesterday, on a cloudy afternoon. To the surprise of the people in charge, about 50 bronies showed up at the Central Railway Station meeting spot. Getting plugged by EQD sure made a difference. While waiting for the last people to show up, someone put on a ridiculously creepy horse mask, and another guy started passing around slices of mettwurst (a spiced sausage which often contains horse-flesh). Eventually, the group moved to a nearby park, where those interested started to custom-paint pony figurines.

Those of us who were feeling like lunch left for a nearby shopping centre, where I bought the best kebab I've ever had. By the time I got back to the park, it was raining cats and dogs, so the parkists went to a nearby tunnel for cover while trying to contact the rest of the people to regroup. I left them soon thereafter, and from what I've come to understand, they hung out in that tunnel for an hour before ending the meetup.

I had a bunch of fun, and the people I met were really nice and easy-going. There was some talk about staring a PoniCon or KoniCon*
"koni" is a Finnish word for a horse, and is pronounced identically to the short version of convention, "coni"
. If it's not at a terribly inconvenient place or time, I'll definitely attend.
