As far as the seasons go, I think they're both about the same quality-wise. Season 2 is way more ambitious in the stuff they try to cover; Season one was safer and didn't take as many risks.

Here's my "very brief" breakdown of season 2:

Good:
  • Vastly improved animation
  • More variety in setting and plot
  • More world building
  • The CMC are used a lot better

Not Good:
  • Not nearly as much ensemble action; I don't mind the episodes that are more focused on individuals, but I think they went too far in that direction at the expense of group dynamics.

Could go either way:
  • Enhanced focus on side characters. While it's nice to see the CMC, Cheerilee, Big Mac, Granny Smith, etc. get some more screen time, I think they overdid it to the point of excluding the main cast on occasion.
