It does mean they had the most bargaining power, though. The tribes lived in divided lands, so it's not like the E Ps had to pay landlords. The unicorns needed the sun and moon every bit as much as the E Ps, and couldn't hoard it. The pegasi could harass the EP's with bad weather, but that would ruin the crops and leave everyone to starve, meaning they'd have two tribes to deal with if they tried it.

The E Ps, meanwhile, could do with the food as they pleased. They could maintain it all for themselves, and could therefore set prices. IF the other tribes were unhappy, their only response was force, at which point the E Ps could simply cut off all supply(Or have it cut off in general if they were conquered).

Basically, the reason the hunger games, ie. through 26 teenagers into a ring and watch them fight to the death, works is because the centralized government bullies the 13 city-states into submission through military might.

The problem is that each city-state produces a single resource - one of which is food. The only place in the country that produces this - and because it's not a luxury it's also the worst-funded city state. If it rebelled, the central government would respond by wiping it out like it did with the last state rebelled, at which point they would promptly starve to death because they just wiped out their only food source.
