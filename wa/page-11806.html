#295127 Perpetual Lurker Mon, 22nd Oct '12 12:55:21 PM
There was a lot more behind the Derpy thing than Moral Guardians. Primarily, the voice actor had been misinformed about Derpy's gender, and her performance had to be altered in post-production to make it sound more gender-neutral, so she wanted to give a proper performance with an appropriate voice. The removal of the name was just a prudent step considering that if the writer had known the origins of the name, she wouldn't have used it in the first place.

Derpy has since then been embraced by Hasbro in all of her cross-eyed glory, and appearing on tons of official merch. She's not going anywhere, considering that she's practically the show's mascot at this point.
