#75952 Perpetual Lurker Sat, 21st Jan '12 9:15:11 AM
Well, I'm going to have to say I really liked this episode. A lot. My main points:

1) My mouth was agape at the whole Derpy scene, and it was hilarious. The voice was cute IMO, and for a short scene they actually managed to characterize her quite well. She really does just want to help. I never cared about which name she got, so I'm fine there. You all should have seen it coming though with her name being used on the Facebook page and such.

2) I was watching Pinkie closely this episode mostly because I wanted to see if her deepened characterization from the last episode stuck. I'd say it has. She's still silly, but it's not ignored by others (and is even used to their advantage) and she actually shows a range of emotion, from concern when AJ didn't come back to pure fury when she broke the Pinkie Promise. I mean, when, outside of being in "crazy mode" in Party of One have we actually seen Pinkie angry? I was delightfully surprised.

3) Like Ginger pointed out earlier, the conflict comes from AJ's honesty. She promised to bring home money, so she did everything she could to try and bring home money after losing the competitions. Her pride kept her from telling them the details until she had to money to pay off her perceived debt from the promise. Now, the lengths to try and get her to talk about it was a bit much, but I chalk that up to this being a cartoon, and it was pretty humorous.

4) The chase scene was just awesome, so yeah.

Looks like I'm in the minority here though...
