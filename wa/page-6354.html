#158843 kegisak Mon, 16th Apr '12 3:03:38 PM
Well they are, Orion. It's a natural aptitude for magic. It's in the same way that I mention my ability to Dowse - if you consider dowsing magic, then we both have an affinity for 'ritual' magic(That is to say, magic that requires a specific set of actions and/or materials to activate). You activate flight magic by flapping your wings, while I activate a magic that let's me determine how many and which gender of child a woman has had by holding a gold ring over the wrist of her dominant hand with a bit of string(Okay, so your ritual is a bit less complicated than mine).

The thing is, neither is a ritual that someone else can learn. It's something you're born with, and is thus natural. This is what I mean when I claim that magic is really just advanced science - 'magic' is all archaic, all spirits and malarkey. This is just a natural affinity for something that exists all the time. It's no different from someone who was born with brown hari or blue eyes - you were born with flight, I was born with dousing. Simple, really.
