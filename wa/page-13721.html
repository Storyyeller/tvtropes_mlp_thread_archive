#343016 Rainbow Sun, 24th Feb '13 3:15:55 PM
We're ranking the Mane 6 and discussing Pinkie? My rankings are in four groups:

  • 1st place: Twilight (for now)
  • 2nd place: Applejack and Pinkie (often fighting with Twilight for first place)
  • 3rd place: Rainbow Dash and Fluttershy
  • 4th place: Rarity (I still like her, I just happen to like the other 5 more)

If other characters are allowed in there, I would put Sweetie Belle up around 2nd place, potentially fighting with Twilight for 1st.

About Pinkie, I think the reason she's endeared herself to me so much is because she reminds me of my dog Luna so much, personality-wise. Or at least what one would get if Luna (the dog) was crossed with the Warner kids from Animaniacs, since Pinkie is probably much more hyper than Luna, who can be pretty good at being calm and relaxed. (Luna basically has two modes, friendly and excited and friendly and calm.) I could easily imagine Luna the dog singing the Smile song if she could talk and sing. And she has a similar "everyone is a potential friend!" attitude to Pinkie.

Pinkie probably could stand to have some more times where she has to face the consequences of her actions, although I personally think that has happened, like with the Too Many Pinkies thing or when she found out in Baby Cakes that babysitting is a lot different than simply being a friend to the babies (which is one of my favorite Pinkie episodes). And it has been shown that she is capable of learning from her mistakes and applying what she learned, like when Twilight taught her how to actually look for clues in the cake mystery story and then she applied that knowledge to identify the ones who ate the other three entries at the end. Probably the big test for her would be if Cranky showed up again and they showed that she learned something about respecting privacy (although the idea of actually liking privacy is probably difficult for her to comprehend).
