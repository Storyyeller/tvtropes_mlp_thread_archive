In the latest episode, many have commented on the fact that Fluttershy was taller and lankier than the other fillies, and have chalked it up to the fact that she's a year older than the others, as she said to Pinkie in an earlier episode. But why would she be in flight school with the younger fillies? Well, we also learned that she was a terrible flier when she was young, therefore, Fluttershy was held back in Flight School.

Twilight Sparkle = Belle. Emphasis on learning and being smarter than the others.

Applejack = Tiana. Hard worker, but stubborn, and doesn't always loosen up.

Fluttershy = Snow White. Friend to All Living Things and the most nature-oriented.
