Okay, played the shmup.

Gonna have to chip in with WASD not working well with Shift. With little exception you want to be able to let one hand do exclusively basic movement without having to hit anything else. You also don't want your pinky in particular having to hold a button for long periods of time.

The manual vs. autofire is weird, but I can see what you were trying to do. I played a flash shmup a while back that did both with one button — it only went into focus fire if you held the button, but pulsing it gave you the unfocus. You might want to consider something similar to cut down on how many buttons you're keeping track of, especially as you're already using Shift.
