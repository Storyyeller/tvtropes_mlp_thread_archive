#118908 Seraphem Thu, 8th Mar '12 2:15:04 PM from Delamare
legal reasons.

Best real example is from Babylon 5, JMS used to frequent message boards and chat rooms about the show and read fan ideas, until one fan wrote a story that was almost identical to an episode JMS had already planned out, he then needed to go though a massive amount of legal hurdles to prove that the idea was his first and he wasn't stealing it from the fan.

so it's to prevent a fan from suing them if they use an idea that the fan had written as story about. If they can prove they never read the story, they can't have stollen the idea.
