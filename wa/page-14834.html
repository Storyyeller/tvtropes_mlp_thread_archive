What I'm getting at is that there's a trap in providing World Building too quickly. As the writers impose limits and concepts even if vague, it restricts the universe and makes it feel smaller.

Which is why season 4 should take the opertunity to take Twilight and friends beyond Equestria and free themselves of so many restrictions for a fresh start. It's the perfect time now Twilight is Princess to go to new places.
