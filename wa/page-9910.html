#247746 Japanese Teeth Thu, 12th Jul '12 3:27:05 PM from Meinong's jungle
Yeah, AJ can do a good chunk of the harvest herself, but no way could she possibly run the farm without help from her family.

The way I'm using it is that "Superhuman" is to "Superpony" what "Human" is to "pony". That is, in this context it would mean that a pony's performance is so high above average that they'd have to have savant level skill to pull it off. In other words, having that level of skill in something would be highly unusual. Which is why I don't think that's the case. The Cutie Mark doesn't grant awesome abilities, it just serves as a symbol that the pony in question likes and is good at a certain skill.

I.e. If a pony's skill is "music", it just means that they're better than the average pony at it, and they enjoy it. It doesn't mean that every pony with that CM is a Mozart level virtuoso.
