My opinion on ponies and chess:

  • Twi: She plays well, but overthinks too much despite her massive chess theory knowledge from studying.
  • Pinkie: What theory? She gets by on intuition and creative moves, but she sometimes misses some pretty neat fakeouts.
  • Fluttershy: She doesn't usually play chess, but she's picked something up from Rarity and is at least fairly decent.
  • Dashie: She doesn't do chess
  • AJ: It's a way to kill time in winter when less time is needed for farm maintenance. She learnt from Granny Smith, who happens to be Ponyville's most experienced chess grandmaster.
  • Rarity: She learnt it as part of her plan to climb the social ladder. While no high-tier grandmistress, she's fairly competent at chess.
  • Trixie: May or may not play. Most likely favors aggressive play and mindgames in the form of misdirection plays and taunting.
  • Shining Armor: Plays chess socially with his guards. Has a commemorative set with his likeness on the Knight pieces.
  • Cadence: Used to play socially with Twi when she was little. Got some commemorative chesspieces to go with Shiny's set, with her as the Queens and Shiny as the Kings :3
  • Blueblood: May or may not be competent at chess, but can be deceived with a little metagaming and mindgames, especially if his opponent is a cute mare.
  • Fancy Pants: My fanon says he's a Canterlot Spymaster, so naturally he's very good at chess.
  • Celestia and Luna: both are Old-era grandmistresses at chess. Luna plays a mean, mean game using old-era strategies from pre-Discordian times, while Celestia uses a lot of modern strategies and can even mimic some of the grandmasters she's clashed with on the board. They've got a special set with their likenesses on the matching black and white Queen pieces, and play against each other for fun. Just like old times :3
  • Flim Flam brothers: Fairly competant. I want a fanfic where they make a fake automatic chess engine
