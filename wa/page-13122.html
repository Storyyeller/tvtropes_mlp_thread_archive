time for another fanfic report! and it's rather appropriate for the current discussion, as it has some changeling fanon!

Dear Thread Manor...

I have a few more changeling stories today! but this time, it's about changeling who love ponies for real. Then we have kinda of a sad Pinkie fic, and finally some sheer absurdity with RD.

Mendacity. Bon Bon and Lyra are two poines that love eachother very much and would do anything eachother. Well, that's only kind of true, as Bon Bon isn't a pony. She's a changeling. She's done her best these past 20 years to distance herself from other changelings, the Unseelie Court, and all things Fae, just so that she can live peacefully with Lyra, but it seems fate has other things in store for her. When she hears word of changelings preparing an invasion on Canterlot, she goes to Canterlot to try and warn someone. However, when she returns home, she finds that Lyra's been taken by the changelings. Now it's up to her and a supremely snarky kelpie who Bon Bon wishes would just go away to go to Canterlot and do whatever they can to prevent this mess and save Lyra.

This fic was notable in that it takes changelings and goes full on 100% The Fair Folk with them. Complete with vulnerabilities to iron and time being wonky in the Land of Faerie. I'd recommend it for that alone.

Flitter. Morning Rain and Swift Star have lived in Canterlot for years, married, even raised a colt and saw him move out. But this peaceful existence is shattered when changelings invade Canterlot and Swift Star, real name Flitter, finds himself blasted out of Canterlot like all the other changelings, the form he's held for so long dispelled and lost forever because you can't copy the form of a pony that's been dead for years. Fortunately, their love proves true and Morning Rain stays with him. Now they have to decide what to do, about Flitters own hive (which was not the one that attacked Canterlot), about how they're going to break this to their son, and finally, about each other.

This one was ok, not as good as mendacity but it would be hard to beat that. Still has some pretty neat fanon for changelings.

What is a Name- in progress. Pinkie Pie, while in the Everfree, is struck with a magical malady, an aetheric amnesia that has caused to mind to revert to her state as she was back on the rock farm, before the sonic rainboom. It's so severe she's even lost her cutie mark. Pinkamena Diane Pie knows nothing about Ponyville, has no idea who these 5 other mares claiming to be her best friends are, and certainly has no idea how to spread joy and laughter to others. Twilight and the others are going to do everything they can to help her out, but since this magical disease was thought to be a myth until now, it won't be easy.

This is an interesting one. rock farm pinkie trying to amke friends again even though the only existence she's known dull and listless to the point of being unbearable.

Rainbow Dash and the Ultimate Showdown of Ultimate Destiny. I'd like to write a snappy synopsis for this one, but it's just too crazy for that. Yes, it's based on that Ultimate Showdown. Warning: Abandon all Hope of Anything Making Sense, Ye Who Enter Here.

I think Mendacity was probably my favorite one from this batch, which was funny because it started out as my least favorite, but really grew on me. See you next time all!
