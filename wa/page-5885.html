Any tips for coming up with backstory?
Start with a basic concept and grow your character from there, one detail at a time. Occasionally, you can invent a detail at random, and then try to connect it with what you already have. As your character gets more detailed, you may have to start pruning stuff that doesn't fit as nicely as it used to, but that's natural.

Every summer the town holds a Founder's day celebration that involves a lot of turtle themed stuff. Just about everyone in the town has a turtle themed cutie mark of some sort. My character got caught cheating by greasing up her turtle for the turtle races and actually earned the cutie mark for it, which lead people to believe she got her cutie mark for cheating (thats not exactly the case though... I havent fully worked out that part of the story). She gets renamed based on her cutie mark and eventually leaves town but keeps the name to remind her of her past.

She eventually finds herself in the seedy underbelly of a larger city, and sets up a gambling den. She gets accused of fixing her games (someone finds out about her cutie mark story) and she gets run out of town... leaving her a bit of a wanderer at the moment.
