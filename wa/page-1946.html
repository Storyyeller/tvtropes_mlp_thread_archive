@ Will: About six months ago I had to make a neverwinter module for a school project, and the sotry was pretty loosley based on MLP, more specifically the story of how Celestia nad Luna got their divinity in the first place.

The basic idea was that they were citizens of a kingdom ruled by "The King of Heaven" (A single god who ruled over the sun, moon and stars, and a surprisingly good anaolog for discord), who had long since gone insane and spent time torturing his citizens. Magic users (rare and not necessarily genetic in this universe) were exempt from this treatment, being brought to the palace to be raised as nobility and trained in the kings personal army. The Celestia and Luna analogs went out into the city at one point and found out what was happening, and went on a quest to stop it.

The end result is that 'Discord' got his power from an ancient, powerful artifact (an analog for the elemnts of harmony). The sisters found the pieces of the artifact, put it back together, and used it to steal his power. The power needed a receptacle, however, as it was what made the sun and moon move in the first place, so Luna and Celestia had to take the powers on themselves.

So yeah, basically my head canon is that Discord used to be the one in charage of everything, but Luna and Celestia stole his power and used it to become goddesses themselves.
