Also, I know I've said this before, but my headcanon on the different races is that they all have magic, but it manifests differently due to differing biology:

  • Unicorn horns act as focus points (like a built in wand) that allows them to deliberately construct specific spells with pre-determined effects. However, this means that in order to do any magic at all, they have to sufficiently understand it in order to perform it.

  • Pegasus magic is also active, but largely subconscious rather than conscious. The magic manifest through physical contact; any time they move, they subconsciously shove magic out of their bodies and manipulate any part of the environment that they physically interact with. I.e. they're able to fly because when they flap their wings, they're shoving magic into the air that allows them to stay aloft even when it should be physically impossible.

  • Earth pony magic is pretty much entirely passive. They can't really invoke it on purpose, but the magic is constantly flowing through their bodies and into their surroundings. The constant flow of magic through their bodies makes them more powerful and also creates a connection to their surroundings that doesn't exist for the other races.
