That score is the final total of two written assignments, a test and general contribution. The test was worth 50% of this total mark, the two assignments were 10 and 30 respectively, and contribution was also worth 10.

So, if you did exceptionally well on the test and got 70%, that would be 35% to your total. And your total must exceed 40% to earn a pass. 40-49% is a level 3. 50-59% is a level 2.2. 60-69% is a 2.1, and is generally what we are told to aim for. 70%+ is a level 1 (a first), and gets you on the honors list.
