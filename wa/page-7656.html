#191398 kegisak Fri, 11th May '12 2:47:00 PM
The Zoan fruits are... They're sort of Boring, but Practical, in universe. They're pretty much solely physical, but they give you one hell of a physical boost, depending on which animal you get. Even non-predatory animals like cows or Giraffes give you a huge strength boost too.

Of course, like every other power they seem to get more useful with creativity. One of the main characters is a zoan user who unlocks I think about 8 forms, while normally zoan users only have 'human', 'animal' and 'mix' forms. Of course he's a doctor and originally had to use medicine to get to the other points, so it's up in the air whether any zoan could unlock multiple forms or if it's just the chemical cocktail he's pumped himself with. By this point in the story he no longer needs the medicine to unlock his more advanced forms
