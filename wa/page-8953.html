Don't know. We know almost nothing about Zecora, I don't think I could pin down specifically what her special talent is. Even if you could, the cutie marks are usually symbolic of the events that made their owners realize what their talents were. Fluttershy's talent is communicating with animals, but her CM is a trio of butterflies because they saved her when she fell to earth. Rarity's talent is creating beauty, but her CM is a trio of diamonds because discovering a cache of jewels is what led her to realizing this. Applejack's CM is a trio of apples, but her talent is running a farm, not anything to do with apples specifically. Cutie Marks are nebulous things, you have to know the story behind them to get the full symbolism.
