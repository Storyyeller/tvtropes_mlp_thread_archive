Although I have to wonder what the hell the purpose of a rock farm is.
WHY IS THERE A ROCK FARM?! WHY?!

I'm going with the explanation that on account of it being a story told to us by Pinky Pie (which ended with her saying 'and that's how Equestria was made!'), I think we're better off looking at her story from the point of an Unreliable Narrator or Broad Strokes.

I'd say the general basics of her story are accurate (such as her family being uptight amish types, the Sonic Rainboom inspiring her, and getting her mark through throwing her family a party), but beyond that I think we can interpret things like that her family had a 'rock farm' as a case of Pinky Pie being, well, Pinky Pie. After all, we also have her previous story from the second episode in the woods (told through song) that doesn't seem to entirely mesh with what we have here.

Long story short, don't take Pinky's story at face value.

That aside, while I thought the episode was cute, I get a sense I went in with over-hightened expectations since I'm not getting the same 'best episode ever' vibe that I expected based on the hype I read prior to watching it and that everyone else seems to be experiencing. My own fault, really.
