My thoughts on Celestia, Luna, and any other potential 'gods' of the setting is that - due to the fact that being able to remember anything at their age would require absurd amounts of mental processing power - they're astronomically intelligent being, capable of seeming omniscient to the lay pony. they are, however, still completely fallable. As such some ponies may treat them like gods, and some may fall heavier into the roll than others, while others may simply sit back and only step in where needed.

While it's all rather absurd fanon TCYB required me to do a lot of thinking about the nature of the gods, so I have a lot of 'ideas' about them and how they operate. Like they have basically their own society and legal system exclusively for gods so they don't step out of line with their powers, for example.
