I think Twilight's panic attacks are part of her normal personality. What I've come to notice is, yes, she is good at planning and tends to always have a plan. But that's also the thing: she often has exactly one plan. For whatever reason, she tends to never have any contingencies. This tends to lead to her panicking when things go wrong and, once again, she has no back-up plan.

I would argue that assuming the theory (which I do personally agree with) is true, the instant where she experienced her "personality reversal" would be when she finally went beyond the simple, normal panic to realizing that despite her powerful magic, there is absolutely nothing she could possibly do to fix the situation, at which point she starts thinking completely illogically.

I think the cutie mark failure theory fits really well in Luna's case, as from what we've seen with the main six, it could easily be the starting point of her rage and jealousy. Given how vulnerable they all end up in those moments, I could see that as the perfect time for whatever it is that helped corrupt Luna into Nightmare Moon to strike.
