About the Canterlot aristocrats speaking RP, it first annoyed me when I saw Sweet and Elite, especially because the Princesses don't speak that way themselves (so it didn't make sense to me that the nobles wouldn't try to imitate the way the princesses speak). It's also possible that snobby culture doesn't take dictation from the royal sisters. After all, I doubt Celestia wears fancy clothes.
