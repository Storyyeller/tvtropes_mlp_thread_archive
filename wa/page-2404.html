#60086 Perpetual Lurker Sun, 25th Dec '11 9:40:46 AM
I didn't find MMDW to be that bad of an episode. The main problem with it is that the genre shift is so abrupt compared to a normal episode that reality itself seems to warp to have things happen. You can't have a superhero episode without people to save, and since there's no crime in Ponyville (none that they will show us, at least) that leaves accidents and disasters to save ponies from. So they had to have 5+ potentially fatal events happen in what appears to be quick succession to get the plot to work. As a stand-alone episode, it's fun and makes some cool Batman nods (especially in the music near the end) so I like it. In the context of the series as a whole, though, it sticks out like s sore thumb.
