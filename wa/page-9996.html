#249884 Ace Of Scarabs Sun, 15th Jul '12 9:58:28 AM from Singapore
Pony!Iji repost

I tried using an old technique I learnt from Dean "Noogy" Dodrill, and refined using other how-to-draws from the Internet.

Pencil art comes first, which I make a copy of using modifed contrast settings to reduce the lightest outlines to nothingness. I then ink using my Zig Writer felt-tip, very carefully (admittedly I need more practice ). I then scan in the inked duplicate with further-tweaked settings, exploiting the Histogram/Levelling tool to further eliminate the faint pencil lines.

Ta-da~ somewhat clean linework!

To be honestly I can actually cheatyface and use GIMP and my Wacom tablet and layering to separate the outlines used for construction/composition, but I still have an old-fashioned touch in my drawing technique :3
