#38697 Japanese Teeth Fri, 11th Nov '11 2:02:32 PM from Meinong's jungle from Meinong's jungle
I have a love/hate relationship with puns. The only ones I actually like are the ones that are just so contrived and horrible that they go straight past So Bad, It's Good and into awesomeness territory. But the vast majorities of standard puns just feel like filler to me. As far as I'm concerned, the best puns are the ones that are situation specific and could not possibly work in any other context. Multi-context puns tend to be overused and end up in "okay, that was just lame" territory most of the time.

As far as the antagonists go:
  • Trixie was a bitch, but a very entertaining bitch because of her Large Ham tendencies.
  • Blueblood is a douche, but I can't really hate him because Rarity kinda brought it on herself by hanging out with him the whole time
  • Gilda is totally and irredeemably evil because she made Fluttershy cry is probably my least favorite, mainly because she wasn't as entertaining as Trixie or Blueblood. I felt like she overdid the "cool" personality, but didn't overdo it enough to be funny. I don't hate her, per se, but I didn't think she was that interesting.

EDIT: I was okay with Twist, although I think the lisp would have gotten grating if she would have had a lot of screentime. I've never really understood why people consider speech impediments to be cute.
