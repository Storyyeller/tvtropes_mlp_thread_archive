#22917 Calnos Tue, 27th Sep '11 2:50:10 PM from Nowhere from Nowhere
As one of the main contributors to the background ponies section, obviously I'm for it staying. Not because they're important to the fandom but they do exist as characters that can be troped. We know that gray pegasus with a blone mane competed in the Best Young Flyer's Competition, helps out with the weather in Ponyville, hangs out with the umbrella cutie mark pegasus and her raindrop cutie mark Palette Swap quite a bit (More so than Carrot Top, if I recall correctly), she occasionally has Fish Eyes, and she and her Palette Swap buddy accidentally dropped a bunch of stuff on Twilight Sparkle. She is one of many Recurring Extras.

In fact, we know more about her than we do about Twilight's mom, who by all means should be much more important in the show. What do we know about her?...She looks like G1 Twilight.

What we don't know about that pegasus is if she really is Ditzy Doo, the pony that went north for the southern birds, her intelligence level, if she's a mother, her favorite food, etc, and I think that's the main problem. Keeping Canon separate from Fanon.

I'd like (and have been trying to keep) the background ponies page strictly what's canon about them with a few Fanon tidbits in their descriptions and their Fan Nicknames. the Ensemble Darkhorse page can go into deeper details about their general fanon personalities, but the actual character page should stay with what's shown in the show.
