Regarding the "was Discord nerfed?" discussion from a few pages back:

I think we need to keep his motivation in mind here. In the Ro H episodes, he was acting the way he did because he needed to split up the elements of Harmony to avoid being resealed, and as such basically jumped right to the extremes, and he only turned the world into his personal playground when he was sure that the elements were neutralized. In this episode, he had the elements hanging Sword of Damocles style over his head the whole time. He wasn't going to go totally insane with his powers until he was reasonably sure there was no risk of getting zapped, and by the time he reaches that point, it's tempered by Fluttershy. Basically, he wasn't nerfed; he was deliberatly holding back because he knew that if he did anything major (like fucking with the sun and moon) he'd get sent right back into the statue.
