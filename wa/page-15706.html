Well, for an on topic discussion, instead of somewhat creepy magic bus humanizations, you know the bat ponies? Faust tweeted that she believes that they are their own race. (Read some of her other tweets on this too. :P)

A guy on reddit dug up a bit of obscure Greek mythology on this. You know that pegasi are based on, well, Pegasus? Bat ponies may be based on a mythological race called Pyrippus, a type of horse with leathery wings. apparently, they pulled the chariot of Hades. It might be coincidental, especially seeing how obscure this race is (I can find hardly anything on them), but it's cool nonetheless.
