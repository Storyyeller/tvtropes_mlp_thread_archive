#234547 kegisak Mon, 25th Jun '12 1:42:33 PM
I honestly don't see Rarity as being that egotistical at all. She confidant in her skills as a dressmaker, and in her beauty, and both are not for no reason. But beyond that she doesn't really have any sort of an 'I'm awesome, love me" air, at least not to my mind. She has strong opinions which she sticks to, and I can see how they might come across as egotism by association, but compare how she acted when she was first getting invited to high society - she actually seemed shocked that they wanted her. It's obvious she wants to be good enough, and she was lavishing in being considered so, but I think on the whole she doesn't think she's reached her dreams yet, which is refreshing.

Now this is in contrast to Dash who clearly thinks she's already good enough to achieve her dreams. She doesn't want to earn her way into the wonderbolts, she assumes she simply has to show off and they'll accept her. That smacks much more of egotism to me than anything Rarity does.
