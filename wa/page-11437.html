I think there's a particular problem with a tendency to make Pikie really crazy and fourth wall breaking.
Canon Pinkie does break the fourth wall occasionally, but to my knowledge she never does it in a way that the other characters notice. She looks at the camera occasionally, directly talks to the audience at the end of episode 2, she rants at the voiceover at the end of "Over a Barrell". But she never says anything that the other characters could hear that would tip them off.

(Equestria has a Lighter and Softer equivalent of the SCP Foundation. Pinkie is one of their agents tasked with protecting against threats from beyond the fourth wall. )

I think fanfic's tendency to have Pinkie blatantly referencing the fourth wall in conversations with other characters is rather less interesting.

I did like how Background Pony handled the Pinkie-centric chapter. Her dialogue is loaded with references to production details about the show, or other fanfics, or even Lampshade Hanging Background Pony's own conventions—yet none of it is outright fourth-wall breaking, and all her lines make sense from a purely in-universe perspective as well.
