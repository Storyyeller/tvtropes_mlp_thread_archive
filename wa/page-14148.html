How did I first get into the show? I've been a fan of My Little Pony in general since I was at least 4, but it's actually more complicated than that. When I read about it, I wasn't sure about it at first because of the "Twilight learns to socialize more and stop studying so much" thing that gets mentioned in the first episode (I was afraid it would portray being smart as a bad thing, which it luckily doesn't do most of the time) but it did sound like an interesting new version of the show from what I read about it on this site. I also was wary about the idea that the nighttime sister was the "villain" but was relieved to learn that Luna turns good and isn't inherently evil (I like how she's turned into Dark Is Not Evil as the show has gone on, like in Luna Eclipsed or when she helps Scootaloo). The first episode I tried out was Applebuck Season, which sounded the most interesting to me when I read what it was about, and I ended up really liking it, especially after the scene where she and Pinkie make the "baked bads." I then looked up the other episodes and watched through the rest of the first season. I think I joined the fandom around sometime between the first and second seasons, too.

It all started when I started seeing pony sprays whenever I played TF2. I remember one of them was a picture of the Last Supper, but with the character's heads photoshopped in place of the apostles'. Then, I ended up seeing a video that took the audio from a Starcraft 2 trailer and used clips from the show for the visuals and I thought, "Huh. This actually looks like it has some action in it." Since I prefer watching things in order, I watched all the episodes before that before watching it. Then I continued watching, eventually coming to the most recent episode, and when I tried watching the next one (Cutie Mark Chronicles), I found out it hadn't aired yet. Then as I waited for it to air, I officially became a fan.
