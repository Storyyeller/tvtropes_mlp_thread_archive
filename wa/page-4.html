I gotta admit I'm not familiar with the eighties series outside of the pilot episodes but did it really get that effed-up?

Welllll...deadly peril was a frequent thing, and the gallery of villains and monsters could have filled the Dungeons&Dragons Monsters Manual. Threats have included a demonic overlord out to cause eternal night, a genocidal penguin king out to exterminate all non-penguin races, an inter-dimensional invasion, evil flowers...

And yet, at the same time, we had these cutesy, adorable protagonists trying to have garden parties, eat cakes, sing, dance, and lead happy lives in their magical land. In-between fighting all the aforementioned villains, sometimes to the death.

So...it WAS pretty effed-up. Like I said on rpg.net, it kinda had a "the Disney Princesses move to Sunnydale" feel.
