#27658 Japanese Teeth Thu, 13th Oct '11 9:45:39 PM from Meinong's jungle from Meinong's jungle
Weirdly, a lot of people claim that the appeal is the fact that the games' portrait art kinda sucks. It makes people less likely to worry about nailing the art style because honestly, you don't have to be very good to imitate it or do better. So it generated a lot more fanart and fancomics than most other series. It also helps that while the art isn't that great, the designs are really distinct, so it's easy for people to latch onto them. Throw in the fact that the setting is really malleable, and you have a recipe for people to obsess over it.

It's not that dissimilar to MLP; the art style is simplistic enough that making fan art isn't too difficult, and the way the setting is presented makes it really easy to fit fanon into the holes.

#27660 Ace Of Scarabs Thu, 13th Oct '11 10:28:42 PM from Singapore from Singapore
There's a whole stack of reasons why Touhou is popular.

  1. Lots of different characters, and I mean well over a hundred girls to get to know :D
  2. ZUN is a godly musician. With chiptunes and FM Synth at that.
  3. A surprisingly in-depth world with pretty neat stories that draw from mythology.
  4. Gensokyo is a fantasy wonderland, even more whimsical than Equstria, although a little more dangerous.
  5. Danmaku is prettier than conventional bullet hell. Also, many resources online so it's easy to introduce to people.
  6. Fanwork and meme critical mass makes the fandom almost self-sustaining. Especially the fanmusic.

Ponies are approaching that territory.

  1. If you include all variations of ponies cross-generationally, you have hundreds of ponies to choose from.
  2. Daniel Ingram is an aces musician, and some of the songs from the older generations were pretty neat too.
  3. Quite a lot of pop-culture and mythology references. FiM in particular has a well-shaped mythology behind it. (Thank you Lauren Faust, you really are best pony :3)
  4. Never mind Ponyland and the older worlds, Equestria is practically a utopia, especially compared to a crapsack world like Earth.
  5. National TV makes it easily accessible by everyone, making it easy to welcome bronies to the herd.
  6. Fanwork has begun to mushroom, approaching Touhou tier (even Madoka Magica hasn't got that far yet! Must be all the Urobutcher-tier GRIMDARK scaring people away :V)
