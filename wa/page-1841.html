I'm supposed to be Applejack. Huh. That is surprising, to say the least. Let's break it down:

A real hard worker you are, yup. If there's one thing you hate, it's being inactive or coming across as lazy. You'll put in more effort than anyone else, even if it may be against your better judgement or even if delegating your work is a smarter option. It's not surprising to see you working all day long, even if it's to help friends out. However, friendship is still important to you, so you will always, always stick to your commitments and keep your promises.

You're a tell-it-like-it-is pony, not holding your tongue and telling whoever whatever's on your mind or what your true thoughts are. Thankfully, you can still pick your words carefully and still keep some sensitivity towards what you say. But, others may find you irritable in appearance if you're not getting your way or you feel against something.

You are also most likely to be athletic and into staying fit, knowing that if you're not, you won't be able to keep up with your work. Fashion and looking good aren't on the top of your priorities, to say the least. You also tend to be very protective of your friends, standing up for them against anyone who is causing them problems, and usually succeeding in warding off any foes. You'd gladly lose a limb if it meant saving a friend's life.

Okay, first of all, I am NOT a hard worker. At least not when it comes to the only duty I have currently, studying. That being said, the bosses of those few part-time jobs I've had always commended me for constantly trying hard (At the end of a particularly hard day, one guy asked "Don't you ever get tired at all?"). I guess I just like to please people, rather then do something for myself. That said, I'll always hold up my end of a bargain, as long as it doesn't involve studying. Which is sort of sad, since I'm supposed to be a student with no strong traits besides intelligence.

Second paragraph is pretty much spot-on. I have a low capacity for bullshit, and you will know it.

I am not athletic in any meaningful sense of the word. Sports never interested me much, and my daily life involves mostly books and internet. As a result, I'm 185 cm tall, weight 53 kilos, and can't make a run for the subway without ending up panting like a dog. That said, fashion is indeed incredibly low on my list. T-shirt, sweater and jeans function just fine in any situation, as long as there are not too many holes in it. As for the standing up for your friends, yeah, that did happen. Multiple times, in fact, I saved them from bullies by directing their attention to me instead.

Soooo, I guess it sort of fits. This pleases me somewhat, as out of the mane 6, Applejack is the character I can actually look up to.
