Ooo, RP is fun. In my experience, it's really tough to keep anyone together for anything, but so worth it if they do turn out. To put things in perspective, out of probably at least a dozen table top campaigns, I've only seen three of them play out entirely (and I was GM all three times). I wouldn't give up those experiences for anything.

One thing I want to warn about in terms of play by post is, even if you consistently have posters online and around, it's easy for people to lose interest (this was the case for a handful of PBPs I've been in). And all it takes is the loss of one character to have stuff start unraveling.

And the timezone thing is definitely a big issue with real time. I'm actually in a free form one in the joint Training Grounds' IRC and it's such an anomaly to my previous experience so far (everyone's super enthusiastic and online almost every night). And that's with a few people from timezones where our usual run time is like 6 in the morning for them (we have had them pass out in game a couple times >.>).
