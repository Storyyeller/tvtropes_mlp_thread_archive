@Apple: Helpful long-term hint for writer's block: any time you think of an interesting story idea, put it on a list on google docs or something. Over time you'll accumulate a bunch of them. Then when you have writer's block on one thing you can shelve it and work on something else. Even if you never develop the other ideas, working on them will keep the creative juices flowing. If that fails, forget writing and do something else. Watch a movie, play a video game, take a nap, whatever. Anything to change your mental state. Forcing it will just make you more frustrated.
