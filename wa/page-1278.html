LO, Praise the prowess of pony-queens,

of unicorned Celestians in days long sped.

Oft Celestia, filly of one Not Known,

from squadroned foes, from many a herd,

the feed trough tore, awing the earth ponies.

She prospered under the sun, in wealth she throve,
