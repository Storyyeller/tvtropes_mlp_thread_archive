#40938 kegisak Thu, 17th Nov '11 2:24:30 PM
Even with a model, the skeleton is still the most important part by far. the model should only help you arrange the pose of the skeleton. But the lines and circles are what guide your overall drawing. It's like stretching a canvas over a wireframe - you can arrange the wireframe on your own, but the canvas over top of it wll follow the frame. It's also a really good 'rough. It's simple enough to draw in seconds, but expressive enough that you can immediately see when something is wrong with the drawing.
