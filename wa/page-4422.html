I think I said it before when this thing firs re-surfaced but I'll paraphrase myself: as much as I like Derpy (and most of the fandom feels some reverence for the character) I think in hindsight it was a mistake spotlighting her in "The Last Roundup". Before all this the character still had a certain freedom; she was canon enough that we could get a funny little background scene of her doing something that was not too invasive but still showed the people behind the show acknowledging us.

But after that line between canon and fanon was crossed and the resulting back-lash it seems any further development of Derpy's character from the brony perspective has been crushed and now the fandom is simply fighting (and losing) just to keep what is or is considered left of her (or what made her unique, one only needs to read the many entries of Derpy on This Wiki).

So after this perceived warping or violation of Word of Dante the "natural" order takes effect, and Derpy moved back to the background and it is highly unlikely that she will get the attention in the show that she had before all of this.

This is just part of my opinion on this whole thing. I was one of those who cheered win she spoke, but, in retrospect probably should of never happened.
