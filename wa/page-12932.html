All right, saw the episode. Thoughts:

  • Really liked AJ in this one. I thought she had a good mix of being put off by Spike's behavior but not wanting to hurt his feelings. I guess some people were saying that season 1 AJ would be more likely to get angry at him, but I don't see it. Yeah AJ can get upset, but usually at ponies that will get angry back, like RD or Rarity. She never gets angry at Fluttershy for instance, and I don't see her getting angry at a baby dragon either, even if he is being annoying.

  • RD writing cheesy self insert Mary Sue fiction is hilarious and perfect.

  • As is OCD Twilight stopping her reading to readjust an inkwell.

  • I didn't mind the CGI on the timberwolves. I've seen some pretty bad uses of CGI in cartoons, and this blended pretty well. Besides, those things look like a pain in the ass to animate in flash.

  • Loved the attempt at a fake timberwolf attack.

  • My biggest issue with the episode was Spike's sudden incompetence. I think it would have worked just as well if he was capable but AJ wasn't comfortable with him helping so much or was annoyed with how overbearing he was, but I guess then we wouldn't get wacky hijinks. Just sort of weird that he immediately goes back to being useful when he returns to Twilight.
