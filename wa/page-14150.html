#353732 marston Thu, 4th Apr '13 12:09:34 PM from America
I discovered the show when I kept hearing more and more people talk about it back in early 2011. At first I dismissed it, but when it got to the point where I heard that people like Weird Al, Seth Green and a couple online video creators I liked were fans of the series, I had to check it out.

I looked up random episodes of the show, and the very first one I watched was Sonic Rainboom. Needless to say, I was shocked at how great the animation was and how good the jokes were {I also got a kick out of the fact that one of RD's bullies is ovbiously voiced by the same person who voiced Kevin in Ed Edd 'n Eddy}, and the climax of the episode was freaking epic. I also ended up watching Bridle Gossip, which I loved too. But there were a couple things that prevented me from watching all the episodes immediatly and becoming a brony: I was already distracted since I was in the process of watching Yu Yu Hakusho and Cowboy Bebop as time went on, along with the various video games and movies I had on my to do list. So I put off watching the show for the time being.

Eventually I finally got done with all the other stuff I was playing/watching, and I got so bored that I went back and started watching more episodes of Mlp Fim. As soon as I was done, I started checking out the fanworks of the show like Story of the Blanks, Fallout Equestria and other fanfics, and I eventually got into stuff like shipping and fan speculation. The show's fanbase is so great that it's what inspired me to get some online accounts and interact with other bronies {Before this, I was just a lurker who never got actually involved in online stuff}. And now here I am.
