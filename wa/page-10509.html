If anyone asked me about possible shipping between MLPFIM characters, well....

  • Rainbow Dash/Pinkie Pie: As "Griffon The Brush-Off" showed, Pinkie was bouncing after Dash like Pepe La Pew, and acting like a stalker. Fortunately, Dash warmed up to her and they have proven to be quite the pair. Then there's that set of scenes in "Party Of One" where Dash went and got Pinkie....
  • Rainbow Dash/Fluttershy: A number of episodes in Season 2 had the two of them paired up a lot, as "May The Best Pet Win!", "The Super Speedy Cider Squeezy 6000", "Hurricane Fluttershy", and "A Canterlot Wedding - Part 2" have shown. I wonder where they'll go with it in Season 3!
  • Rainbow Dash/Applejack: "Fall Weather Friends" demonstrated that these two are Not so Different when it comes to being competitive. At least they can bond other that!
  • Rainbow Dash/Twilight Sparkle: I don't there's been an episode thus far that has the two of them together. In fact, before "Read It And Weep", they had little in common, due to Dash being an athlete and Twilight being an egghead. Of course, now that Dash has become an egghead herself, it may result in stronger ties between RD and TS.
  • Rainbow Dash/Rarity: Okay, there hasn't been an episode that has the two of them together. In fact, they have little in common due to Rarity being the fashionista and Dash being a tomboy. Unless I see an episode that has them bonding over something, I just don't see much potential with this pairing!
