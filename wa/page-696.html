#17377 Japanese Teeth Thu, 8th Sep '11 7:17:31 AM from Meinong's jungle from Meinong's jungle
I kinda agree with RS about it. It's less about what kind of content is involved and more what end the content is used for. It's entirely possible to write a fanfic with sexual content and still be faithful to the original characterization (although it's very hard to do correctly, especially with a show like MLP). It really comes down to whether the author is writing about the characters for their own sake, or whether he's just using them as an excuse to write about porn or gore or whatever. In the later case, it feels like they don't care about the characters or the work because they sacrifice any sort of fidelity to the source material in favor of shock value.

EDIT: To use that as an example, I'm okay with it because the artist put a lot of work into making sure that he captured the character's personalities in the drawings rather than just going "BOOBS EVERYWHERE".
