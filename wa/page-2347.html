IN COMING UNFOUNDED LUNA FANWANK

Luna still feels guilty for her crimes as Nightmare Moon, but feels even guiltier that she forced to neglect her duties to her domain. If Everfree used to be her kingdom much like Equistria is to Princess Celstia, then think about how screwed up it got in the last Thousand Years.

Everything in Equistria is ordered and controlled, the weather, the plants, the animals are all dependent on the work of the Ponies and their traditions. Spring cant even come until the Ponies "Clean Up Winter". Presumably all of this is maintained and run by Celstia who rules with near total power over her country. There are many reasons for the ponies to be scared of Everfree but notably they are shocked and horrified at the thought of Everfree being completely self sufficient.

But what if Everfree wasn't always such a dangerous place or isn't as bad as we are lead to believe? After all Zecora seems to live there just fine. What if Everfree used to be exactly what Luna represented, not in opposition to her sister but in balance to it? During the day the sun rises and shows the world to all, people can build and construct and order and form laws, but at Night when they sleep the world around them is unseen and unknown, wild and untamed and free, not evil just different and mysterious.

The Everfree Forest as it is today the result of a thousand years of Luna's absence, a twisted wanton mockery of what it was supposed to be: a wild and untamed testament to the beauty of the night and the nature of the wilds. It became corrupt in her absence when she wasn't there to watch over it like Celstia watches over Equstria.

If I was Luna that would make mad with guilt.
