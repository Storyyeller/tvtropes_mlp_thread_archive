Well, by "instinctively greedy" it sounds like hoarding is just some thing dragons do.

And it makes sense. In this show, dragons eat precious gems and (presumably) metals. Having an instinct to hoard these (imperishable) foodstuffs makes a lot of sense when you live extremely long and are huge. After all, the older dragons are huge, and that means they have huge guts, which need to be filled with a lot of treasure.

It's more of a justified hoard than some other works. Best explanation since "I lie on the treasure to protect my underbelly"
