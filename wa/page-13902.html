Case in point, if you go by that metric, it would mean that I'm gay, because all of my closest friends are male. Needless to say, this isn't the case. You can't really judge a person's romantic inclinations on their platonic relationships. The two things are very different.

Honestly, part of why I'm more into shipping in this fandom is because it's basically a blank slate. You could make pretty much any of the mane cast gay, straight, bisexual or whatever and still have it be in character because there's no frame of reference for it.
