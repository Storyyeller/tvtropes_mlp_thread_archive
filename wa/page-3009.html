Personally, while I LOVE chocolate, I find plain chocolate ice cream to be inferior to any other type of chocolate. I LOVE chocolate ice cream smothered in chocolate sauce.

And speaking of chocolate, I think Rarity would be some type of dark chocolate and/or white chocolate ice cream, marshmallows and/or some type of mint flavoring optional addition. I saw dark chocolate is because chocolate had always been seen as a rich food and a symbol of decadence, with darker chocolates seemingly more sophisticated and upper class. This description I think matches Rarity's personality as well as her wish to be in high society. The marshmallows and/or mint flavoring represent her generosity, having something super sweet to offset the intense flavor of the chocolate.
