To be fair, I can admit to basically abusing the Internet as a springboard for my style by replicating the styles of a lot of random artists/fanartists from the Web to springboard my drawing technique, as well as using how-to-draw guides aplenty for extra experience. On general principle, I absolutely refused to trace because I hold the belief that if I can reverse-engineer a drawing and (partially) reproduce it (or better if the original piece was... lacking), I learn much more than if I cheaped out and traced it.
