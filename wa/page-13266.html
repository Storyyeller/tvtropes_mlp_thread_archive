An interesting system that could probably work for ponies is the system used by Star Wars: Edge of the Empire. The dice don't do numbers. They simply do success/failure, and a neat system of Advantage/Threat that adds to the action. For example, you could succeed in unlocking a door with added Threat, meaning that while you picked the lock, a guard heard the noise and is suspicious. I haven't looked too far into things, but it would definitely help to sort out all of the numbers upon numbers that tabletop games tend to devolve into.
