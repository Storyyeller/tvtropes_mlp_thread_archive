#315772 Enlong Wed, 12th Dec '12 11:31:36 AM from The Magitek Holyland
Also, this is kind of an old question, but in light of new info, I'm curious: if we assume the elements were split 3/3 between them, who got what?

Celestia:
  • Kindness: This is really evident with her personality. She is a very sweet and kind individual.
  • Laughter: Celesita also has a bit more of a sense of humor than Luna, and relishes a good joke, but to go with the other definition of the Element, she does what she does to spread joy.
  • Honesty: The last two Elements are difficult to place, but Celestia seems to place a strong emphasis on striving to do the right thing.

Luna:
  • Magic: One thought I like is that Luna's use of the elements was sort of twisted during her fall. If Magic is an element that represents faith in one's friends, then Luna lost her connection to the element when she fell deep into her despair and lost faith in her subjects and her sister. However, from what we've seen of her, she values peoples' trust and love, when she is normal.
  • Generosity: Another aspect that sort of led to her fall. She puts a lot of time and effort into raising the moon and creating a beautiful night. Part of her fall is because she believed that nopony appreciated what she did for them. She began to feel that she deserved more in return for what she generously gave her subjects.
  • Loyalty: She lost her faith in ties of loyalty, and... okay, again, this one is hard to place.

However, I like the idea that Celestia was able to take up the remaining three elements because it was something she had to do. As the ruler, she had an obligation, one of loyalty to her subjects, to defeat Nightmare Moon. She selflessly did it, even though I'm sure it tore at her heart. And in the end, she never lost her faith that Luna could be returned to normal.
