Tealove was also a part of the Peeler fanon. Peeler was Pip's father, an old-school copper from Trottingham who moved himself and Pip over because Trottingham was getting too dangerous to be raising a foal. Being a Trottinghamite Peeler of course put a good stock in tea, which is how he met Tealove. The two had a sort of healthy respect and admiration for each other that would eventually lead to friendship, and as Peeler spent more and more time at the tea shop(As it became obvious work for a copper was not as busy in Ponyville), their friendship blossomed into a relationship.
