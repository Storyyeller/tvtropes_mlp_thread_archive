I've been really worried about Batty lately. I'm worried that the whole 'dark, aloft, different loner type' has just been done to death. That 6 ordinary ponies would be a safer bet that going into the rarer aspects of the show that might get undone by canon in the future.

And I've been thinking...maybe I should remake Batty closer to the 'Aviary' concept instead of the 'dark knight' concept, only with bats instead. His flight googles could be tinted to protect his eyes in the day...and maybe, he's addicted to fruit...so much so that he steals it during the night, which causes him immense problems of course. I am hoping that my crew all have 'problems' which prevent them from functioning in society.

The clues in the name...he's Areobat...he's Batty...he's crazy! I feel like I should really jump on that, what with the naming conventions of ponies and all that. Of course, he'd probably need a recolouring...and a major design rethink, if he's now this mischievous trickster thief type instead of a dark brooding knight. That's if I even decide to do it.
