#35194 Vree Thu, 3rd Nov '11 2:32:26 AM
My two coins: fanon is ALWAYS about having fun with Alternative Character Interpretation. Two people's interpretation may differ radically because at the end of the day it's about having fun.

So the people who argue that "fanon should be like this, because that's what just happened on the show" are, while well intentioned, are arguing about nonsense. No-one's making up things because they expect it to overwrite the show. No-one's expecting their fanon to even overwrite other people's fanon. If a person writes a fanfic where Dr. Hooves is a clockmaker (as it happened) you don't flame her because you want there to be a single interpretation.

What happens when things on the show change is that people start making up non-official material about THAT as well. If it contradicts previous fanon, it becomes a source of in jokes. I'm sure everyone is mature enough to come up with their own solution, whether it's sticking with the old or the new name or making up something completely outlandish, but none is less valid.

...

I think the drama is really more about character tags. xD Because some over-enthuastic people may go on a pony site and cause work for each other by changing the tags back and forth.
