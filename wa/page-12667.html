Dear Thread Manor...

I've read a lot of fics since my last report! So many that I'm gonna need two letters to report on them all! Lets get the lighter fair out of the way now and save the heavier stuff for the second part, hmm?

Twilight Sparkle: History's Greatest Monster -in progress. Twilight wakes up in an AU where she is an evil mastermind that has taken over Equestria. And simply being there seems to be turning her into the evil mastermind that she apparently should be in this verse! She has to find a way back before she changes for good!

MLP: Anthology of Interest. Twilights friends discover that she has a What If Machine, capable of showing them alternate timelines based on "what if" questions. Hilarity ensues.

Not much to say here, as it's kind of self explanatory. Though I will say that the last chapter isn't quite what you might expect...

In The Twahlaht Of Her Youth -in progress. Twahlaht Sporkle, personal student of Princess Luna, is sick and tired of all the magic studying and test taking. So when she stumbles upon a strange portal in a clearing outside Canterlot, she doesn't hesitate to jump through. She's notably disappointed to find herself in seemingly the exact same place, though she'll come to realize that isn't quite the case...

In case you're wondering, her special talent is Spork magic.

Higher Flier OR My Little Blackbird. A strange pegasus mare, Dainty Dish, is saved from a tailspin by Dash, but her extremely expensive, sophisticated, custom made camera isn't quite so lucky. She leaves to get a replacement straight from the manufacturer in Seaddle. The girls, especially Dash, are shocked and amazed when she returns not 4 hours later with a brand new camera, from a trip that should have taken 2 days. What is Dainty's secret? and how will Dash handle this evidence that she apparently isn't the fastest flier in Equestria?

this story is what happens if you ponify an SR-71 Blackbird.

Why am I Pinkie Pie?! A totally normal human male finds himself somehow mindswapped with Pinkie Pie. He has to get help from the rest of her(his?) friends if he's ever going to fix this, but how can he convince them that there's a real problem and that it's not just Pinkie Pie being Pinkie Pie? And why does it seem like someone, or something, is conspiring to keep him from finding a solution to all this?

I know the premise sounds like disaster waiting to happen, but the author totally pulled it off! Definitely recommend this one.

and there's part one! Stay tuned for part 2!
