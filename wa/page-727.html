#18163 d Galloway Mon, 12th Sep '11 6:22:36 PM
I'll admit, I don't want to see Discord dead. But at the same time, you can't have him just in the background. He'd be a major Elephant In The Living Room otherwise.

He's not something you can ignore like the Elements or Nightmare Moon. The former were plot devices (and indeed are again), and the latter was pretty well defeated. Discord is a Reality Warper Eldritch Abomination. He's not something you can just go back to every few episodes, and since Hasbro wants the show to be viewable in any order, they can't make a grand season-long epic.

A better idea would be to have him defeated and resealed, or otherwise taken out of commission, at the end of the two-parter. That way, if the production team decides to revisit the character, they can do so without having to explain where he's been for the last ten episodes.

Sorry if I'm harping on the subject. I'm just...really concerned about the show. This is one of the best shows to premiere in a long time, and I want to see it stay that way. Faust leaving isn't what worries me; she only got the show off the ground, and the three episodes she penned weren't the greatest. I'd probably feel better when they actually release more info on episodes besides this one.
