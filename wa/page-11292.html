This here is arguably my favourite episode of the show: "Sisterhooves Social". I guess it's comparable to "Look Before You Sleep". It is a Rarity episode, but I would argue that it's a Sweetie Belle episode, too.

Did you know that some sources claim that Sweetie Belle is an excellent baker, and that she's the youngest of the group? I believe this episode disproves the excellent baker claim. Besides, I'm not sure that she's the youngest filly in the Cutie Mark Crusaders.

Let me tell you, my heart was really going out to poor Sweetie Belle. She was just trying to be helpful. I can understand that disrupting your workspace does not produce a good mood, but Rarity didn't have to flip out like that. Still, Sweetie showed just how much of a Deadpan Snarker she can be, and just how brutal her "The Reason You Suck" Speech can be, didn't she?

Rarity has been compared to Scarlett O'Hara from Gone with the Wind. I just wish Scarlett's kids told her off for her neglectful, unmotherly behaviour!

Anyway, Apple Bloom and Applejack seem to have a much improved relationship between them. Still, it was interesting how AJ was saying, "Well, actually, I...." Maybe she was trying to communicate to Sweetie that she's not that great of a sister? By the way, that was fantastic, the way AJ and AB defined the word "uncouth"!

When Sweetie and Rarity were reporting the moral of story, I did find myself wondering if Princesses Celestia and Luna have a relationship comparable to theirs. I guess we'll never know for sure!
