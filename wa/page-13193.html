It's a D&D concept. Basically, someone figured out a way to exploit a poorly worded ability in order to ascend to omnipotence (basically becoming the DM). The original was a Kobold who ascended at level 4, but people later improved it to work at level 1.

Nowadays Pun-pun is often used as shorthand for similarly theoretical infinite loops and the like.
