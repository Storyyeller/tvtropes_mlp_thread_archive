#16955 Vree Mon, 5th Sep '11 6:32:52 PM
There are MULES, which require horses and donkeys to both exist first.

Pony + donkey still equals mule, innit?

I once had an idea about exploring just how many hooved species are out there - I imagined a huge "old continent" out there ruled by even toed ungulates where ancestral wild horses exist in the same state that cows (who are also sentient in canon) do in Equestria. But I gave up because it started to become too distracting from ponies. It's easier to imagine that this is a magical land where ponies exist by virtue of being magical (unicorns and pegasi), and every other species is some sort of legendary creature (gryphon, dragon and such) as well.

On donkeys: on one hand, I like to think that they are sentient and live in some far-off part of Equestria, like, if Equestria is the U.K. then donkeys are Scottish or Irish. (There was this funny fan theory about Rarity being adopted by donkeys, hence her dislike for mules.)

But a fun possibility is if donkeys aren't sentient in Equestria at all, but can still interbreed with ponies. (All equine races - horses, donkeys and zebras - can have offsprings in real life.) That'd explain why mules are looked down upon by the pony populace, why they tend to be idiotic and why they don't have a cutie mark.
