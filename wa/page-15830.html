#395736 Rainbow Wed, 4th Sep '13 9:56:53 AM
I have a question or maybe just a discussion topic about a pony fan controversy that I've had on my mind. What does one do when there is more than one name for a pony, and both names are used by at least somewhat official sources? I would assume the general rule would be "each fan decides the answer for him/herself" but it was something I was wondering about.

Specifically, with Bon Bon/Sweetie Drops. She isn't named on the show, but apparently while the toys and the Elements of Harmony book use the name Sweetie Drops, the trading cards and the Gameloft game use the name Bon Bon, as well as some other things such as closed captioning. I personally tend to take my hierarchy of what is considered to be canon as Show>Toys>Other official sources including the comics, so following that, I'm more inclined to assume that Sweetie Drops is her formal name, at least. It's just more confusing when multiple official sources give the same character different names, and it's not something I've ever experienced before becoming a fan of this particular version of My Little Pony.

By the way, some of this is for a fanfic I've been planning that focuses on background and toyline ponies and only recently considered including Sweetie Drops/Bon Bon and then wasn't sure what to call her in the story. When communicating with other fans in general, I would understand and use both names for her, since I'm personally fine with either one. It's just that when writing a story I would rather have one name to stick to. An obvious solution, of course, is to have "Bon Bon" be her nickname like how the trading cards list "Carrot Top" as being Golden Harvest's nickname.

(and I am also a big fan of Pretty Cure and Sailor Moon and most of the fanfics I write are based off of those two shows, especially the latter)
