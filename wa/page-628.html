The character tier as determined by Calnos right at this moment

Mane Characters - Mane 6 + Spike, Celestia, and the Cutie Mark Crusaders
Supporting Characters - Recurring Characters with names and speaking parts (Big Macintosh, Cheerilee, Spitfire)

Background Ponies - (Mostly) Nameless extras used for crowd filler and occasional gags (Ditzy Doo, Lyra, Doctor Whoof)
