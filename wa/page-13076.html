Dear Thread Manor...

only 3 stories today, but there's a theme this time! and that theme is changelings! What ind of stories do our favorite little shape shifters have for us? Lets see!

Thrown Abroad. Chrysalis, looking for a scapegoat, blames the entirety of the failed Canterlot invasion on a single changeling and banishes him from the hive. He finds himself outside Ponyville. Unfortunately, our little changeling doesn't even have enough love in him to shape change into pony. No matter, ponies are stupid creatures, he should be able to infiltrate the town just fine, even without shape changing! Hilarity Ensues.

A fairly simple story, well worth a read. Has a sequel, though I have yet to read that one.

Interview With A Changeling -in progress. After the failed invasion of Canterlot, Shining Armor has a serious hatred against changelings, both for what they did to his country and what they did to his wife. Now, a week later, a lone changeling in his natural form approaches the gates of Canterlot with a white flag. He claims to be from a different hive than the one that attacked Canterlot, with a different queen ruling over it. Now that the existence of changelings is public knowledge, this new hive wishes to open diplomatic relations with Equestria. Can Shining Armor put aside his not so irrational hatred of changelings and give peace a chance?

Yep, a specieism story. SA is a racist! He actually has a reason for it, but still. I recommend this story.

My Little Behemoth: Friendship is More than Food. Using fell magics, Queen Chrysalis changes a simple worker changeling, Cys, into a changeling behemoth, a changeling the size of a manticore. He can no longer fly or shape change, but has vastly increased physical and magical capabilities to compensate, including a full on invisibility spell. The problem is, Cys never asked for this and hates Chrysalis for what she's done to him, and for iron hold she has on him forcing him to serve her. Fast forward to the Canterlot invasion. Cys is incapacitated by Princess Luna, and due to his size is not blasted out of Canterlot by the love bomb. In deciding his punishment, Princess Celestia learns that he never wanted to be here, and actually did his best to not severely hurt anypony. Tia decides on his punishment: community service in Ponyville.

This was a neat story, but I felt that it ended a bit too fast. Still definitely worth a read though.

That's all for now, see you all next time!

Your Faithful Fanfic Reader, -Blissey1
