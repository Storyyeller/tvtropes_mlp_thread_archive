It's time for another fanfic report!

Dear Thread Manor...

Today I have a few of short and not so short one shots, a couple of normal sized stories, and a big one at 200k words and going! Lets go form shortest to longest, hmm?

Pinkie Watches Paint Dry. Pinkie, after painting her orange room pink, has an existential crisis.

I'm sure you all know about this one. I liked it!

A Million Things to Do. Pinkie gets a doozy so bad it can only mean one thing- the world is going to end! Now she and Dash have to do everything they can while there's still time!

Some Pinkie/Dash friendshipping. Pinkie really is afraid the world is going to end, just not in that way...a nice mix of sad and happy.

Her Weight in Affection. SA and Cadance's love blast during the wedding blasted Chrysalis right through the fourth wall into real life. Fortunately, the queen of the changelings is nothing if not adaptable. Some years later, under the guise of a human female, Chrysalis is financially successful and climbing the ladder of corporate America. Unfortunately, it's true what they say that money can't buy happiness, and when you literally require love and affection to sustain yourself, that's kinda a big problem. Can Chrysalis ever find a source of love that would accept her for who she really is?

Now, this was kind of an impulse read for me. This really isn't my kind of story. That being said, I did still enjoy it. Oh, and it has fat chrysalis too, which I think some people around here might enjoy.

An Earth Pony's Guide to Magic. Star Swirl the Hundred and Forty Seventh is having an existential crisis. She has the same cutie mark as her ancestor, Star Swirl the Bearded, one of the greatest spell casters in history. But how can she possibly have the same talent as him when she is an Earth pony? She decides to move to Ponyville to get a new perspective on things.

So I finally got around to reading this! I really liked the backdrop of her going to see her psychologist, but I felt that The Doctor wrapping everything up at the end was kind of a copout. Overall, it was an OK story.

Mort Takes a Holiday- in progress. The Pale Pony of Death has not been very happy lately. He is in fact quite miserable. The pony population has exploded the last couple 100 years, which means the pony death rate has also increased, and Mort is more busy than ever. He hardly has time to get to know the spirits of the recently deceased before they pass on to the other side, and stopping by Canterlot to visit his best friends Celestia and Luna is right out of the question. Celestia decides that it's time for him to take a vacation, and she knows the perfect quaint little town for him to go to, a place where even Death itself can make some friends! Just so long as nobody finds out who he really is...

I really enjoyed this one! Crazy shenanigans as Mort tries to adapt to mortal life and make friends with the girls while keeping his identity a secret. I need to read more comedy type fics...

Waking Nightmares- in progress. Blu Team finds themselves in Equestria and ponified, and nopony seems to know why or how. But before anypony can even think of finding a way to get them back to their own world, strange and terrible things begin happening. Can BLU team and the girls stand up to the evil of the Smooze and it's agent, the Slender Pony?

Here's that really long one. Takes some interesting liberties with canon (there's actually 9 elements of harmony!) and creative uses of fanon (the various girmdark fics like Cupcakes are evil induced nightmares in an attempt to break the girls). I'm enjoying it overall, even if it does get a bit too dark for my tastes at times.

Your Faithful Fanfic Reader
-Blissey1
