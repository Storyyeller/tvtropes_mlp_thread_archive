I had a case of Fridge Brilliance about the Cutie Mark Crusaders and how it is that they are so cute. To put it simply, they are similar to puppies, in terms of looks (to some degree) and behaviour. Let's see:

  1. All three have big round eyes that get associated to puppies, and it comes off as adorable rather than creepy.
  2. Apple Bloom actually gave sad puppy eyes and whimpering little mouth in the first episode.
  3. Apple Bloom spun around like a dog chasing its tail in "Call of the Cutie".
  4. All three have shown themselves to be as energetic as puppies, with how they like to play and play before falling asleep.
  5. Sweetie Belle in particular has voice cracks (or squeaks) in her voice, which come off as cute rather than annoying.
  6. "Sisterhooves Social" had Sweetie Belle hop and skip on a mud pit, and that was just cute to watch!

Oh, Equestria, we stand on guard for thee!
