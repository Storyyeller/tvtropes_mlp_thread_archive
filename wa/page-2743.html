Sessalisk and anyone else wondering about the NATG: The only real rules of the event are nothing sexually explicit (some sauciness seems to be acceptable) and no excessively violent content and it has to at least vaguely fit the theme (the original NATG had some very interesting and bizarre interpretations of the themes). The event is supposed to encourage people to draw ponies and they'll accept pretty much anything, including two minute doodles (the stuff I submitted to the first one definitely looked much worse than what you guys have shown here).

As for submitting, apparently the initial surge of submissions broke the script site that was supposed to handle it, so they're working on an alternate solution. Since then, it looks like they've asked people to hold off on submitting for the time being since it's killing Phoe's e-mail. :/ So I guess tuck those files into a safe place until they bring everything back online.
