#353619 crimsonstorm 15 Wed, 3rd Apr '13 9:51:55 PM from A parallel universe
so guys. it's been three seasons. a roller coaster of fun, friendship and magic. and to pass some of the time, how about reminisce? does anyone remember the story of how they became a brony (or pegasister)? i still remember it like it was yesterday... *cue flashback ripple dissolve*

the show itself had been running for about a season and a quarter when i joined up. i was browsing the internet—just checking my usual haunts: deviantART, YouTube, Facebook—when i saw it. ponies. ponies everywhere. all tied to this one girls' show: My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. i was baffled as to why a such a girly show brought about such fanfare. so, one night when i was home alone, i fired up YouTube and looked up the first episode of the series, expecting it to be just more of "the internet being stupid."

but what i found was something... surprising. as the episode went on, i was shocked to find that the show wasn't nearly as girly as i thought. i... i started to sympathize with the characters, specifically the purple unicorn with her mind on her studies. the white fashion diva unicorn's drama queen reactions and the pink one's hyperactive antics amused me. i began to care about the characters on this... this girls' show. by the end of the episode—when the villain was revealed—i actually felt the same sense of peril that the the characters felt.

then the episode ended. in the suggested videos list was part two of the premiere. i believe that the moment i became a brony was when i moved my mouse to that thumbnail out of my own free will... and clicked on it.

anyway, that's my story. what's yours?
