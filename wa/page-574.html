So, I was streamlining the Hey, It's That Voice! section on the Trivia page, I remembered that Trixie's voice actress voiced ponies in G3 (I sat down and watched one episode and I went "Oh my Luna, is that Trixie" at one point). That Other Wiki has a huge list of every pony character ever, and looking at some of the other veteran pony actresses like Tabitha St. Germain I of course come across Luna's paragraph and found an eye ripping mistake.

"Princess Luna is Celestia and Dazzleglow's little sister..."

Wondering who the hell Dazzleglow is, I Ctrl+F my way to her and apparently she's a pegacorn that appeared in My Little Pony Tales. Apparently Bon Bon met her.

"Dazzleglow is a Pegasus Unicorn pony with a pink body and a Blue and pink mane and tail. Her symbol is Three yellow umbrellas. She was first released as a Unicorn Pony in 1991 as one of the Glow 'N Show Ponies. She then later appeared in the 1992 Animated series My Little Pony Tales in the episode Up, Up and Away. They live on an island far away from the regular ponies' homes in Coltonville but sometimes visit the city as a glowing cloud of stardust. Starlight and her friends think they were UF Os until Patch and Bon Bon met them up close. Unlike the other Glowing Magic Ponies, Dazzleglow is considered to be an pegacorn, making her the first pegacorn to officially appear in animation until the appearance of Princess Celestia and Princess Luna in My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. She seems to have great magic powers, and uses her horn to fix the balloon."

So, uh, what? I haven't you tubed this yet, but I thought I'd share this odd little discovery with you all...
