About the question of how I got interested in the current MLP series, I actually wasn't really interested in it at first. I liked the other MLP stories from before but was still rather hesitant about this newest one. All I had read about it was about the pilot and I was uninterested in a story where an introverted bookworm was told to go make friends instead of study, since I assumed that it was going to imply that Twilight being smart and bookish was a bad thing. I also wasn't too fond of the idea of the moon goddess pony being evil, either. However, I read more about it on this site and read things that made it sound more interesting, like learning that Luna isn't really "evil." Also, the idea of having a main character who was bookish and introverted rather than outgoing intrigued me as well. The first episode I watched was Applebuck Season, and the comedy in it, particularly the part where the "baked bads" are made, got me to like the show a lot. I didn't watch the episodes in order the first time through, I skipped to ones that sounded more interesting to me first or ones that I saw were talked about a lot on this site.
