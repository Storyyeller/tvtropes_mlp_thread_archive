#309099 Perpetual Lurker Wed, 28th Nov '12 6:17:31 AM
My solution to the issue of "she can move the sun so she can crush the world" is that the more specific a spell is in function, the more efficient and powerful it is. Telekinesis is very loose in purpose, as it can affect just about anything, so it's not very efficient. I doubt even a hundred Celestias could move the sun with normal Telekinesis. Consider, though, a spell that does only one thing, and in a very specific way, namely set the sun, and only the sun, in a very specific orbit. Something that specific is powerful enough to do its job, and even then it takes the collective unicorn tribe to pull it off. Celestia has power equal to that of several hundred average unicorns, but I personally wouldn't put her past "leveling a mountain" in terms of strength.
