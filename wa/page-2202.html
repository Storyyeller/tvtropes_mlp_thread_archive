How I spent Hearth Warming's Eve:

Went to a Science Center at a Mall with some friends. Not the best time I've ever had. Some exhibits were great. (They had a hurricane simulation booth) Others were meh, some were just broken. I got to play a little Child of Eden on Kinect in the last exhibit. It's alright. Friends and I parted ways slightly after.

Went to Kinokuniya bookstore and attended a book signing by Sonny Liew, the author of Malinky Robot, a damn fine indie comic from Image Comics. I got a copy of the collection signed, plus a cool robot sketch. I gave him my deviantart profile. (I said I was interested in art, heh.) He was also giving away a bunch of prints of his work and some other Asian artists. I got one. It had hot girls with guns.

The mall I went to has a LRT (Light Rail Transit- basically a monorail system) stop in the basement. On my way back there I observed bronies in the wild for the first time. They had been attending a comics convention nearby and were heading back. They were decked out in Pins, T-shirts, balloons, the whole nine yards. I struck up a conversation with one guy dressed up as Surprise. Apparently no one recognized his costume. Everyone thought he was an angel with a blonde afro.

Said he was a member of the Malaysian Bronies Society and invited me to join their Facebook group. We said our goodbyes; then they rounded the corner and disappeared.

Took the train home, then the bus. It started drizzling slightly. I stuffed my goods under my shirt and made it home relatively dry.

Yep, just about perfect.
