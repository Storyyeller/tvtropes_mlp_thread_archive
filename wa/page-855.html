#21374 kyun Fri, 23rd Sep '11 2:00:56 PM
Ever hear of the Rashomon Effect? Stories parody and utilize this framework where several characters all try to tell what happened during a given situation in the past, and they are all completely different and contradict one another. Flashbacks can lie, because they're all formulated out of someone's memory, and being (usually) human, the character can make up false stories just as much as anything else.
