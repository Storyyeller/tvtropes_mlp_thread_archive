Just start from the beginning, but watch a couple of episodes before making up your mind. The two part pilot is good, but not really representative of the series as a whole. The character-driven stand-alone episodes that make up most of the season are generally held to be an improvement.

Personally, I don't think The Cutie Mark Chronicles is a very good starter episode, since a lot of its entertainment value come from the cool little callbacks (Call Forwards?) and references. Overall, I do think the pilot can be a good starter, but there's always the danger of people, especially men, judging the show too early. In that case, I'd recommend Swarm of the Century, Sonic Rainboom, or maybe Fall Weather Friends. I love Dragonshy, and it'd probably work okay as a starter, but I feel the ending is more awesome if the viewer's gotten to see more of Fluttershy's... well, shy nature.

On that note, I finally was able to convince one of my friends (that I mentioned waaaaaay earlier in this thread) who refused to watch past the first episode to watch Sonic Rainboom. It seemed like he enjoyed it, but still can't get over the fact that it's My Little Pony. Oh well, I tried.
